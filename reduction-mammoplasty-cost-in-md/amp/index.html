Technique

Surgery for breast reduction in Maryland is performed in specially equipped operating rooms. In plastic surgery, there are many methods, the purpose of which is to reduce and restore the large and lowered breasts.

Before surgery, the doctor makes marks on your chest, on which he must strictly follow during the operation. This is one of the most important stages of surgical intervention to achieve the desired effect, since it is at this stage that the doctor determines what it should remove and what to leave in order to form a new breast shape. The operation lasts 3-3.5 hours.

Postoperative period

Immediately after the operation, the woman is placed in the ward without experiencing pain. Within 3 weeks she should wear a special bra. The next morning, after the first inspection and bandaging, the patient is discharged home.

A woman can immediately engage in daily activities, and return to work maximum within a week (depending on the type of work). In 7-10 days after the operation, we remove the sutures around the areola.
