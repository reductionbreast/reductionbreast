How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Saint Louis MO?

Lush and large breasts are not always a gift of nature for its owner.

Often, it causes a sense of discomfort and pain in the back, and also creates additional difficulties for women in the selection of underwear and clothing, adds a visual weight and year.

Therefore, many ladies are thinking about reducing breast size.

In aesthetic medicine, reduction mammoplasty in Saint Louis, that is, breast reduction is less demanded by patients than breast augmentation, but nevertheless quite a lot of women turn to a surgeon to adjust the size of their breasts.

Description of procedure

If you have decided on an operation to reduce the breast, then initially you need to decide on a clinic and a surgeon who will carry out this plastic surgery.

40 Year Old Woman Treated With Breast Reduction By Dr Richard Kofkoff, MD, FACS, Saint Louis Plastic Surgeon

Do your own research, see how many years the service provides this or that medical institution, read reviews, look for information about whether doctors are working permanently or are incoming specialists, get acquainted with the experience of their work in the plastic surgery industry.

Choose two or four surgeons whose information you most trust and sign up for an initial free admission to everyone (even if they work in the same clinic).

The surgeon will inspect the mammary glands, assess their size and shape, and the ratio of glandular and adipose tissue, talk about the tactics of the operation, the preparatory stage which includes the collection of necessary tests and visits to other doctors to eliminate contraindications, the postoperative period and recommendations.

Personal meeting gives the opportunity to make an impression about each doctor and determine the choice of the clinic.

Indication for the procedure

Hypertrophy of the mammary glands, which can be hereditary or acquired (for example, in pregnancy or endocrine diseases);

52 Year Old Woman Treated With Breast Reduction Before After By Doctor James H. Scheu, MD, FACS, Saint Louis Plastic Surgeon

Breast asymmetry

Big breasts contribute to the appearance of curvature of the spine, violation of posture, the appearance of back pain, the emergence of dermatological diseases in the folds of the mammary glands, as well as a woman accompanies a feeling of heaviness and limits the possibility of exercising active physical activities.

Contraindications

First, it is worth to visit a breast physician to assess the risks and negative consequences of this procedure.

Conduct ultrasound and radiographic examination of the mammary glands, have a set of laboratory tests, ECG and consult with other specialists.

It is recommended that reduction mammoplasty be carried out already for women who have children and who do not plan pregnancy in the future, since removing part of the glandular tissue will make lactation difficult.

If a woman is determined to lose weight, the operation should be postponed until the desired result is obtained and after that the procedure for reducing the breast is carried out.

57 Year Old Woman Treated With Breast Reduction With Dr Jeffrey G. Copeland, MD, Saint Louis Plastic Surgeon

Breasts should be healthy before the operation, so contraindications are various diseases in the field of surgery, as well as a number of other: infectious diseases, blood coagulability problems, diabetes, oncology. To get acquainted with the full list of contraindications, you can consult a surgeon.

During the preparation for surgery, the patient together with the surgeon will determine the optimal variant of the size and shape of the mammary glands, the surgeon considers the technical features of the operation.

The procedure consists in removing excess fatty, glandular tissue and skin with further suturing the incision. If necessary, moves the nipple to a new location and corrects the shape of the areola.

In this procedure, a very important factor is the professionalism and skill of the surgeon, since:

  • Removes a sufficiently large volume of tissues, among which are the milk ducts;
  • It is necessary to preserve the breast sensitivity as much as possible;
  • A scar after an operative incision, should be practically imperceptible.

Recovery period

The patient is in the hospital after the surgery one night. Then, in the period up to 7 days made removal of sutures. In the first month, it is necessary to wear compression underwear. Within two months should refrain from physical exertion, lifting heavy weight.

Reduction of edema can be achieved if you sleep solely on your back, putting several pillows under it. And the most important thing is to do all the doctor’s recommendations and refer to it, if there are any complications.

Duration of the procedure

The time of surgery depends on the volume of tissue removed and can take from two to three hours.

Result

The final result of the operation can be estimated after six months. It is quite stable, but excess weight and hormonal disorders will affect the size and shape of the breast.
