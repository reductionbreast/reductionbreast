Right breast suddenly numb 1 month post op?
Breast numbness after surgery
Mar 1, 2017
It is very common for changes in sensation to occur after surgery. Cutaneous sensory nerves are divided during dissection, and nerves grow back at a rate of 1 mm/day. If you had feeling in your breasts after surgery, and then you had a change without undergoing any other surgery, I would expect that you will regain sensation in your breasts again. If the numbness persists, then you may want to go back to your plastic surgeon to see if any other reasons for the change can be found.

Breast Reduction and Lift
Breast reduction vs breast lift
Feb 28, 2017
Both a breast reduction and a breast lift will lift your breasts. The difference is that a breast reduction will also resect a greater amount of breast tissue so that your breasts are smaller afterwards in addition to being lifted. If you want smaller breasts that are lifted, then you want a breast reduction. If you want breasts that are the same size that are lifted, then you want a breast lift.
