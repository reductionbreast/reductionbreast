Is it required that you need someone to pick you up after having a breast augmentation surgery?
Help after Augmentation
Mar 20, 2019
Hello. Yes, you definitely need someone with you after your surgery. You will be drowsy from anesthesia and need someone to help you get around safely. If you just lay in bed without moving around because you don't have help, you can develop blood clots. Furthermore, you will be sore and will likely need help even with little things. You will also need help with carrying luggage and such. Good luck!
