Hello! A breast lift and implant can be placed at the same time safely. The larger the implant, the higher the risk of complications, but it can be done safely in one stage in a non-smoker. Is a breast lift almost always part of breast reduction surgery?
Breast lifting as part of a Breast reduction
Jun 5, 2017
Hello!
Thank you for you question. As a plastic surgeon reduces and reconstructs the breasts to a more desired shape and size the breast is also lifted. We are able to bring the nipples into a higher and more appropriate position as well as reduce the skin and tissue to greatly reduce back, neck, and shoulder pain. There should not be separate charges for the reduction and the lift - the lift comes as part of the reduction!
Best wishes for a speedy recover.
Dr. Della Bennet
