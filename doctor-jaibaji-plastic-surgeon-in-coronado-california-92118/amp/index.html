Fat transfer to breasts?
Fat transfer to breasts?
Jan 17, 2017
Thank you for the questions. Fat transfer to the breast is now an established procedure. It's done by removing the fat from other parts of your body using liposuction. The removed fat is then placed into the breasts. The procedure does give you a natural way of breast enhancement but it has downsides such as asymmetry, fat necrosis, fat calcification and infection. The procedure may also have to be repeated to get the desired breast size.
