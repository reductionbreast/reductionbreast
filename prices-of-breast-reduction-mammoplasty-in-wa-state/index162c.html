Prices Of Breast Reduction Mammoplasty In Washington State

Breast reduction in Washington is performed under epidural anesthesia or general anesthesia. Currently, there are many methods of reduction mammoplasty (breast reduction surgery).

Modern reduction mammoplasty should provide a reduction in the volume of the breast, improve its shape, preserve the sensitivity of the nipples, as well as the possibility of lactation with minimal postoperative scars. In our Washington clinic, preference is given to vertical mammoplasty, since when performing this particular technique it is possible to achieve a high aesthetic result, with a minimal amount of scars on the skin of the breast.

In the course of the breast reduction surgery, the excess skin and tissues of the gland is removed, the incisions are performed around the nipple and go down to the inframammary fold in the vertical direction.

Before After Breast Reduction In Spokane By Doctor Alfonso Oliva

The mammary gland rises higher and is fixed. After the breast reduction, incisions are sutured intradermal cosmetic suture. In this case, the scar is only around the nipple and from the nipple to the inframammary fold, along it are formed thin folds, which disappear within the first 2 months after the operation.

The absence of a horizontal scar beneath the breast is an advantage of this method of breast reduction. Most of the other techniques of breast reduction surgery involve the presence of both a vertical and a horizontal scar.

In the postoperative period within 3-4 weeks after breast reduction surgery it is advisable to wear a special compression underwear around the clock, which helps to maintain a new form of the breasts.

The first important step is consulting with a surgeon. At the first consultation you will meet the doctor and tell him about your problem. You will receive the most detailed information about possible options for solving it with the help of modern techniques of plastic surgery, which are owned by our surgeons. After that, you and the doctor make a decision and draw up a detailed plan.

Stages of the operation:

Preparation

In the process of preparation for the operation (not more than 2 weeks), it is necessary to have the tests prescribed by the doctor:

  • General analysis of blood with platelets and duration of bleeding
  • Biochemical blood test (liver tests, ureas, sugar, total protein, creatinine)
  • General urine analysis
  • Coagulogram (prothrombin index)
  • Test for HIV and hepatitis B, C
  • Wasserman reaction
  • Blood group, Rh factor
  • Fluorography
  • Electrocardiogram.

Then you come to the clinic, sign the information consent to the operation and anesthesia, pay for the operation. Before the operation, the doctor takes a photo to compare the results.
