How long is the wait period to replace an implant after a Staph infection?
Breast Implant Replacement After Infection
Aug 8, 2019
I am sorry to hear about your infection. Infections of implanted devices require that the bacteria is completely treated and close follow up with your surgeon. He or she must be very satisfied that you are at low risk for developing a second infection and it is not uncommon to have to wait 3-6 months. As with any complication it is important for you not to become discouraged and let your surgeon determine the proper time for surgery.
