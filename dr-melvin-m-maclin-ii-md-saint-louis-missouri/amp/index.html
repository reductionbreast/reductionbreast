Allergy reaction to silicone sheets?
Skin irritation with silicone sheets
Feb 8, 2017
Silicone sheets on surgical scars are very helpful to manage scarring, are very easy to use, and work very well to minimize scar appearance.
However one of the most frequent problems with the sheets are redness and itching under the sheets. This is not an allergic reaction. It is more than likely "fungal excoriation" (diaper rash, athlete's foot, jock itch) caused by trapping moisture close to the skin and allowing yeast to proliferate and irritate the skin....
