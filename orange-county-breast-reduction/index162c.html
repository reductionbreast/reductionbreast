Orange County Breast Reduction

Many women seek breast reduction because they are suffering from oversized breasts, a condition that is medically known as breast hypertrophy.

It can occur in both breasts and usually happens at puberty or later. It can also happen during pregnancy and then the enlargement of the breasts can persist.

Usually plastic surgery can treat this problem. The cause of the enlargement of the breasts is unknown. Many of these articles provide information about the surgery and who should take the surgery.

Should you have breast reduction surgery?

There are many important things to consider if you want to determine candidacy for breast reduction. Among some points, the greatest candidates are women whose oversized breasts can cause health and medical problems as well as constant back pain. In the following section, there are explanations and details on how surgery is performed and what can the patient expect after the surgery.

Breast reduction for male patients suffering from Gynecomastia

Almost half of all men suffer from Gynecomastia – the enlargement of one or both breasts.

Drugs and anti-depressants, blood pressure, medicine, marijuana, steroids, and others are one of the primary causes of gynecomastia. It cannot be improved with diet and exercise and liposuction or scalpel must be performed.

More About Gynecomastia

Breast reduction surgery is typically very successful procedure and patients are usually very pleased with the results. Although there is a slight chance of complications and you should be aware of them.
