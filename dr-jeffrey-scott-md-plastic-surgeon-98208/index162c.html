Breast Augmentation
Sep 26, 2013
This is really important and several consultations and sizing methods can help with this issue. Am I Bottoming Out?
Breast Augmentation
Sep 26, 2013
Breast augmentation always changes in the first 3-6 months as the tissue softens and the implants settle into their more permanent position after surgery. With a tightening procedure like a breast lift, the tension can often be high at the lower incisions. Check with your surgeon.
Im Going for a Breast Augmentation in 7days and I Havent Stopped Smoking?
Breast Augmentation
Sep 26, 2013
Breast Augmentation and smoking can pose some additional risks. With smoking, the skin has a blood supply that is less robust. Making skin tighter is generally a little more dangerous with smoking. Placing an implant alone without a breast lift is usually safe with smoking, but I would check with your surgeon.
