Cost Of Breast Reduction In Indianapolis

Breast reduction surgery, or reduction mammoplasty, is a surgical intervention in Indianapolis that is performed by resection of fatty and glandular tissue, and by removing excess remaining skin. This operation allows you to solve both the physiological and psychological problems associated with breast hypertrophy.

What is reduction mammoplasty?

The breast consists of a glandular tissue that is attached to the chest wall. Between the lobules of the gland is a layer of adipose tissue, the volume of which can vary in a significant range. It is due to this the breasts may become smaller during the diet. The operation involves the removal of excess glandular tissue, as well as “extra” skin.

Dr Jeffrey D. Wagner Reduction Of Breast

In the process, the surgeon forms the volume of the breast, which the patient wants to receive. The result of reduction mammoplasty is an elastic and beautiful breasts.

Simultaneously with the reduction of the breast, other plastic surgeries can be performed, for example, mastopexy, or a breast lift, nipple correction, areola and other procedures.

Indications for breast reduction surgery

Women with a small bust often look enviously at the owners of lush breasts, not even guessing how many problems it can deliver. Hypertrophy of the mammary glands leads to the fact that the breast becomes very heavy.

The consequence is an increase in the load on the spine, and in the future – tension in the neck, pain in the shoulders and lower back, and often headaches.In addition, due to moisture in the folds under the breast may appear intertrigos and irritation, which also causes discomfort.

That’s why, the breast reduction surgery is in demand as much as breast augmentation.

The main indications for reduction mammoplasty are:

  • Mammary gland hypertrophy;
  • Physical and psychological problems associated with large breast size;
  • Deterioration of posture, which is a consequence of increased stress on the spine;
  • Displacement of the torso axis;
  • Pain in the chest and neck;

In addition, it should be noted that breast hypertrophy can be a consequence of endocrine diseases and a number of hormonal disorders.

That is why before making a decision about surgical breast reduction, it is important to visit an endocrinologist. Perhaps, first conservative treatment will be required, and after that it will be possible to perform reduction mammoplasty.

Contraindications for breast reduction surgery.

Thinking about breast reduction, many women forget that such a procedure has its contraindications. All restrictions to this operation can be divided into 2 groups.

Absolute contraindications. This group includes the conditions under which the operation is associated with a risk to health and even life. These include serious diseases of the heart, liver and kidneys, diabetes mellitus, HIV infection, hepatitis and some other diseases.

Temporary contraindications. The states included in this group assume the possibility of surgical intervention in the near or in the distant future. These include pregnancy and lactation recent, high temperature, fever, and a number of other conditions. Also, among the contraindications – the age of 18 years, because during this period the mammary glands are just forming.

Preparation for surgery

Today, breast reduction surgery is quite common. However, like any other surgical intervention, it requires special preparation of the patient. First of all, you need to get a detailed consultation of a plastic surgeon.

The doctor will tell the patient about the effect of which breast reduction is carried out, listen to all her wishes and on their basis suggest one way or another to solve the problem.

After the compromise between the requirements of the patient and the possibilities of modern plastic surgery will be found, the surgeon will assign the necessary tests and examinations.

Doctor Joseph Fata Breast Reduction Surgery Before And After

Usually their list includes a general and biochemical blood test, chest X-ray, ECG, breast ultrasound, HIV test and not only.

Approximately one week before the procedure, you must stop using alcohol, and stop taking drugs that dilute blood (aspirin, hormones, etc.).

Operation

The duration of reduction mammoplasty is approximately 2-3 hours. Immediately before the operation, the surgeon measures the parameters of the breast. After that, he marks the lines of the incisions with a marker and sterilizes the chest.

The procedure is performed under general anesthesia, with the use of intravenous or endotracheal anesthesia. Seams are most often in the form of an inverted “T”. This reduction allows you to get the most aesthetic results, regardless of the complexity of the operation.

If necessary, the doctor can choose a vertical seam: this technique is suitable for small or medium amounts of work. The surgeon makes incisions, removes excess skin and fat tissue and move the areola and nipple above, without separating from the nerves.

At the end of the procedure the doctor applies bioresorbable sutures. In the future, after the operation, the lines of the incisions pale or become almost invisible. To remove fluid and blood, drainages are installed, and gauze bandages are applied to the seams. To fix the breast use a special compression bra.

Recovery period

Despite the fact, that reduction mammoplasty in Indianapolis is performed under general anesthesia and does not require much time, the recovery period after the intervention is usually one and a half to two months. In order to make the recovery as comfortable as possible, this time you should be avoided:

  • Visits to the solarium,
  • Physical activity,
  • the sauna or steam bath,
  • Sunbathing.

Also in the first few weeks after reduction mammoplasty, it is recommended to constantly wear compression underwear, which is responsible for maintaining the breast in its normal position.
