Difference between 545cc and 560cc Implants and sagging

Can I still get a breast lift possibly with augmentation with large areolas?
Breast Lift and Large Areolas
Nov 7, 2019
You can certainly get a breast lift which will reduce the size of your areolas.  Occasionally a small amount of the pigmented areola tissue that would normally be removed has to be left to avoid excess tension on the skin.  That is best determined at the time of surgery.  It can be removed later if it is an issue.  I would recommend doing the lift first and then coming back at a second operation to add implants if you would like more volume.

Breast Reduction Good Candidate?

Should I opt for a breast lift after losing 58 lbs? How can I reduce the size of my areolas? (
Breast Lift Versus Areola Reduction
Aug 5, 2019
It would be best to have a consultation with a plastic surgeon to determine if a breast lift is a good option for you.  If the position of your nipple drops below the crease of your breast then a breast lift is typically needed.  If your surgeon feels that a breast lift is best, part of that operation is to reduce the size of your areola.  If it is felt a breast lift is not necessary an areola reduction can be done instead.

What procedure would work best to have my desired results of a flat stomach?
Wanting a Flat Stomach

What is the medical terminology for 1000ccs of fat?
Comparing ccs to milliliters of fat
Mar 25, 2019
Thank you for your question.  A “cc” is a cubic centimeter and is equal to a “ml” or milliliter.  This is the terminology used in the metric system.1000cc 1000ml 1 literFor a visual reference 1 ounce is about 30ml, 1 cup is about 236ml, and 1 quart is about 946ml.
Could an upper back lift improve breast laxity?
Will an upper back lift improve breast laxity?
Mar 11, 2019
Thank you for your question.  An upper back lift will do an excellent job at removing skin and fat from the upper back.  While the incision may extend around to the sides of the breasts, it will have little to no effect on breast laxity.  To more directly address your concerns about the shape of your breasts you should consider discussing breast procedures such as a breast lift with your plastic surgeon.  Often these procedures can be combined. 
Can you drop a cup size after liposuction of the upper back?
Will Liposuction Of The Upper Back Change My Cup Size?
Feb 12, 2019
Thanks for your question.  Liposuction of the upper back is very effective at removing unwanted fat in the area of concern but will not change the breast cup size as nothing was removed from the breast itself.  Hope this helps.
