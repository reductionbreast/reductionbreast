How soon can I have a tummy tuck after hernia repair?
Tummy Tuck
Nov 29, 2018
I would wait for at least 6 weeks after hernia repair before proceeding with a tummy tuck. The strength of the repair should be adequate for the next step. Best wishes. 
Severe window shading deformity – Is an explant and lift my best option?
Breast Revision
Nov 29, 2018
For your best result, a lift is needed. If you are bothered by being able to feel the implant, then you should explant them. You are thin so you will always be able to feel them. If you are bothered by the animation deformity, change the implants to a shaped implant and they can be placed in the prepectoral space and your muscle can be returned to the chest wall. A combination approach would be best but ultimately it depends on your goals and what you will be happy with. 
Do I need a lift? What’s the least scarring lift I could get away with?
Breast Revision
Nov 8, 2018
You will be very happy with the shape improvement with a lollipop lift. Do not accept a full anchor. And the Ideal implant is great if you want to stay with saline. The scarring overall may seem scary but it fades and you will be much happier with the result in the long run. Best wishes. 
Is liposuction enough for my stomach or would I need a tummy tuck too?
Tummy Tuck
Nov 8, 2018
Truthfully, you will be far worse with liposuction alone. Based on your photos, you already have excess skin and removing fat will make that excess skin worse. Since you have stretch marks, it also suggests that your skin will not recoil well from liposuction. Go for a full tummy tuck or do nothing. Best wishes.

Are my nipples/breasts too far apart to get a breast augmentation?
Breast Augmentation

Is this a realistic expectation for breast augmentation?
Breast Augmentation
Nov 1, 2018

It also depends on the implant size chosen to some degree. Seek a consultation with a plastic surgeon who offers 3D imaging to get a good sense of your result. Best wishes. 
How bad is the pain with breast reduction leaving my implants in?
Breast Reduction
Nov 1, 2018
Removing your implants will not be as painful as placing them. And a lift at the time would be well tolerated. Th pain is manageable. If you want to be smaller, I would prioritize removing implants as opposed to your own breast tissue. Best wishes.
