How Much Does Breast Reduction Surgery Cost In USA?

Cost Varies Based on a Few Factors

As with most cosmetic surgeries, there is no sure price tag attached to breast reduction.

The cost of your surgery will vary based on a few different factors, including where in the country you’re located, where your doctor performs surgery, and how much tissue needs to be removed.

According to RealSelf users, the average cost in the U. S. for breast reduction, including anesthesia, is just under $6,000.

Today, many plastic surgeons offer options for financing and may also work with your insurance provider so you don’t have to.

The best way to know for sure how much you can expect to pay is by visiting a board-certified plastic surgeon for a consultation!(Arthur M. Cambeiro, MD, Las Vegas Plastic Surgeon)

Boob Reduction Costs Can Vary Widely

The total cost of a cosmetic breast reduction at my clinic is $5000-7000 dollars. Remember, most insurance plans will cover the cost of a breast reduction if a patient presents with signs and symptoms of neck pain, back pain, shoulder grooving and rashes underneath the breast.

Additionally, if the patients daily activities of living are limited by the large size of the breast the insurance is more likely to cover the procedure. For those patients who do not have insurance the cost of a breast reduction is variable.

It depends on the extent of surgery to be performed on the breast. For example, a smaller breast will require less surgery.

A very large breast is going to require more extensive surgical treatment. One must consider the cost of the facility, the cost of anesthesia, the cost of postoperative garments, and the cost of preoperative testing, and the cost of prescription medications.

Another consideration is liposuction of the lateral folds of the chest wall which definitely complements the postoperative result of the breast reduction. Actual total fees can range from $6000-$12,000. A total fee of $9,000 is not unreasonable, when considering total reshaping of the breast. (James Fernau, MD, FACS, Pittsburgh Plastic Surgeon)

COSTS & FINANCING

Breast Reduction can be a complex surgery, and with any complex surgery, the cost can be higher than for other things such as breast implant surgery.

The reason for the complexity is that the breasts have to be reshaped, the nipples have to be moved and the two breast have to be made to look even.

The complexity is increased when the patient begins with two different breast sizes and niplles are off center and at different locations. Typical cost in my practice is $9500 – $20,000 depending on what is needed. (Christopher Khorsandi, MD, Las Vegas Plastic Surgeon)

Breast reduction has one of the highest satisfaction rates seen in plastic surgery. In most cases, this procedure has been covered by insurance carriers.

Recently, we have seen more and more insurance companies not covering breast reduction surgery or denying its medical necessity. When this happens the procedure can still be performed as a cosmetic procedure.
