What's the breast reduction wait period after gastric sleeve?
Breast reduction after gastric sleeve
Oct 13, 2020
There's no definitive wait period, but generally speaking your weight should be stable for at least 6 months before undergoing breast reduction surgery. Always consult with a surgeon certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Good luck!
