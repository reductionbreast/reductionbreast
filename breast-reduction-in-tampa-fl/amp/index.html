How is the reduction of the mammary glands performed?

There are different methods. The easiest way is to reduce the volume of the breast with traditional or ultrasound lipoaspiration. This technique is used quite rarely. It is suitable for young women with predominance in the parenchyma of the fat component. The operation is performed if the skin is elastic and there are no signs of ptosis.

More often this technique is used together with classical reduction mammoplasty. In this case, it is possible to correct the fat folds in the zone of the anterior axillary line and the excess fat in the region of the lateral surface of the breast. Also, a technique using a vertical scar is often used. The incision in this case is done around the areola and continues downward, in the direction of the inframammary fold. This method is suitable if the patient has medium-sized mammary glands.

If the breast size is large, it will be necessary to make a cut around the areola, vertically in the direction of the fold of the breast, and then horizontally.

The postoperative scar in this case has the shape of the letter “T” inverted. After the operation, painful sensations, bruises, swelling occur. Pain relief is provided by pain medication if necessary. But in most cases it is very moderate.

Postoperative complications

Breast reduction, like any operation, involves certain risks. These include:

  • Loss of sensitivity of the skin of the breast and nipples;
  • scarring;
  • Breast asymmetry.

Dr. Abraham Marcadis Reduce Breast

Loss of sensitivity (temporary or permanent) may occur due to the inevitable intersection of nerve branches. The risk of this complication is higher in operations on very large glands. In such a situation, it is impossible to simply displace the nipple with the underlying tissues, that is, they must be separated.

The size of the scars is affected by various factors, including a predilection for smoking and complications in the form of infections or bleeding. Breast asymmetry can occur if the surgeon, due to lack of proper qualifications or experience, does not accurately remove excess tissue. In this case, there may be a phenomenon such as an incorrect location of the nipples or their location is not at the same level.
