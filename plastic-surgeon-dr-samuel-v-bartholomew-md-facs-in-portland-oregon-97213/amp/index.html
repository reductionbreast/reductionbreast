Can I undergo elective cosmetic surgery with cancer?
Treat your cancer first
Oct 2, 2020
Hello,Without knowing your medical history I will give you some general observations.
Most patients that get a lumpectomy have radiation to the breast afterwards. Radiation may start 4-8 weeks after the lumpectomy. You may not feel like undergoing an elective cosmetic surgery for awhile after radiation. Always ask your PS what they recommend in terms of a delay. I would not recommend any elective cosmetic surgery until your have recovered from your chemo, lumpectomy, and possible radiation….

Areolar vs inframammary incision for implant removal and en bloc capsulectomy?
inframammary incision for implant removal and en bloc capsulectomy
Oct 2, 2020
I prefer an inframammary fold (IMF) incision for en bloc capsulectomy and implant removal in most cases unless a patient’s areola is very wide. It also depends on the size of the implant, as a larger implant with the capsule would be harder to get out en bloc thorough a periareolar incision. Good luck.
