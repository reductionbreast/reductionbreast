SEROMA OR HEMATOMA
Dec 23, 2018
HI!
A seroma and hematoma are different things - both are fluid and create swelling but generally a seroma occurs after the drains are out, and a hematoma usually only occurs shortly after the surgery and can be painful, and is accompanied with bruising.
