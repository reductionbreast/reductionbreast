Breast Reduction Surgery In Colombia

The shape and volume of the breast are laid at the genetic level, and the woman can not significantly affect the development of the breast.

A voluminous breast always attracts attention, emphasizes sexuality and is a real reason for pride.

But in fact, large breasts can become one of the causes of pain in the neck and back, frequent abrasions on the shoulders, which are formed from the bra straps.

Large breasts increase the likelihood of infection and rash under the mammary gland, especially during the hot season. Breast reduction surgery is recommended if a woman experiences discomfort at household loads, with deterioration of sleep, inability to perform various exercises.

Breast reduction methods

Dr Alex Campbell, MD, Before And After

There are several ways to reduce breasts. Each of them is selected strictly individually after a thorough examination by a doctor and a plastic surgeon and in accordance with the wishes of the patient.

The most safe is the operation to reduce the breasts with the help of liposuction. Liposuction is the pumping of subcutaneous fat. The advantage of liposuction is that after this operation there are no visible scars and scars.

Unfortunately, liposuction can be done only for those patients who do not need to significantly reduce the size. If the breast is lowered down, then this method will not give the desired result. Fatty mass will be pumped out, but the mammary gland will remain the same hanging down.

Breast Reduction Procedure In Bogota

One of the popular methods of breast reduction is the operation with a vertical short seam. This method is recommended, if it is necessary to remove no more than 28,21 oz of adipose tissue from one breast.

The operation provides for a small incision from the nipple to the lower fold of the breast. As a result, a good cosmetic effect is achieved, the nipple sensitivity remains completely, and the breast takes on a natural shape. This operation has practically no complications.

Classics mammoplasty is breast reduction with T-shaped seam or “anchor incision”. The incision starts from the nipple and goes downward, where it conected with seams that passes under the pectoral fold.

This technique is well developed by plastic surgeons, it is recommended to reduce the mammary gland of large volume. The operation allows you to get rid of a fairly large amount of adipose tissue.

In severe cases, for example, when the breast is enlarged very much, and the doctor diagnosed gigantomastia, a simple operation with one suture will not be enough. In gigantomastia, amputation (excision) of the breast is performed, the nipples moved to the place of the formed mammary glands.

Unfortunately, in this complex operation there is a risk of poor tissue survival. Nipples take root for a long time and remain insensitive. This method excludes the subsequent possibility of breastfeeding. This operation is very dangerous and is only used in special cases.

Breast asymmetry as the cause

In women is quite common natural asymmetry of the breasts. In some, it is almost invisible, while in others it is expressed quite brightly. Plastic surgeons recommend in this case to perform a breast lift or a change in its size.

And you can do this in two ways: by increasing the smaller and decreasing the larger. Techniques for determining the desired shape and size are selected after agreement between the doctor and the patient. In the case of a reduction in one breast with asymmetry, surgery is performed on only one breast.

Contraindications to surgery

There are a number of contraindications to breast reduction surgery. These include: cardiovascular diseases, diabetes mellitus, the presence of tumorous diseases, a violation of blood clotting, venereal diseases, lactation. After breast-feeding must be at least 12 months prior to surgery.

Scars after surgery

Doctor Fulvio Correa, MD Reduction Mammoplasty

All the sutures after the operation will be swollen and red, but eventually it will disappear, they will become almost invisible. Scars heal well, turn into thin lines around the areola of the nipple. So, you can safely wear a blouse with a deep neckline or a swimsuit.

The seam, which is under the pectoral fold, will become completely invisible. Axillary sutures cause discomfort only for the first 1-2 weeks, while remaining imperceptible to others. The final form of the sutures is acquired after 6-12 months (the period depends on the chosen method of operation and the amount of excised tissue).

If, after this time, the stitches are still clear, it is better to contact a cosmetologist to solve this problem. You can remove scars with a laser. There are also special creams that promote resorption of sutures.

The result of the operation, you can evaluate by looking at the photo reduction of the breast before and after surgery.

The breast after the operation becomes taut, neat, close to the ideal form. Reduction mammoplasty can restore elasticity to the mammary glands, so you will feel more confident and comfortable.

Recovery after reduction mammoplasty

After the operation there is a change in the color of the tissues and swelling, which eventually disappears. Painful sensations that arise after surgical intervention can be controlled by drugs that relieve pain. After 1-2 days, surgical drainage and gauze bandages can be removed. External seams are removed after 1-2 weeks. During the recovery period, plastic surgeons recommend wearing compression garment, avoiding excessive physical activity, sleeping on the stomach. It is necessary to avoid lifting weights within a month to ensure that the seams are well healed and there are no problems.

Cost of breast reduction surgery

The cost of surgery depends primarily on the clinic in which you decide to do the operation. The second determinant of prices is the method of the surgery. Each of the operations has its complexity, and therefore the price. The average price range for breast reduction ranges from 3,000 to 8,000 US dollars.
