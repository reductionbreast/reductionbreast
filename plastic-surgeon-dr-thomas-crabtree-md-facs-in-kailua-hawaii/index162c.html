Can you use J-Plasma/Renuvion on top of the skin of the body as well as under the skin of the
renuvion over the skin
Nov 8, 2018
Facial resurfacing with Reunion is a new technique with remarkable results. It also works very well for skin tightening (shrink wrapping) when used under the skin in multiple areas - abdomen, arms, knees etc. Using it over the skin in those areas will not tighten. The way the procedure tightens th skin has to do with contraction of the underlying element of the skin not just the skin itself. Great question though. Dr C
Renuvion/J-plasma Skin Resurfacing - How Long to Wait After Rhinoplasty to Get it?
renuvion and rhinoplasty
Nov 8, 2018
Both could be done in the same period but ideally you would space them several weeks apart to minimize any confusion over swelling and even potentially hidden infection even though that's very unlikely. Good luck. Aloha. Dr C.
"Internal Lift" with Breast Explantation?
internal lift

The skin contracts, the breast reshapes and the end result often is very satisfying. Ask your board certified plastic surgeon. Aloha. Dr C
Implants Were Removed 2 Weeks Ago, and my Nipples Are Caved in
collapsed nipple
Nov 8, 2018
Your collapsed nipple is almost certainly a result of scar tissue either directly under the nipple (if that's how the implant was placed) or as a result of the new capsule. Whatever the case there are a few techniques that could significantly help - like fat grafting. Your surgeon may want to wait a few months though to let the scar soften some. Ask them about options. Aloha. Dr C

If you have adult asthma, can you still undergo a tummy tuck? It's under control
asthma and surgery
Apr 4, 2017
The single most important aspect of any elective surgery is to perform it safely. Well controlled asthma is a common condition and does not prohibit one from getting elective procedures like tummy tucks as long as you are well controlled and your anesthesia provider is made aware. If you are on any kind of steroids for your asthma (other than inhalers) make sure your surgeon and anesthesiologist are aware as well. Good luck.

2 wks post op TT. Why does my incision line look knotted and bulky? Can I start using the sca
Normal healing
Apr 4, 2017
The pictures are very helpful and show what appears to be normal helping for two weeks. The incision line still has significant swelling at two weeks and the stretch marks on the upper part of the incision swell even more than the "regular" skin next to them. As such you get a wavy appearance but this is temporary. It is too early to place any scar strips. Talk to your surgeon about the best time.

Is it true that the body can absorb a seroma? After 17 days mine seems to have disappeared.
Seroma
Dec 26, 2016
Seromas routinely absorb on their own provided they are not too large. How large depends on the area of the body and the type of procedure performed. Sometimes however it is best to have them drained. This is usually an easy office procedure. Please follow up with your surgeon and have them examine you. Good luck.

Aloha

Dr C

11 days post Vaser. Right knee is 2 inches bigger. Is it a seroma?
Knee swelling after VASER
Dec 26, 2016
I do not think it is a seroma but you should see your surgeon. At 2 weeks it is very common to have asymmetric swelling. This can be caused by how you sleep, the fit of your post op garment or just the way you are healing. One thing to check is to make sure you have a garment that goes below the knee. If it stops above the knee it may exacerbate this. Good luck.
