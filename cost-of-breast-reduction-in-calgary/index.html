Indications for breast reduction surgery in Calgary

Hypermastia (excessive development of mammary glands). This condition is usually accompanied by a significant degree of ptosis of the mammary glands.

Preoperative marking of breast reduction is performed with the participation of the patient. As a result of the operation, a circular scar is formed along the border of the areola and a T-shaped scar.

The horizontal part of this scar is located under the mammary glands and is practically invisible in the future.

The visible part of the scar is vertical. The visible vertical scar is a disadvantage, however, the geometry of the breast is such that it is possible to achieve a cosmetically acceptable conical shape of the gland only by using such a marking to form a visible vertical scar.

After breast reduction surgery

It is necessary to wear specialized compression underwear. Terms of wearing compression underwear after the operation is determined individually for each patient (mean – 2 weeks).

The terms of rehabilitation after the operation of breast reduction are 10-14 days. The observation in the hospital for 3-4 days and then on an outpatient basis.

Breast reduction surgery, called “reduction mammoplasty” in Calgary does not eliminate the effects of gravity and age factors in the future.

Medical science has established that reduction mammoplasty (breast reduction) does not affect the condition of the mammary gland tissue and can not be the cause of the development of various forms of mastopathy or breast cancer.

Nevertheless, before the operation of breast reduction, the condition of the mammary glands should be examined by ultrasound or consulted by breast physician in order to exclude already existing processes in the mammary glands.

During the reduction mammoplasty, histological examination of the mammary gland tissues is possible.

CONSEQUENCES AND POSSIBLE COMPLICATIONS

Usually reduction mammoplasty, performed by a qualified and experienced surgeon in a good clinic, has no complications.

However, there are exceptions, because far from everything depends on the skill of the doctor, the peculiarities of the organism of the woman itself also have significance. Possible complications after reduction of mammary glands:

PREGNANCY AND BREAST FEEDING

During pregnancy, and also during lactation, reduction mammoplasty is not performed. Planning the operation can be no earlier than six months after the termination of breastfeeding. It is possible to plan pregnancy only 10-12 months after the operation to reduce the mammary glands.

After mammoplasty, breastfeeding is contraindicated, therefore, after childbirth, urgent measures must be taken to immediately stop lactation. But still, doctors advise to conduct a similar operation for women who have reached the age of thirty, who in the future do not plan to give birth to children.
