Reduction mammoplasty in Latin means “breast reduction”, which is performed for women with too large or opposite undeveloped breasts.

The indication for reduction mammoplasty may be congenital bilateral or unilateral asymmetry. In such a case, either augmentation of the small breast or a reduction in the larger breast is performed.

Preparing for Reduction mammoplastyin Phoenix is a long process that begins several months before surgery. You need to take a blood test, urine, biochemical blood test, blood for HIV, RW, HBS, HSV, ECG, fluorography, ultrasound, mammography (x-ray method for examining the mammary glands for benign and malignant tumors), get a therapist’s conclusion, go to a dentist.

The operation is performed only after for girls 18-19 years old. Consultation with a doctor is the most important stage of preparation for a breast reduction surgery.

You should inform your doctor about all diseases and important nuances of your health, pathologies, chronic diseases and so on.

You should not conceal anything deliberately, it is strictly forbidden, because you, therefore, can create a health risk situation in which the doctor will not be aware. It will also be necessary to consult an anesthesiologist.

At least 2 weeks prior to surgery, you should quit smoking, stop using alcohol, as well as any medications without the knowledge of the doctor.

Before the operation, you should prepare comfortable clothes and shoes, preferably without fasteners, since after the operation your movements will be limited.

Reduction mammoplasty

Breast reduction surgery in Phoenix is performed 2-3 hours under general anesthesia or local anesthesia with intravenous sedation. During the operation, excess fat and other mammary gland tissues are excised, stitches are applied.

The operation begins with the disinfection of the operated field, then an incision with a scalpel is performed. There are several options for performing the operation: liposuction, L-shaped incision, T-shaped incision and excision (amputation) of the breast with nipple transfer.

Breast reduction by liposuction is done in just 1 hour. With this technique, the nipple is not affected, hence its sensitivity is preserved. But this method is suitable for a slight reduction in breast size.

Healing occurs in just 1 week, while with other techniques of performing breast reduction, healing takes 4 to 6 weeks. There is a technique in which the pumping of adipose tissue is carried out through a tube inserted in a small incision less than a centimeter in length. Again, this method is suitable for a slight reduction in breast size.

The most difficult and traumatic operation is the excision (amputation) of the breast with the transfer of nipples, which is performed by women with gigantomastia. During the operation, the nipple is completely separated from the skin flap, and then placed on the formed breast.

The operation is very heavy, performed in extremely rare cases, the sensitivity of the nipple disappears, as during the operation all nerve endings are dissected.

If it is necessary to remove more than 28.2oz of tissue from one breast, a breast reduction using the T-shaped incision is performed.
