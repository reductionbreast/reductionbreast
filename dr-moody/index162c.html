Breast tingling and sharp pains 6 weeks post op
Tingling and sharp pains after breast augmentation
Oct 27, 2017
During breast surgery small sensory nerves may be injured. These nerves try to regenerate themselves over time. As these nerves regenerate (regrow) there may be associated tingling and sharp, sometimes shooting pains. Nerve regeneration may last for several months or even longer and therefore so will the tingling, sharp, and sometimes shooting pains.
