Breast lift versus implants
Aug 28, 2017
Start with a breast lift and if still unhappy with the upper pole fullness you can always get an implant. The combination procedure of lift + implant has a high revision rate and you already have plenty of breast tissue to begin with.

There are a few ways to determine the size of breast augmentation to give your desired look. Find some wish photos to share with your surgeon. Best, Dr. Yegiyants
