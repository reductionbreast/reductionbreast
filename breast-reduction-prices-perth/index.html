Women, who are unhappy with the shape and size of their breasts, can be divided into those who want to enlarge breasts, and those who need to reduce it.

If in the first case the operation is done solely to improve aesthetics, then breast reduction is often a necessary operation to improve a woman’s health.

Plastic surgery to reduce the size of the breast is called reduction mammoplasty in Perth.

Reasons for breast reduction surgery

A specialist should determine if a woman has a reason for breast reduction and how serious they are.

He explains the complexity of the procedure, adjusts for the result, acquaints with possible risks and rules of behavior in the postoperative period.

Far from all the owners of a large breasts consider it a drawback, and only serious health problems may cause them to think about reducing it.

The weight of the breast more than 0.5 kg gives a greater load on the spine, which significantly increases the risk of acquiring the following problems:

  • Pain in the thoracic spine and in the neck, giving to the shoulders;
  • Mastodynia – pain in the chest area;
  • Difficulty breathing (as for women the chest type of breathing is characteristic);
  • Irritations, intertrigo and inflammation of the skin under the breast;
  • Difficulty in diagnosing mammary gland disease;
  • Limitation of physical activity.

All these problems are only aggravated with age. Because of the uneven load on the spine, there is osteochondrosis and scoliosis, and the back and neck are subjected to a heavy load, which causes migraine and muscle pain.

Preparation for breast reduction surgery

If a decision is made to perform a breast reduction surgery in order to increase the chance of a successful outcome, the following conditions must be met before the operation:

  • Examination of the breast physician and a mammogram;
  • If you follow a diet to achieve a final result before the operation, as weight reduction after surgery will adversely affect the appearance of the bust;
  • Eat plenty of vegetables and fruits, meat and fish. In addition, you can take vitamins;
  • Smokers should quit smoking at least a week or two before the forthcoming surgery, since smoking is a negative factor in healing after carrying out reduction mammoplasty in Perth;

Stop taking medications that reduce blood clotting (such as aspirin) two weeks before surgery and do not take them within a week after surgery.

It should be noted that breast reduction surgery is not recommended for women who intend to breastfeed in the future, women with high-grade obesity, as well as with heart, kidney or respiratory system diseases.

Breast reduction surgery

In plastic surgery, breast reduction surgery is considered one of the most difficult, given the large volume of tissue fragments to be removed, as well as the need to maintain blood circulation in the areas to be moved.

In order to correctly choose one of the methods of reduction mammoplasty, you need to know that:

  • Large breasts can be reduced to small sizes;
  • Modern surgery allows you to perform a breast reduction operation in one stage without any references to further corrective operations;

    • Large Breasts Can Be Debilitating

  • After the operation, the nipples with areolas should look natural, the diameter of the areolas, if necessary, can be reduced;
  • The operated breast should not be lactated, therefore the operation can be planned six months after the cessation of lactation and at least one year before pregnancy.
  • Currently, in countries with developed insurance medicine, breast reduction surgery is included in the insurance policy as reconstructive plastic surgery and is done free of charge, solely for the purpose of improving women’s health.

Immediately before the operation, the surgeon applies the lines of the proposed incisions to the patient in the standing position, depending on the characteristics of her structure and the wishes for an aesthetic result after a reduction in the breast.

During the operation, removal of excess glandular and adipose tissue, as well as excess stretched skin is carried out. In most cases, the nipple moves to a new position, and a new breast is formed from the remaining tissues.
