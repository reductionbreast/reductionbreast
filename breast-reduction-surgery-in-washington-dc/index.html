Breast Reduction Surgery In Washington DC

Breast reduction surgery in Washington in a professional language is called “reduction mammoplasty.”

Too large breasts can cause aesthetic dissatisfaction of a woman. However, too heavy a breast becomes the reason of many physical ailments and interferes with its owner to conduct an active way of life (running, fitness and so on).

Some women are owners of such an excessively large breasts that the bra straps leave deep marks on their shoulders, until the appearance of ulcers.

On the skin in the fold under the mammary glands, there may be sweating and irritation. A massive breast creates an increased strain on the spine and can cause back and cervical pain, as well as migraine attacks (headaches).

Breast Reduction Significantly Reduces The Size And Weight Of Cumbersome Breasts

Such breasts require reduction not so much in aesthetic, as for medical reasons.

With the onset of menopause, the level of female sex hormones in the blood decreases, as a consequence, the ligament supporting the breasts and the skin become thinner and lose their elasticity. Breasts sag.

In such cases, usually resort to surgery for breast lift. Other reasons for the deterioration of shape, the appearance of sagging breasts is significant weight loss, irreversible stretching of tissue due to changes in the breast size during pregnancy and breastfeeding, refusal to wear a supportive bra breasts (with a large breast size).

Once the breast loses its shape, no exercise or therapeutic measures can no longer improve it, and even more so to return the original appearance.

If breast correction becomes a necessity, a radical, surgical method is used reduction mammoplasty.

During Reduction Mammoplasty, Excess Breast Tissue And Fat Are Permanently Removed From The Chest

The objectives of reduction mammoplasty:

Restoration of the form of the mammary glands with a simultaneous decrease in their volume. Achieved by reducing the size of the breast with a simultaneous tightening (reduction mammoplasty).

It should be noted that reducing the mass of the mammary glands leads to a decrease in the load on the spine (reducing the frequency of exacerbations of osteochondrosis) and neck muscles (migraine headaches).

Breast size reduction (reduction mammoplasty).

Technically, breast reduction surgery and breast lift have much in common. The incision is made along the edge of the areola and then vertically downward. With a large excess of skin, the vertical line can be continued horizontally in the fold under breast.

The result is achieved by removing the strip of skin and tissues of the mammary gland. The nipples thus move upward to take an aesthetically advantageous position.

But it should be noted, that reduction mammoplasty in DC is more complicated, in comparison with a breast lift. The duration of reduction mammoplasty can reach up to 3-4 hours. The operation is performed under general anesthesia. In the clinic, the patient usually spends 2-3 days.
