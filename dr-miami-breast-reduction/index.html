Dr Miami Breast Reduction

Here you will find prices and photos on breast reduction performed by Dr Michael Salzhauer (Dr. Miami).

These prices and photos are taken from free sources on the Internet (such as personal site and instagram of the surgeon, realself.com, other reviews sites like yelp.com).

Breast reduction is a surgical procedure that removes excess fat, skin and breast tissue, and also aims at raising the breast and reducing the size of the areola in women who have too large a breast size.

Ideal candidates for a breast reduction operation are women with large breasts who suffer from pain and discomfort, both physical and psychological.

Due to the reduction of the breast, the operated patients can significantly improve their appearance and make sports more comfortable, reduce pain in the neck, shoulders and back or completely get rid of it, correct bad posture and get rid of skin problems of the breast.

Dr Miami Photo

Also, patients always notice improvement in the quality of life and become more confident in themselves.

When a patient undergoes a breast reduction they also secondarily receive a breast lift. The difference between the two procedures are: With a Breast Reduction we are removing extra breast weight that has contributed to symptoms; such as back problems, neck strain, bra strap irritation.

Because of the symptoms from Breast enlargement, insurance may cover the breast reduction surgery. With the breast lift the surgeon only removes breast skin and leaves the nipple attached in order to preserve sensation. With the removal of the predetermined skin, the breast is lifted into a more perky position.

The breast lift is considered a cosmetic procedure and is not covered by insurance. Both procedures take about 2 hrs to perform. The Breast Reduction may require a over night stay in hospital and the breast lift can be done on a outpatient basis.

The recovery after each procedure is basically the same.
