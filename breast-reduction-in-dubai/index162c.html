Breast Reduction In Dubai

Breast reduction (reduction mammoplasty) in Dubai is a surgical technique for reducing the size of hypertrophied mammary glands, improving their shape and contours.

During the breast reduction surgery, excision of excess skin, fat and breast tissue is performed, as well as the movement of the nipple-areolar complex to a higher position.

Reduction mammoplasty also reduces the size of the areola and nipple.

Excessively large breasts (gigantomastia) can be caused by heredity, hormonal failures, a sharp increase in body weight.

This problem is most often observed in the adolescent period, after childbirth or with the onset of menopause.

Often, too large breasts are not only aesthetic, but also a medical problem, since it gives the patient discomfort during movements, sports, causes heaviness and pain in the spine, violation of posture, breathing, the appearance of sweating in the pectoral fold.

Preparation for breast reduction surgery

Like other types of mammoplasty, the planning of a breast reduction surgery involves monitoring the general and biochemical parameters of the blood, coagulating systems, examining the electrocardiogram, examining the mammary glands (ultrasound or mammography), and examining by the breast physician.

During the consultation with the surgeon, the wishes of the patient and the expert’s opinion are listened to and discussed, the question of the shape and optimal size of the breast is decided.

The patient is compulsorily informed about the features of the course of the operation and the postoperative period, possible risks and complications.

At the planning stage of the breast reduction surgery, the necessary measurements are taken, the skin condition, the actual shape and size of the mammary glands are evaluated. Breast reduction surgery is not recommended for patients planning future pregnancies.

Carrying out breast reduction surgery

Breast reduction surgery in Dubai is performed under general anesthesia and lasts from 1.5 to 3 hours. Reduction mammoplasty is one of the most complex and traumatic operations on the mammary glands. Currently, there are many techniques for performing breast reduction surgery.

After carefull examination of the patient, the surgeon chooses one of the methods according to her wishes. The most common method is applying a short (vertical) seam, which runs from the nipple down to the pectoral fold.

This technique is used to remove no more than 800 g of tissue from each gland. Classics of reduction mammoplasty is a method of reducing the breast using a T-shaped incision (anchor suture) that descends down from the nipple and connects with the line of the incision passing in the pectoral fold. This method is used when it is necessary to excise a large volume of tissue.

Regardless of the chosen method of breast reduction, reduction mammoplasty should help reduce the volume and preserve the shape of the breast, preserve the sensitivity of the nipples and lactation function, and create the most cosmetic scar.

During the operation, exess of the tissues of the breast, skin and fatty tissue are removed, the nipple and areola are moved to a new, higher position.

Then the skin flaps, surrounding the areola, are juxtaposed and a new contour of the breast is formed. Tissue are sutured with special cosmetic sutures.

Sutures are usually masked around the areola, under the breast and in the pectoral fold. In the course of breast reduction surgery, up to 1 kg of tissues can be removed from each mammary gland.

Postoperative scars pale after 2 months after reduction mammoplasty, although for 1-2 years can be sufficiently visible, until finally ripe. The final results of breast reduction surgery are evaluated after 4-6 months.

After breast reduction surgery

The period of hospitalization after breast reduction surgery is 1 to 2 days. Sensitivity and soreness of the chest is noted during the first postoperative day. Painful sensations in rest and during movement are stopped by taking analgesics. After surgery, the mammary glands look swollen and restrict movement.

To avoid seams tension, active movements by hands are not recommended, especially their upward lifts. Immediately after the operation, the patient wears special compression underwear, which should be worn around the clock for 3-4 weeks. In the future, it is mandatory to wear everyday bras that provide adequate support to the breasts.

Physical activity and exercise is prohibited for 1 month, followed by a gradual resumption. However, it should be remembered that early physical activity promotes tension and stretching of the scars, so that they can become wider. The final stabilization of scar tissue occurs 3 months after the breast reduction surgery.

Complications and risks of breast reduction surgery

In addition to the usual postoperative complications, the probability of which is not excluded during any surgical intervention (bleeding, inflammation, etc.), after breast reduction there are specific risks associated with the removal of breast tissue. The risk of such complications is higher among smokers, elderly, full patients and those who have a large volume of tissues removed.

Often, after breast reduction, there is a decrease in the sensitivity of the nipple and surrounding skin, a decrease in the size of the nipple-areolar complex. The most serious complication is insufficient blood supply to the breast with the development of necrosis of the nipple.

With a significant reduction in the breast, accompanied by the removal of a large volume of glandular tissue, some of the milk ducts can be crossed, which entails the risk of losing the possibility of breastfeeding.

Relieving Your Physical Pain And Discomfort

After performing breast reduction surgery may experience breast asymmetry or unaesthetic, unnatural form, mismatch resulting size and shape of the breast of the patient’s expectations. To avoid such situations, you need a thorough planning, measuring and marking the breast before breast surgery reduction.

The condition of postoperative scars depends on the features of the healing processes and compliance with the technique of the operation. With a favorable result, the scars after a while turn pale and become inconspicuous. However, traces of breast reduction surgery around the nipples and on the lower surface of the chest remain for life.
