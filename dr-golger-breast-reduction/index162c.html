Will I be able to go out 6 days post op in a car to an outing that requires minimal movement?
BBR Post OP
Nov 19, 2013
You should be fine to go out for an event 6 days post breast reduction surgery. As long as you feel well and have no issue with hematoma or infection, ensure that you are well hydrated and have your anemia under control. Remember to not lift anything heavy and make sure you inform your PS. All the best.
