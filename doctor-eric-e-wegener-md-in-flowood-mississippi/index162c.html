Can Dysport cause numbness and twitching in your cheek and droopy eyelids?
Dysport
Dec 17, 2017
I am sorry you are unhappy.
Dysport and Botox are essentially the same product. Both temporarily paralyze muscles to soften wrinkles in the skin that is directly connected to those muscles. Luckily both are temporary, lasting only 3-6 months (3-4 months is more realistic).
Neither product should cause numbness as they do not affect sensory nerves.
I recommend you return to your physician to discuss your concerns.
Good luck.

Why did my stitches split 3 weeks post-op?
Sutures
Dec 17, 2017
Diamond,
Routinely surgeons use buried absorbable sutures to minimize scarring. The sutures take 90-120 days to be fully absorbed. It is not uncommon for these sutures to sometimes "spit" out. Rarely are spitting sutures a cause for concern.
Contact your surgeon's office for a follow up examination.
Good luck.
