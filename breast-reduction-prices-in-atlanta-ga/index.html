Breast Reduction Prices In Atlanta GA

Reduction mamoplasty in Atlanta, GA is a plastic operation to reduce the breast by removing excess skin, glandular tissue and fat.

Thanks to this surgical intervention, it is possible to minimize the burden on the spine associated with a large bust, and literally make life easier.

Indications for breast reduction surgery:

– Too large bust size (D size or more), which causes daily discomfort;
– Sagging of the breast after pregnancy or breast-feeding of the child;
– Ptosis of skin tissues due to age-related changes;
– Asymmetry of mammary glands.

Breast reduction: recovery of the body

Breast reduction surgery involves a phased recovery period:

  • On the 5th-7th day, seams are removed.
  • During the first 2-3 weeks, the basic swelling of the tissues disappears.
  • After 4-6 weeks it is allowed to remove the compression underwear.
  • After a month, the result of the operation is evaluated.
  • After a month and a half you can gradually return to the usual activity.
