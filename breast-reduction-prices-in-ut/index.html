Breast Reduction Prices In Utah

Reduction mammoplasty is an operation that is aimed at reducing the volume, correcting, improving the shape and tightening the mammary glands. In the overwhelming majority of cases, this operation is performed for medical reasons, since a large breast often causes a lot of physical discomfort for a woman, and may also be a prerequisite for the development of psychological complexes.

The load on the spine, back pain, sleep disturbance, difficulty finding clothing and fitness – these are the reasons that overwhelmed the scales and forced to seek help from a plastic surgeon with a request to perform an operation to reduce the size of the breast.

An important factor is the aesthetic appeal of the breast. It’s no secret that large breasts have a tendency to more rapid ptosis, to change their outlines in conjunction with the formation of “stretch marks” on the skin.

A Bust Reduction Can Help Relieve The Pain And Physical Discomfort

Consultation

Consultation of a plastic surgeon is necessary to determine the indications and contraindications to the planned reduction mammoplasty, as well as to discuss the use of the most appropriate surgical technique in each individual case.

Contraindications to surgery are:

  • Severe systemic diseases;
  • diabetes;
  • oncological diseases;
  • Violations of the blood clotting system.

Due to the risk of breastfeeding after performing reduction mammoplasty, it is not recommended to perform surgery for persons under 18 years of age, or when planning pregnancy in the future.

Operation

Reduction mammoplasty consists in removing excess volume of breast tissue and moving the nipple-areolar complex to a higher position. As a rule, this operation also includes a tightening of the mammary glands.

The technique of carrying out reduction mammoplasty presupposes the presence of two variants of incisions on the skin of the gland:

Breast Reduction Surgery In St George

Vertical from the lower edge of the areola to the submammary fold;
T-shaped with the capture of submammary folds.

Recovery after reduction mammoplasty

After the operation, the patient is staying in the hospital for a day. In the postoperative period, it is necessary to constantly wear compression underwear for a month.

The first few days are unavoidable discomfort, painful sensations in the field of surgery. In this regard, it is necessary to take analgesics in tablet form, as well as an antibiotic for the prevention of inflammation.

Sutures are usually removed on the 7-10th day after the operation, before that you should visit a plastic surgeon for examination and bandaging.

The usual way of life can be returned to the 7-8 days after the operation, but you should remember about the limitation of physical activity during the month of recovery after reduction mammoplasty.
