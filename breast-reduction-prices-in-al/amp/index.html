Breast Reduction Prices In Alabama

Breast reduction surgery can perk up the appearance of your breasts and reduce the burden on your frame.

Breast reduction surgery re-models and reduces the size and weight of your breasts in harmony with the rest of your body.

Excess skin and breast tissue are removed. The nipple is lifted upwards and reduced if necessary. The breasts are reshaped in accordance with the consultation discussion and brought back against the chest muscles in order to obtain an improved appearance.

Breast reduction is carried out under intravenous sedation, meaning that you will be asleep during the operation.

A breast reduction cannot be done without any scars and the different techniques will be discussed at consultation with you.
