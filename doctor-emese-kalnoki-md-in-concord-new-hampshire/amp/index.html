Do I need a lift with my implants?
Do I need a lift with my implants?
Dec 14, 2018
After viewing your photos, the better question to ask is "What appearance do I want after a breast augmentation?"  A dual plane augmentation that is performed lifts the nipple slightly (maybe 1cm), but generally only adds back lost volume.  If you feel that the aesthetic breast is more perky, then you would require a lift to get that appearance.  It is best to be evaluated by a plastic surgeon to discuss your desires.
