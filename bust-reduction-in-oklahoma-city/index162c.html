Bust Reduction In Oklahoma City

Often, the cause of excessively large breasts (hypertrophy) lies in heredity, hormonal imbalance, a sharp increase in weight.

And this problem is not only aesthetic, but physical and psychological. The spine has a heavy load, which leads to pain in the back and lower back. In addition, women develop complexes: she begins to feel shy.

This problem can be solved with the help of reduction mammoplasty, which allows to restore the symmetry of the mammary glands, reduce the breast and give the woman greater emancipation.

The essence of the operation to reduce the size of the breast is that the mammary gland removes an excessive stretch of skin and some of the fat tissue, and the nipple moves higher.

In addition, the dark zone at the nipple (areola) decreases.

Breast Reduction Removes Excess Breast Fat, Glandular Tissue And Skin

Women with small breasts often envy ladies, in relation to whom nature was more generous. At the same time, women with lush forms in turn sometimes dream of having a breast of a more compact size.

After all, large breasts can cause serious inconveniences, including pain in the cervical and thoracic spine, difficulty breathing during sleep, intertrigos in the crease under the breast and so on.

Often this list is replenished and a certain psychological discomfort. In such cases, the most effective solution is breast reduction surgery – in the language of plastic surgery it is called reduction mammoplasty.

Breast reduction in Oklahoma City is the best way to achieve harmonious forms:

  • If the excessive volume of mammary glands is the cause of physical discomfort;
  • If a woman does not satisfy the appearance of her breasts.

Reduction of mammary glands can be combined with a decrease in the areola of the nipples (if necessary, this operation is carried out separately).

Enhancing Your Appearance With Breast Reduction Surgery

Breast reduction surgery in Oklahoma City is one of the directions of work of our clinic. The solid experience of specialists and the use of innovative techniques allow us to correctly predict the aesthetic result and to conduct such large operations with a minimum risk of complications.

Reduction of mammary glands

The operation to reduce the mammary glands is carried out under general anesthesia. During surgery, the surgeon removes excess fat and skin, moving the nipple with the areola to normal position. Reduction of the mammary glands does not require a long stay in the clinic after the operation – as a rule, the patient can go home a few hours after the operation (if necessary, the doctor can always be reached by phone), and in the morning to come for an examination. After 5-7 days, you can return to the usual rhythm of life. The full result of the procedure can be assessed in 2-3 months.
