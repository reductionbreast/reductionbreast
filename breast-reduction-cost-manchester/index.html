Breast Reduction Cost In Manchester

Breast reduction in Manchester is a surgical operation whose purpose is to reduce the mass and volume of the mammary glands by removing excess glandular tissue and subcutaneous fat.

The reasons why women want to reduce their breasts are different. Someone from the severity of the mammary glands is broken posture and there are pains in the back.

Someone’s skin is rubbed with straps from a bra, someone because of the big difference between the volume of the chest and the waist can not choose clothes.

Indications for breast reduction surgery

Indications for breast reduction may be primarily physiological and psychological problems in women with large breasts, such as: regular pain in the thoracic and cervical spine, deformity of the skeleton, respiratory disorders, intertrigo and psychological discomfort caused by excessive volumes of mammary glands.

Reduction of mammary glands in Manchester is indicated for the improvement of the shape of the mammary gland, which is lost after breast-feeding and at the expense of loss of skin elasticity in middle-aged women.

Course of breast reduction surgery

In reduction mammoplasty, in addition to the skin, fat and tissue of the gland are also removed. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, duration 1-3 hours depending on the volume of the breast. The minus in these operations is a sad necessity to make quite long incisions.

Scars after such operations are located around the areola vertically from the nipple to the pectoral fold. With a giant breast, a classic scar is made in the form of an inverted “T”.

Inevitable and noticeable scars after this operation will put you before a difficult choice – whether it is worthwhile to be operated at all. These operations, like, however, all others, must be done when it is really necessary.

In a hospital patients after such operations are two days. Suture removal on the 12th day after surgery. During the month, it is necessary to wear a special, supporting breast, bra around the clock.

Postoperative period

There are practically no pain sensations after operations on breast reduction. Our patients do not use painkillers. Postoperative edema and bruising are moderately expressed in these operations, disappear after 1-2 weeks. However, for a while, there may be increased fatigue, against usual fatigue, and it is desirable to reduce the load before full recovery.

Postoperative swelling can cause a temporary loss of normal sensitivity of the skin and nipples, but it takes about 6 weeks.

In some patients, sensitivity is restored only after a year, for some it is lost forever. The breast takes the final form 6-12 months after the operation. Subsequently, the shape of the breast can vary depending on the hormonal changes, weight fluctuations, pregnancy.

This operation, like all other plastic operations, allows to quickly change your appearance. You will get rid of physical and mental discomfort. The feeling of harmony will return to you.

Complications after breast reduction surgery

With all the complexity and painstakingness of reduction mammoplasty in patients, as a rule, there is only one reason for postoperative discontent. We mean the quality of scars. Due to the severity of soft tissues, skin features, constant friction of clothes, these scars, unfortunately, can not be made unnoticeable, although during a period (6-12 months) they undergo significant changes for the better.

As with any surgery, there is a risk of complications, including bleeding and infection. In addition, the mammary glands may differ slightly in size or location of the nipple.

At large resections, loss of ability to breast-feeding is possible, since during the operation the milk ducts that go to the nipples are removed. These operations are recommended for women after feeding the children.

In any operation there is a risk. Breast reduction is not an easy operation, but being carried out by a qualified plastic surgeon is safe. We have successfully carried out such operations for more than 13 years.

Reduction of the giant breast

The most difficult are the operations to reduce giant mammary glands. In such cases, it is difficult to achieve a good aesthetic result. Therefore, before the operation, you need to warn these patients that after 6 months you may need a re-operation to correct the shape of the breast.

The larger the chest, the longer the process of recovery and longer the process of lightening the scars. It depends not so much on the skill of the surgeon, but on the individual characteristics of the organism and the way of life after the operation.
