Breast reduction surgery in Dominican Republic is performed for women who are concerned about too large breasts.

This operation is carried out not only for aesthetic reasons, but also for the purpose of eliminating physical discomfort: back pain, breathing disorders or skin inflammation.

Breast reduction is a surgical procedure in which too large, saggy breasts, lost elasticity, reduced and tightened, it is attached to the natural form.

Operation

Breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia. During the operation, the lowered nipple is reduced and pulled up, excess breast tissue is removed. A vertical breast lift or a T-shaped lift is performed.

Postoperative period

The patient can return to work after 7-12 days. Swelling of the breasts, bruises may persist for several weeks.

A Surgical Procedure Aimed At Raising And Improving The Shape Of The Female Breast

It is recommended to restrict physical activity for several months

Risk of complications

Like every intervention, breast reduction surgery can have undesirable effects, such as reaction to anesthesia, temporary swelling, pulling sensation, decreased nipple sensitivity, postoperative pain, hematomas.

Very rarely bleeding occurs, infection of the wound, hypertrophy of the scars. Too large a sagging breast causes the owner of a lot of psychological and physical suffering.
