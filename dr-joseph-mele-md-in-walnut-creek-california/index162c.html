Gynecomastia vs Pseudogynecomastia
Apr 11, 2019
Gynecomastia is growth of the glandular tissue beneath the nipple. It usually feels like a firm, rubbery mass under the skin. Pseudogynecomastia, literally "fake" gynecomastia, is caused by disproportionate fat and is soft. Often a little of both is present, so I approach this with a combination of liposuction for the fat and direct excision of the glandular component.
