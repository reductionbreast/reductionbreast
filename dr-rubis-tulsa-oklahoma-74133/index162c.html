2 weeks post op and still pretty sore with muscle spasms frequently, is that normal?
Post breast augmentation muscle tightness
Apr 11, 2019
Congratulations on your surgery. It is completely normal to continue to have muscle tightness at this point. The cycle of spasms can be different for all patients, based on muscle tightness and implant size. The roller coaster generally starts to subside around the two week mark, but can take up to several months before you get to your final result. Give it time, following your doctor’s instructions and restrictions. Ice packs can help as well. Best wishes!
