I had a bad experience. I can’t feel my nipples after breast implant surgery. Is there a solution?
Nipple sensation following breast augmentation
Jan 31, 2019
Great question. This can be a common issue following breast augmentation. Changes in nipple sensation following surgery are related to multiple factors. Especially if your surgery was done recently, it is common to have changes in nipple sensation. It can take weeks to months for the sensation to return to normal and sometimes there can be permanent changes. It can also be related to implant size with larger implants possibly affecting sensation more. …
I am 74 and have AFib. Healthy otherwise. Can I have breast implants?
Breast Augmentation and Medical Comorbidities
Jan 30, 2019
Thank you for sharing this question! Afib is certainly not a contraindication for breast implants. The number one goal in any surgery is to ensure that it is done safely under optimal conditions. You would likely need medical clearance and management of any anticoagulation therapy you may be taking. You should seek evaluation with a board certified plastic surgeon with whom you can discuss your options. Good luck!

Too Much Botox
Nov 1, 2016
As others have said, the effects of Botox last around 3-4 months and things should go back to normal by 6 months. It has been shown that when you get Botox regularly (every 2-3 months for years) it may take longer for the effects to go away, which may explain why celebrities who get frequent injections have even longer lasting effects. I hope it all works out for you and get the look you desire. Best wishes.
