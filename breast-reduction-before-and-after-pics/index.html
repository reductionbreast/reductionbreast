Look at the pictures before and after breast reduction. Here you will find photos of the work of surgeons from around the world. Check the professionalism of the surgeon before surgery. The name of the surgeon who performed the surgery, is specified under each photo.

Breast reduction surgery is aimed at removing excess tissues of the breast and adipose tissue, reducing the size of the areola, and tightening the chest, giving it a more natural shape, reducing the load on the chest and shoulder girdle.

The operation significantly reduces pain in the neck and thoracic spine

The results of the operation usually last a long time, although weight fluctuations and hormonal changes can cause a change in the shape of the breast

Indications: Excessive volume of the chest, leading to pain in the back, limiting the freedom of movement

Duration of treatment: 2 – 4 hours

The most popular variant of the operation is the technique of vertical scar, when the cut is around the areola and down towards the fold of the breast. With a large chest volume, the incision is in the form of an inverted “T” or (anchor)

Recovery period: Reduced breast sensitivity, edema, bruises, moderate soreness. You can continue to work after 10-14 days. The examination period is 1-3 days (depending on the time that you have).
