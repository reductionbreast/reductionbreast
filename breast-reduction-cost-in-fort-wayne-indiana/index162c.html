If you approach the plastic surgery of the mammary glands from a mechanical point of view, then all the aesthetic defects here are determined by the ratio of the mammary gland skin and the filler, as well as the position of this entire structure on the front chest wall.

What happens:

The dermal cover is sufficient or tight, the filler is small, there is no ptosis of the breast, then to increase the aesthetic result it is necessary to increase the volume of the filler, therefore, we perform breast enlargement with implants.
The dermal cover is flabby and stretched, there is enough filler, there is ptosis. It is necessary to restore the position of the breast and remove the excess skin cover, therefore, we perform a breast lift.

Skin cover is redundant, there is a lot of filler, ptosis is expressed. In this case, we reduce the breast and tightens it.

In principle for breast lift and reducing the breast can be used three options for the operation:

Periareolar. The scar ends only around the nipple. This type of breast lift is most often used in combination with breast augmentation and is possible only with minimal ptosis.
Lollipop. This type of operation is also called a lollipop, since the scar is around the areola and down to the crease under the gland and resembles a lollipop. Quite often performed operation, it is also used for breast lift and for breast reduction during ptosis of mammary glands 1 and / or 2 degrees
Anchor or inverted T-lift. The scar has the shape of an anchor or an inverted T and goes around the areola, down to the crease under the gland and in both directions along the fold. It is used for breast lift and breast reduction at ptosis 2 and / or 3 degrees.

Breast lift and breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of such operations is from two and a half hours. Before the operation, you need a consultation with a specialist and examination:

Examination necessary for breast lift and / or breast reduction:

  • Fluorography or radiography of chest organs;
  • ECG (electrocardiography);
  • Ultrasound of the mammary glands (if necessary, consultation of the breast physician) for women younger than 40 years old;
  • Blood test
  • General analysis of urine
  • Screening coagulogram
  • Fibrinogen
  • Blood on the group and Rh factor
  • Pregnancy test
  • The conclusion of the therapist with the results of the tests is MANDATORY!

The results of the tests are valid for 14 days.

Possible contraindications

Among the contraindications for breast lift and breast reduction is usually allocated: severe diseases of the cardiovascular system, diseases that affect blood coagulability, cancer, acute inflammatory diseases, diseases of the mammary glands, accompanied by the formation of cysts, inflammatory diseases of the skin over the mammary glands, unrealistic expectations of the patient.

Recovery period

As for the features of the rehabilitation period, there should be a lot of patience. The shrinkage of tissues and the resolution of edema takes a long time and can take several months. It is compulsory to wear compression underwear and limit visits to baths, saunas, solariums, gyms for 30 days.

It is also necessary to take into account, that a person adapts to any changes in his appearance psychologically within 3 months. Therefore, there is no need to look every day in the mirror. Step inspections and assessments should be done once in two weeks.
