Breast Reduction Surgery In Fort Lauderdale

Large breasts cause many women inconveniences (pain in the cervical and thoracic spine, disturbed breathing during sleep, intertrigos under the fold of the breast) and psychological discomfort.

Breast reduction in Fort Lauderdale is a surgical procedure that eliminates the above inconveniences and cosmetic defects. As a result – beauty, excellent state of health and ease of choosing clothes.

Indications

– Discomfort due to excessive volume of mammary glands;
– The shape of the breast is not satisfying to the woman.

Operation technique

The surgical procedure for breast reduction is performed under general anesthesia. During the operation, excess fatty tissue, gland and skin tissues are removed, and the nipple and areola moves to the normal position. In connection with the fact that the excess volume of the breast is always combined with its ptosis, the volume of the gland is not only redistributed but also decreases. The duration of the surgical procedure is 2-3 hours.

Recovery after breast reduction

It is recommended to stay in the hospital for the first 2-3 days. After 5-7 days, you can do everyday things. The effect of the surgical procedure can be assessed after 2-3 months.

Pregnancy and lactation. Other contraindications

Reduction mammoplasty is not a reason to forget about pregnancy. However, it is better to perform an operation to reduce the breasts of women, who do not plan to have children in the future, since the milk ducts are affected during the operation.

This will call into question the possibility of breastfeeding. It will be necessary to resort to treatment, which reduces the production of milk. And feed the baby with milk formula.

That is why surgeons recommend doing this operation only after reaching the age of thirty years. And after the birth of children. In addition, changes in the hormonal background, which are unavoidable in any pregnancy, can change the shape of the already operated mammary glands.

In very rare cases (with hormonal diseases that cause gigantomastia, and with rare syndromes that cause asymmetric development of the breast), this operation is done by teenage girls and young girls in order to avoid protracted depressions and neuroses. Here, from two evils choose the lesser.

Absolute contraindications to reduction mammoplasty in Fort Lauderdale are the following diseases:

  • Infectious diseases.
  • Severe diseases of internal organs.
  • Oncological diseases.
  • Obesity.
  • Diabetes. With this disease, any surgical intervention is fraught with serious complications.
  • Blood clotting disorders.
  • Incomplete lactation – this is the term about one year after childbirth. Even if you stopped breastfeeding, when the baby was not even three months old.

Preparing for breast reduction surgery, the woman should take seriously the examination to identify possible contraindications.

It is recommended to undergo a comprehensive mammological examination. Namely: ultrasound of mammary glands, to make a mammogram, to undergo clinical and laboratory examination (the main thing is to study the hormonal background: hormones of the pituitary gland, sex hormones, thyroid hormones), to have the analysis for oncomarkers, hemostasiogram (blood coagulability analysis), clinical analysis of blood and urine and other tests recommended by the doctor.

It is necessary to quit smoking, at least temporarily, a couple of weeks before the operation. And to have preliminary consultation at the surgeon and the anaesthesiologist. At a preliminary consultation, the surgeon will choose the mode of operation and give recommendations for the recovery period, depending on the size and shape of the breast, age and health status of the woman.
