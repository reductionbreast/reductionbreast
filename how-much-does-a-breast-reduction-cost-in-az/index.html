Breast Reduction Cosmetic Surgery With Dr Arun Rao

Breast reduction (reduction mammoplasty) in Arizona is a therapeutic and aesthetic surgical intervention, as a result of which the breast volume decreases and its shape significantly improves. During the operation, a part of the mammary gland is removed and, if necessary, the areola (the nipple-areolar complex) moves to a higher and correct position, both anatomically and from the point of view of aesthetics. The sensitivity of the areola and nipple is preserved.

This surgery, aimed at reducing the size of the breast, helps to eliminate a number of female discomforts, such as aesthetic dissatisfaction, physiological and neurological disorders. As a result of this correction, you will find a breast of the ideal shape and size that suits you, perfect well-being, forget about constant tension in the back and neck area, and will be able to fully enjoy the ease of choosing underwear and clothes.

When is breast reduction surgery advisable?

  • enlarged mammary gland causing discomfort
  • a large load on the back and neck (result – headaches, back and neck pain)
  • uneven load on the spine, problems with posture
  • restrictions when playing sports
  • irritation and intertrigo of the skin under the breast
  • difficulties with the choice of clothes and in the selection of underwear (unpleasant pressure of the bra)
  • dissatisfaction with disproportionately large breasts.

What are the contraindications for breast reduction?

  • general therapeutic contraindications, such as: disturbances in the work of the heart, respiratory system, liver and kidneys
  • hormonal disorders
  • infectious diseases
  • active oncological processes
  • disorders of the endocrine system
  • not recommended for women planning pregnancy and breastfeeding in the coming year
  • venereal diseases.

How to reduce breast size?

The technique of breast reduction surgery in the clinic:

Breast reduction surgery in Arizona requires general anesthesia and stay in the hospital for at least one day, as well as monitoring in the postoperative period for at least 7-10 days. During surgical intervention surgeons remove excess fat and glandular tissue, excess skin, and the areola moves to anatomical and aesthetically correct position, in relation to the new volume and shape of the gland.

Breast Reduction Surgery Not Only Lifts And Reshapes The Breasts

Due to the fact that excess breast volume is directly related to its ptosis, not only the reduction of the gland volume, but also its redistribution is carried out. On average, the duration of the surgery is between 1.5 – 2.5 hours. Plastic surgeons have different techniques for performing a breast reduction operation, which makes it possible to achieve the best aesthetic result.

Recovery period:

During the first 24 hours after surgery, you are under close supervision of experienced and caring doctors and nurses. After a day, after examination by a plastic surgeon of our clinic, you can be discharged from the hospital. The first month after the operation, you will be advised to limit physical activity. During this period it is necessary to wear special underwear. The first time after the operation, there may be some swelling of the breast tissue.

Most often, these are temporary symptoms that will soon disappear. The expected aesthetic result can be estimated first after the first 10 days, and the final result can be estimated after 6 to 8 months, when the postoperative scars become barely noticeable.

Is It Better to Have Breast Reduction Before or After Pregnancy?

If possible, it is better to plan pregnancy and carry out childbirth and breastfeeding with this volume of the breast, despite physiological and aesthetic inconveniences. Breast reduction surgery should be performed after the birthchild, but not earlier than 6 months after the end of lactation.
