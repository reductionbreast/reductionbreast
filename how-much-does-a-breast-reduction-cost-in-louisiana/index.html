How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Louisiana?

Usually women dream of a big and beautiful breasts that would attract the look of men and arouse the envy of other girls. But there are cases when it is necessary to reduce the breast.

This can be caused by both aesthetic desires and physiological indications.

During breast reduction excess fat tissue is removed, the surgeon forms a new, beautiful and symmetrical breast.

Another name for this operation is reduction mammoplasty in Louisiana. During this procedure, you can reduce the size of the nipple, change the shape of the areolas.

Gigantomastia is an excessive breast enlargement. It can appear, due to disruptions in the endocrine system, a sharp increase in body weight, and also because of heredity.

There is not so much information about the role of genetic predisposition yet, so that it is possible to speak with confidence about the fact, but this factor should be borne in mind. The problem of a large breast can appear in adolescence, when its formation occurs or after childbirth.

Anchor suture leaves behind more visible scars, which drop from the nipple and diverge in the pectoral fold. Thus a fairly large volume of tissue is removed at one time.

Regardless of the chosen access, the operation should lead to a decrease in the volume of the mammary glands, but with the preservation of such functions as the sensitivity of the nipple, lactation. In the course of the surgery, the excess of adipose tissue is removed, the skin, the nipple is transferred to a new place, and the tissues are connected by means of cosmetic sutures.

Sutures usually turn pale after a few months, they can be corrected with a laser. The first results are visible right after the procedure, and the final result can be estimated in six months. Painful sensations after surgery are eliminated by taking analgesics in the early days, then swelling subsides. A slight discomfort can cause a rise of hands up.
