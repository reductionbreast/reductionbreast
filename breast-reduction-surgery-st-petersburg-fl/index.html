Breast Reduction Surgery In St. Petersburg FL

For many women, a large, hanging, heavy breast, which recently appears especially often at a young age, brings a lot of discomfort, with which the spine begins to deform, there are diaper rash and other skin problems. This often causes not only severe pain, but also leads to various kinds of diseases. Given this problem, many women are forced to turn to a plastic surgeon for a breast reduction service.

The main thing in the procedure of breast reduction is the removal of a certain amount of skin from the mammary glands, a reduction in part of the skin, a change in the location of the nipples and areola, a decrease in the areola.

The operation breast reduction.

Breast reduction surgery is considered to be the most difficult, since when removing excess tissue it is necessary to preserve blood supply in the skin-fat flap, which is cut to move the nipple and areola to a new, higher position.

The operation is performed under general or intravenous anesthesia. Before the operation, the surgeon conducts preoperative markings, which will remove the tissue, and then create a new shape.

Seams will be made only three: the first – under the breast, horizontal, the second – vertical, to the areola with the nipple, and the third – circular, around the areola.

The Breast Reduction Procedure Can Take Between Two To Four Hours

After breast reduction

Within five days after breast reduction in Saint Petersburg, FL, you should perform daily elastic bandaging and dressings. In the period up to three weeks after the operation, loads and exercise should be avoided. It is necessary to wear a bra, always for one month.

After the termination of the anesthetic, the operation area for several hours will be sensitive and painful, and slight nausea caused by an anesthetic is possible. However, pain after breast reduction is usually weak, much weaker than after breast augmentation.

Many patients are treated with light analgesics. Position in the bed on the back with an elevated headboard. The period of stay in the hospital usually does not exceed 2-3 days. Swelling is usually not heavy, but it hurts and pulls only with certain movements and disappears after about 3 weeks.

What Happens During The Breast Reduction Procedure

Sutures are removed within fourteen days. Patients are recommended to wear supporting sports bras all day and night for the first three weeks and the day after.

For three weeks, you should avoid exercise and exercise, and then gradually resume them. Do not raise your hands high above your head, as this causes excessive tension in the seams. Any physical activity (including sex), which strains and stretches the scars before they stabilize, can make them wider.

It takes about three months for the scar tissue to reach its full strength. During this time (and some surgeons are limited to 1 month) and recommend wearing a bra. The result of the operation can be judged after about six months, although the scars can mature after this period and be still reddish with a half-year inspection.

Sealing scars with a surgical tape for a long time (up to six months) helps to accelerate their maturation.
