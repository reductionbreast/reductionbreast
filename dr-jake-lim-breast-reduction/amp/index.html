I had a breast reduction 1.5 years ago and have significant scarring. Is this normal and any suggestions for treatment?
Feb 11, 2019
Thank you for your question.
Scar maturation after a breast reduction takes at least one year, however this varies for each individual. It is difficult to tell from the photos without examination. I would suggest that you wait a further six months for the colour to improve. In most cases, the scar will improve.
All the best.
