The choice of method and progress of the operation

There are many methods of breast reduction surgically, and the doctor determines the optimal approach, based on the clinical data, as well as the wishes of the patient.

For example, you need to assess the volume of tissue to be removed, the possibility of breastfeeding in the future, whether nipple correction is needed and so on. This will determine the cost of breast reduction surgery.

The surgeon indicates the lines of future incisions, excludes excess fatty tissue, parts of the mammary glands, from the remaining tissues forms a bust of the desired shape and size.

With a decrease in breast size, the areola nipples are not separated from nerves and vessels, they rise higher, the skin of the breast stitches under them. After the operation, the fluid will accumulate in the chest. And the surgeon installs drainage for its removal.

Advanced specialists in breast reduction apply internal seams, which eventually dissolve independently. Such seams allow to better fix the renewed breast shape, reduce skin tension, while external seams are healing. The patient wears a special fixative bandage, which also reduces the tension of the tissues.

Recovery

Usually, after a breast reduction surgery in Corpus Christi, the tissues change their color, and also swelling appears. These are normal phenomena that will eventually disappear.

Within a couple of days, drainage is removed, and 1-2 weeks after the surgery, the surgeon removes the seams, and the patient needs to wear compression underwear to avoid complications after the breast reduction surgery.

Doctors carefully monitor the condition of patients during recovery in the hospital and outpatient supervision. The cost of recovery is included in the price of breast reduction.

Recovery period. Postoperative Scars

After the breast reduction surgery, the mammary glands, naturally, ache a bit. The skin in the chest area can become supersensitive. Therefore, in the immediate after surgery, the doctor prescribes pain medications. Do not be scared! Edema, swelling of the chest after surgery, painful sensations, bruises will go away in a few days.

In the first week after the operation, one should not raise his hands too high, since this gesture causes tension in the superimposed seams and can sometimes lead to their rupture.

In general, within a month after reduction mammoplasty, it is recommended to limit physical activity to a minimum. You should not visit at this time a sauna, a solarium and a swimming pool, sunbathing. Postpone for a time and air travel: the pressure drop during the flight can affect the result of the operation.

After 14 days, the stitches are removed. But in modern clinics where a good suture material is used, you do not need to remove the stitches after the operation. The sutures dissolve within two weeks.

In non-smoking women, the recovery period is much easier and faster. If you smoke, try to refrain from this bad habit three weeks before the operation and as long as possible – after.

Getting Started With Breast Reduction

Breast reduction surgery requires the patient to wear special compression underwear on average three weeks. If you neglect wearing a special bra, you can quickly pull the chest a short time after the operation.

Can start deterioration of blood supply to the nipple, until the development of tissue necrosis. There may also be a breach of symmetry in the mammary glands.

Scars after the operation at first look frightening: swollen, purplish red. But gradually they will turn pale and become flat. In any case, the surgeon will take care that the scars are placed in such a way as not to interfere with a woman wearing decollete clothes.

Finally, you can assess the aesthetic appearance of the “new” reduced breast only six months after the operation.

By then, the scars should fade. But if then the scars will bother you with their appearance, you can make their grinding using the laser correction method. Listen to the doctor advice, and everything will be fine!
