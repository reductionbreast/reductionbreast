How is breast reduction surgery performed?

The essence of the surgery is to remove and redistribute part of the gland and the surrounding skin, the nipple and areola are moved to the normal position. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, with an average duration of 2-3 hours.

Postoperative recovery

After the operation, the patient remains in the clinic under observation for 2 – 3 days, after which the patient is on an outpatient observation.

In the clinic, for the early recovery of patients, special programs for postoperative recovery with the use of modern cosmetology techniques and apparatus were developed.

After the operation it is necessary to constantly wear a special compression bra for 6 weeks. After 5 days after the operation, you can remove the underwear for a short time (for example, take a shower).

All the sutures are performed internally with a self-absorbable thread, so you do not need to remove it. Control examinations are performed 1, 3, 6, 12 months after the operation.

Recovery

Staying in a hospital under constant supervision of a doctor is necessary for one to two days after the operation. On the third day, a repeated examination is mandatory, the bandages and drains are removed and the patient is discharged home.

After reduction mammoplasty, as well as after any operation, it takes time to repair damaged tissues. First, a woman may be troubled by pain, which is mainly due to swelling. However, pain can be easily treated with anesthetics.

The recovery period after the operation is about a month. About ten days after the operation, you must avoid any physical exertion, and within six months – too heavy.
