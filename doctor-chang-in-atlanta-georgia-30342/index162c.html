Constriction of the Breasts
May 17, 2017
Constriction of the breasts may present in various degrees. If you have a truly constricted breast, usually you have a small base, large areola and breast herniation (tissue bulging out) through the areola. If this is the case, you need tissue released at the base, reduction of the areola and silicone implant placed above the muscle. This way, you can have a wider & rounder looking breast.
