Beautiful lush breasts always attracts not only the male gaze. Women with a small breast size envy women with big breasts, dreaming of having the same.

However, women themselves, awarded with big forms, do not always believe, that they were lucky. Excessively large breasts often lead to various medical problems: skin irritation, back and neck pain, posture disorder.

With age or after breast-feeding, its shape worsens, it sags with loss of elasticity. In such cases, reduction mammoplasty in Oklahoma is performed, in which, apart from fat and skin, part of the gland is removed.

The breasts are given a new shape, slightly different from the previous one, the nipples move to a new position.

Breast reduction surgery is not so much aesthetic as necessary for medical indicators, because there is no other way to reduce breasts.

Breast reduction is a rather complicated operation, requiring high qualification of a specialist. There are several ways to reduce breast.

The most common and well-tried is the method of breast reduction with the help of a T-shaped seam, leaving long horizontal and vertical scars.

In our Oklahoma clinic, methods of vertical mammoplasty are used, which without a long horizontal scar, and the dimensions of the available ones, like their number, are minimal.

The patients themselves, referring to the results achieved after the operation, consider these tracks to be quite an acceptable “pay” for a good breast shape.

Breast reduction is not performed during menstruation. Reduction mammoplasty is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation is 2-4 hours. After the operation, the patient wears special compression underwear. In a hospital are from 2 to 3 days, depending on the state of the body.

After the operation, you need to follow the doctor’s recommendations carefully. During the first month, it is recommended to wear a special elastic bra.

Approximately within 12 weeks after breast reduction in Oklahoma the patient should not lift weights and exercise, associated with physical exertion on the upper body. The mammary glands usually heal not simultaneously, therefore in the postoperative period their asymmetry is possible, which will disappear in the future.

How is breast reduction surgery performed?

Any operation begins with preparation. For this, it is necessary to have all the tests: urine analysis, blood test for HIV, biochemical blood test, ECG, ultrasound, fluorography, mammography, get a therapist’s conclusion. Only after this, after making sure that there are no contraindications, it is possible to start the operation.

At least 2 weeks prior surgery, you must quit smoking, drink alcohol and any medications. If there is a need for medication – you need to warn a plastic surgeon about this. The operation is a serious stress for the body, so you must switch to the correct diet and regimen at least a month before the operation.

After preliminary consultation, the surgeon usually makes the necessary measurements and assesses the possibility of using the most suitable surgical method. After this, the desired location is determined and an operation plan is drawn up. This involves photographing and marking, which serves to guide the incisions line and mark areas to be removed. The operation is performed under anesthesia, which means it does not cause painful sensations.

Along the marked lines, neat large incisions are made, excess skin is removed. After this, excision of excess glandular adipose tissue is performed. The remaining tissue in the breast is formed taking into account the desired reduction in the breast. In recent years, plastic surgeons use absorbable internal sutures. It is allow to fix a new shape, reduce skin tension until full tension of the skin.
