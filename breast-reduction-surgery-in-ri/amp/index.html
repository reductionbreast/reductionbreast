Breast Reduction Surgery In Rhode Island

Reduction mammoplasty in Rhode Island is an operation aimed at reducing the size and shape of the mammary glands.

Most often, this operation is carried out for medical reasons, since the large size of the breast causes not only psychological complexes, but also physical inconveniences, which often develop into medical problems.

Women with large mammary glands often experience pain in the cervical and thoracic spine, they find it difficult to choose the size of underwear and clothes, they experience discomfort during sports.

The essence of the operation

The breast reduction surgery involves the removal of excess tissue and fat gland of the breast and the subsequent movement of the nipple-areolar complex to a higher position.

After Breast Reduction, The Breast Is Reduced In Size

Contraindications

  • Severe diseases of internal organs
  • Diabetes
  • Violation of blood clotting
  • Infectious and oncological diseases
  • Unfinished lactation

Reduction mammoplasty is not recommended for persons under 18 years of age, as well as for those who plan pregnancy because of the risk of problems with breastfeeding.

Techniques

Surgery Breast Reduction Recovery With Doctor Lee Edstrom

Among the main methods of breast reduction surgery are:

  • Breast reduction with incision in the form of an inverted T (anchor)
  • Reduction with a vertical incision without scars under the breast (method of M. Lejour)

The duration of the operation takes 2 – 4 hours.

Anesthesia

The operation is performed under general anesthesia.

Stay in the clinic: Stay in the hospital usually takes one day.

Recovery period

Within a month after the operation is necessary to wear a special compression garment. In the first days, a slight increase in temperature is possible. Postoperative swelling begins to subside 2-3 days after surgery. In the recovery period, it is necessary to exclude visits to baths, saunas and swimming pools, as well as active physical activity.

Chest Reduction Surgery Can Alleviate The Symptoms Of Back And Neck Pain

The resumption of normal activity – You can start working 1-2 weeks after the operation.

The final result of the operation can be assessed as a rule, 2 months after the operation.

Complications

Possible complications of breast reduction surgery includes:

  • Pain and discomfort when moving in the initial stage of recovery;
  • Decrease in skin sensitivity in the operated area;
  • Decrease or vice versa increase the sensitivity of nipples;
  • Formation of hematomas;
  • Hypertrophic scars in places of incisions;
  • Asymmetry of mammary glands.

Effectiveness

The result of surgery can be affected by a strong weight gain and hormonal disorders. In other cases, the result is permanent and persists for many years. Scars eventually become invisible.
