Breast Reduction Denver Prices

Reduction mammoplasty (breast reduction) in Denver is a plastic surgery aimed at eliminating the excessive volume of mammary glands.

Quite often it is combined with a breast lift, which allows not only to reduce the mammary glands, but also to give them a new shape.

Reduction mammoplasty in Denver involves the removal of excess skin and fatty layer, in some cases – partially glandular tissue.

The purpose of plastic surgery is to create a breast of this size so that it proportionately matches the patient’s complexion. There is a gradation in weight: up to 500 g is a breast of small and moderate sizes, up to 1000g is a large size, and very large is more than 1500g.

Best Breast Reduction In Denver

During the operation, 300-600 g of tissues are removed, but for a significant effect, which relieves pain in the spine, it is necessary to remove at least 500 g from each breast.

Indications for breast reduction in Denver

Surgical intervention is performed for patients with gigantomastia (very large breasts), which gives them aesthetic and physical discomfort. Problems are associated with the selection of clothes and underwear, excessive sweating under the glands and the appearance of irritation there, back pain, especially in the cervical spine, changes in posture, increased fatigue, and scuffing of the skin at pressure points of the bra’s slips. Such women are limited in sports, they develop faster ptosis of the mammary glands.

Contraindications

  • acute respiratory viral infection;
  • Exacerbation of chronic diseases of the respiratory system and cardiovascular system;
  • Severe somatic conditions (severe cardiac, respiratory, hepatic, renal failure);
  • Bleeding disorder.

In any case, the question of conducting an operative intervention is decided individually by your surgeon. If necessary, additional examination and consultations of doctors of narrow specialists are appointed.

The course of the operation

Before the breast reduction, the doctor takes photos of the patient’s mammary glands, measures some parameters and applies markings. The operation is performed under general anesthesia. In accordance with the marking, the surgeon makes access.

Two types of incisions are most often used for breast reduction: vertical and an anchor. The first option is preferable for a small amount of surgical intervention: an incision goes around the areola and descends vertically down to the pectoral fold.

Anchor access is also done, but is supplemented with a horizontal incision under the breast. This technique is recommended for use when combining reduction mammoplasty and breast lift to achieve the maximum aesthetic result.

Bust Reduction Surgery Is Intended To Give You A More Attractive Breast Contour

After dissection, excess fat and glandular tissue is removed, excess skin is excised. The nipple and areola, not separating from the blood vessels and nerves, are moved to a higher position.

If necessary, then the areola can be reduced so that it proportionally matches the new breast size. Then, a drainage tube is installed in each mammary gland to remove the accumulating fluid, the edges of the wound are sewn and a sterile bandage is applied.

In situations where a woman has a gigantomastia with a high degree of ptosis of the mammary glands and requires significant surgical intervention, the movement of the nipple with the areola becomes possible only after complete separation. Thus, after such an operation, sensitivity and lactation are often lost.

Postoperative rehabilitation

In terms of duration, the recovery period after breast reduction surgery is 1-2 months. Drainages and bandages are removed after 2 days. External sutures are removed after 2 weeks. In a week you can return to work.

Bruising and swelling disappear within a few weeks. Scars remain bright until six months, then become poor. Within 1 month, it is recommended to wear compression underwear, first time to sleep on the back, 6 months to avoid intense physical activity.
