How Long Does Botox Last?
Botox for facial rhytids
Dec 28, 2016
Botox is a fantastic treatment for wrinkles in several areas of the face including the glabellar region (between the brows), the forehead, and the periorbital region (crows feet). The length of the effect is somewhat variable but is usually in the 3-4 month range. In some people the effect can last as short as 2 months or as long as 6 months. The rate at which it wears off is dependent on how fast your body replaces the blocked neurotransmitter receptors.
