Probably, no body is subject to such variations in shape and size as the female breast. The same can be said about the aesthetic ideal of this part of the body.

But if you do not take into account exceptions, then most people like an elastic, natural-shaped chest of medium size or, perhaps, even slightly more than the average.

Problems arise in women with excessively developed full breast or normal volume breasts, but of poor shape.

Often problems with the shape of the breast occur after the birth of the child and in women in middle and older age due to loss of skin elasticity.

As a rule, this causes psychological discomfort, which can seriously affect personal life. If the breast is not hypertrophied, but lost shape due to loss of skin elasticity, the operation is performed without excision of the gland tissue and fat, only excess skin is removed.

Doctor Ben Kirbo Breast Lift Without Implants

This type of operation is called mastopexy. If a patient wishes to see her breast more full, but it is impossible to create a beautiful shape of larger volume with her tissues, simultaneous installation of implants is possible.

Women with very large breasts have various medical problems caused by excessive weight – back and neck pain, skin irritation, deformity of the skeleton and respiratory failure.

For such cases, there is a method known as reduction mammoplasty in Tallahassee. During this operation, in addition to the skin, the fat and tissue of the gland is also removed. Breast becomes smaller, lighter and denser.

In recent decades, reducing mammoplasty has been constantly improved, many variants of the operation have been developed by leading plastic surgeons of the world, which are used depending on specific circumstances.

They differ mainly in the shape and position of the incisions on the mammary gland.

Dr. Larry Harper Breast Lift Before And After Pictures

Classical and the most common so far is the incision, after which the scar is obtained in the form of an anchor or an inverted T.

However, the sad need for fairly long incisions in this operation causes surgeons to continue searching for new methods, for example through incision, which results in a scar in the form of the letter L.

Talking with your doctor, the patient does not make sense to insist on a particular method of operation, even if it is described in the large women’s magazine as the most popular. The surgeon will estimate the size and shape of your mammary glands and suggest the best method.

First of all, the patient should clearly imagine: if the deformity of the mammary glands has become a problem due to excess fat tissue or flabby skin, then there are no other reasonable options, except for the surgical one. At the first conversation with the surgeon, you need to discuss your wishes in detail and listen to his opinion. Each patient, as well as every doctor, has his own point of view regarding the ideal size and shape of the breast.

Breast reduction surgery in Tallahassee is done in the hospital under general anesthesia and lasts from 2 to 4 hours or more. The surgeon removes excess fat, the tissue of the gland itself, the skin and moves the nipple and areola to a new position. Then the surgeon combines the flaps around the areola, forming a new contour of the breast. Tissues are sewn with special seams. Sometimes liposuction can be used to remove fat from the axilla.
