A beautiful breast of good shape has long been a symbol of women’s attractiveness. But here’s the question: What does a perfect breast look like? Should it be full and round or better small and elastic?

The shape and size of the breast must comply with the rest of the body. Only in this case the woman will be a long time to feel good and radiate natural attraction.

Breast reduction

Big breasts can often cause discomfort to women not only during sports, but also in everyday life.

Excessively large breasts can often cause patients emotional experiences or lead to severe pain in the back and shoulders due to the load on the spine.

Many patients complain of headaches and migraines, which can also be a consequence of a disturbance of the body’s balance.

Bust Reduction Surgery By Doctor J. Garrett Harper

In this case, surgical breast reduction in conjunction with a tissue lift can significantly reduce the burden on the musculoskeletal system and lead to the rapid elimination of symptoms.

As a result of surgical removal of glandular and adipose tissue, the weight of the mammary glands decreases. The method used for this depends on the shape and size of your breasts. Our goal is to achieve optimal proportions with the formation of the minimum possible scars. For aesthetic reasons, in most cases it is required to move the nipples slightly upward. In the course of such an operation, it is also possible to eliminate the existing difference in the size of the mammary glands.

During the visits of the doctor’s patients before the operation, it is possible to solve the problem of a new arrangement of areolas and nipples. Also, the problem of a new breast size is solved. The operation lasts from 2.5 to 3 hours, and after the operation it will be necessary to stay in the hospital for 2 to 4 days.

Recovery in the Charlotte clinic will take from one to three days. After 10-14 days, the stitches will be removed, and after 3 – 4 months the postoperative scars will become almost invisible, and you will be able to enjoy the changed body for the better, forgetting about the former discomfort.
