Do I have tuberous breasts? What procedure would you recommend?
Do I have tuberous breasts? What procedure would you recommend? (photos)
Jul 20, 2015
From the picture submitted, it appears that the left breast might be tuberous in shape. It's hard to tell if the right breast is tuberous. The procedure I would recommend would depend on whether you were trying to correct the asymmetry, the shape of the breasts, the size of the breasts, or all three. If all three are of concern, then I would do a differential augmentation mammoplasty, and some sort of mastopexy on the left breast, depending on the physical exam.

Do I need a lift even with implants after weight loss?
Do I need a lift even with implants after weight loss? (photo)
Jun 11, 2014
If it has been a least a year since your weight loss and you still have little skin retraction, then I believe you will need a bilateral mastopexy with your augmentation. In my opinion, a breast augmentation alone would result in a surgical outcome in which you would not be happy.
