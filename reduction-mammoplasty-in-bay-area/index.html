Reduction Mammoplasty In Bay Area

Breast reduction surgery in Bay Area is a procedure for women who, because of large breasts feel back pain and shoulder girdle, faced with limitations of physical activity, psychological and emotional discomfort.

The purpose of this procedure is to remove excess fatty or glandular tissue and skin, and change the shape of the breast so that it fits better with the proportions of the body.

In addition, simultaneously with breast reduction, a breast lift is also performed, so the location of the nipple and areola varies.

When breast reduction, at the request of the patient, you can reduce the size of the areola.

Operation

A lot of different techniques are proposed, how to reduce breast, but most often the operation is performed with a cut in the form of a so-called “keyhole”.

Breast Reduction Candidate By Doctor Eric Mariotti

Depending on the size of the breast, the degree of ptosis of the nipple and the various risk factors that are discussed between the surgeon and the patient, a decision is made: the nipple and areola complex will be tightened while maintaining its own innervation and blood flow, or transplanted to a new place as a free graft.

The patient should know that, regardless of the method chosen for breast reduction surgery, the post-operative scars will remain on the chest anyway, which, with proper operation and absence of complications, should be insignificant and easy to close with a bra or swimsuit.

The risk of surgery

Like every intervention, breast reduction can have an undesirable effect: it is a reaction to anesthesia, temporary swelling, discomfort, a decrease or loss of nipple sensitivity (if the nipple is transplanted as a free graft, it always remains without innervation).

Less frequent bleeding, infection of the wound, hypertrophy of the scars. Other possible complications and adverse reactions should be discussed with a plastic surgeon during the consultation.

Postoperative period

After the procedure, the patient may be advised several weeks to wear a special bra. Usually, patients return to work after 10-14 days, physical exertion should be avoided for at least several weeks. The final cosmetic result will be visible not earlier than in a few months.
