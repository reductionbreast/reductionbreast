The mammary glands are made up of skin and glandular tissue that are attached to the chest wall by Cooper ligaments.

Between the lobules of the gland and near it there is fatty tissue, the volume of which can fluctuate significantly.

That’s why the size of the breast can vary depending on the diet. So, often in older women, the breast consists solely of fat.

Also hormones affect the size of the mammary glands, which explains the change in breast volume during the menstrual cycle or during menopause.

Contrary to popular belief, there are no muscles in the female breasts, so it is impossible to tighten or increase it with the help of physical procedures.

A Surgical Procedure To Reduce The Weight And Volume Of The Breasts

During puberty or during pregnancy in women, there may be excessive breast enlargement, which is an inappropriate reaction of the mammary glands to hormonal changes in the body.

In such periods, the mammary glands can grow to an impressive size within a relatively short period of time. It is called breast hypertrophy.

The large breast is very heavy, so it often becomes a source of discomfort and chronic pain. Often there are pains in the shoulders, neck, lower back, neck strain, headaches.

In the pectoral folds, as a result of constant moisture, intertrigos develops. It should be noted the fact that women with hypertrophied breasts are more likely than others to have a predisposition to breast cancer.

Women whose breasts are too large complain of a strong restriction of physical activity, as hypertrophied mammary glands are very interfering with daily activities. A huge problem is also the selection of clothes. Reduce the size of the mammary glands by using this operation.

Breast Reduction Before After Ireland

Reduction mammoplasty in Ireland is an operation that is indicated for asymmetry or hypertrophy of the mammary glands and pain in the dorsal and cervical spine. Such an operation can be done at virtually any age, although some surgeons may not refuse to conduct, if the patient is not 30 years old.

During reduction mammoplasty performed the removal of excess fat and glandular tissue, and skin. Often after this, mastopexy is performed – a breast lift.

Due to the removal of unnecessary tissues, doctors form such a breast size that the patient desires to receive, make the breast more taut and elastic. In addition, such an intervention can eliminate the asymmetry of the breast.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

  • Age is less than 18 years, when the mammary glands are not yet formed;
  • Pregnancy and lactation;
  • Endocrine diseases that contribute to the growth of the mammary glands (including obesity);
  • Severe diseases of internal organs (liver, kidney, cardiovascular system);
  • Any tumors and cysts located in the mammary glands;
  • Bleeding disorder;
  • Mastopathy.

If, however, the patient has fatty hypertrophy that occurs most often after childbirth, then in this case, it is quite enough and usual survey, which includes mammography and ultrasound of the mammary glands.

Limitations of reduction mammoplasty

It should be noted that the operation has some limitations:

  • The results of the operation can change during pregnancy, as well as serious weight changes.
  • Reduction of the breast areola can not be done during menstruation, and also four days before and after.
  • Pregnancy can be planned only after 10-12 months after the operation.

PREPARATION FOR BREAST REDUCTION

After examination of the breast, the breast physician will offer you the most suitable variant of the operation and prescribe all the necessary tests. Among the tests are mandatory before mammoplasty:

  • General and biochemical blood test;
  • ECG;
  • HbSAg;
  • RW;
  • HCV;
  • HIV;
  • Chest X-ray;
  • Ultrasound of the mammary glands.
  • blood type and Rh factor.

During the consultation yoy must provide information about the presence of diseases, operations, taking vitamins and other drugs.

One week before the operation, you should stop taking blood-thinning medications (hormones, aspirin, etc.), and give up alcohol.

Like any surgery, reduction mammoplasty involves giving up smoking at least two weeks before the operation.

The day before the operation you need to refrain from a dense dinner. If the patient plans to lose weight, it is recommended to stabilize the weight before surgery, especially if the breast is heavily sagging and decreasing.

Progress of the operation

During reduction mammoplasty, the surgeon removes excess glandular tissue, fatty tissue and skin, and moves the nipple to a higher position. The duration of the operation is 2-3 hours.

Immediately before the operation, the doctor should take a picture of the breast and measure its parameters. After this, a marking of the lines of incisions is made by a special marker and the breast is sterilized.

This operation is performed under general anesthesia, and endotracheal or intravenous anesthesia can be used. The doctor determines the optimal method of performing the surgery, based on the shape of the breast and the volume of “extra” tissues.

Large Breasts Can Also Cause Psychological Distress

In most cases, the seams are in the form of an inverted T, or a vertical seam is used. Anchor reduction (using T-incision) makes it possible to obtain long aesthetic results in operations of any complexity.

However, this method has a drawback – it is high traumatic, which entails a long recovery period. An alternative method of breast resection is vertical breast reduction, which is used for a small or medium reduction volume. This technique leaves smaller scars in comparison with the inverted T-technique.

When necessary incisions are made, the surgeon removes excess skin and fat tissue, while the areola and nipple, which are not separated from the blood supply and nerves, move higher. If necessary, the areola can be reduced.

In some cases, with a strong reduction of large mammary glands, the nipples and areoles must be separated and only then moved to another position. In this case, the sensitivity of the nipples and the possibility of breastfeeding are lost.

At the end of the operation bioresorbable sutures are applied. In most cases, the lines of the incisions strongly turn pale or become completely invisible. However, almost all women, who faced this problem decided unequivocally, that even visible scars on the chest are an acceptable result for an operation that allows to improve the size and shape of the breast.

In addition, today there are a lot of different techniques that solve the problem of scarring. To remove blood and fluid, drainages are installed, and bandages are applied to the seams. The breast is fixed with a compression bra.
