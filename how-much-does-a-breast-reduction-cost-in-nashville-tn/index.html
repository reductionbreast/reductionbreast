How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Nashville, TN?

The cause for breast reduction in Nashville is often serious health problems, but sometimes it’s just the desire of women with a large bust to change by reducing their appearance.

Breast reduction or is a plastic surgery of the breast, which leads to a reduction in the excessively large breasts, in specialized terminology this operation is called reduction mammoplasty.

Examination before plastic surgery

Your plastic surgeon will look at your chest and perhaps take a few photos.

He will consider such factors as the size and shape of your breasts, skin and nipple placement and the nipple circle (pigmented skin around the nipple).

You must come to a consultation and be ready to discuss your medical history. This includes information about any diseases, allergy to any medicines, previous treatments, surgeries, including breast biopsy, and the medications that you are currently taking.

A Procedure That Removes Skin, Fat, And Glandular Tissue From The Breasts

You will be asked about the diseases of breast cancer in your family, and on the results of a mammogram. In this case, it is very important to provide complete information.

If you are planning to lose weight (especially significantly), make sure that you tell your doctor. He can recommend you to stabilize weight before surgery.

Individual characteristics of the organism and your personal preferences will help to determine the technique and method that will be used in this procedure. The most common method for reducing breast requires two incisions. One incision is made around the areola. Another – vertically – from the areola down to the skin fold under the breast.

After the surgeon removes excess tissue, fat and skin, nipples and areola will be raised in the upper position. The nipple (which, as a rule, stretches due to a large breast) also decreases in size. The skin, which was previously located above the nipples, falls down, which allows you to change the shape of the breast. To improve the contour in the area at hand, liposuction can be used.

A common technique for reducing breast removes excess mammary tissue, fat and skin through the incisions. The nipple and areola remain attached to the underlying tissue, but are raised above. The size of the areola, as a rule, decreases.

Usually, the nipples and areola remain attached to the main tissue, which allows to preserve the sensitivity of this zone. Also, with the help of this method, the ability to breast-feed (but not guaranteed) remains.

How is surgery performed?

There are many different surgical methods of breast reduction. They differ in the method of reducing excess mammary tissue and the technique of moving areola, which on the large breast are low on the chest. The surgical method is chosen so as to create a beautiful shape of the breast after it is reduced. Even after this operation, the ability to breastfeed should not be compromised.

Breast reduction is performed under general anesthesia. The essence of the operation consists in moving the areola to a normal position and removing excess skin and breast tissue. The remaining edges are sewn together in the middle of the chest, thereby reducing the size and conical shape of the chest. The duration of the operation is usually 3-4 hours. Two days later, drains are removed, the length of stay in the hospital is 2-5 days. The scar is, as a rule, in the form of an inverted T.

What happens after surgery?

Your breasts will be fixed with a sterile bandage, and then with a surgical bra. The sutures are removed three weeks after the operation. In the first days after the operation, rest is necessary, in the future you need to avoid loads, physical activity and, most importantly, lifting heavy things. Based on this recommendation, the terms of return to work are determined – they will depend on what kind of work you do.

Healing and subsequent recovery are very individual, postoperative complications, such as swelling, pain and scars healing, are different for each woman. Reduction surgery bring not only a favorable cosmetic result, since, in addition to reducing, both breast modeling performed simultaneously, physical discomfort also decreases.
