Breast Reduction Recovery
Breast Reduction Recovery/ Complications
Sep 18, 2018
In general, breast reduction surgery is a safe procedure with high patient satisfaction. If a patient is healthy and a non-smoker than the risks of complications are very low. Necrosis (dead tissue) and large wounds are rare. It is important to see a board certified plastic surgeon who can discuss the risks and benefits of surgery with special attention to your exam and medical history. Good luck!
