How to Choose the Best Breast Reduction Surgeon

If you’ve decided that you want to have breast reduction surgery, you need to take special care to choose the best surgeon you can. This is a very important decision as plastic surgery is an invasive procedure which carries many risks. I want to put it bluntly: someone will be cutting into your chest. It needs to be someone who’s competent.

22 Year Old Woman Treated With Breast Reduction With Dr Tom J. Pousti, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon

Here are the steps I recommend that you take to make sure you’ve picked the best surgeon for you:

Research the clinic. Every doctor and clinic will have a record in your local health department. Call them and ask whether there were any complaints recently and what those complaints were about.
Make sure that your surgeon is primarily a plastic surgeon. In some countries any doctor is qualified to perform plastic surgery. You don’t want an Urologist to work on your breasts, right?
Check the doctor’s diplomas. You will be paying a lot of money for this surgery, so make sure you have all the information and don’t be afraid to ask personal questions. It’s not just money on the line. It’s your body.
Ask to see before and after pictures of previous surgeries. Don’t just settle for the brochures because those will only have the best results. Ask to see more pictures.

Best Breast Reduction Surgeon In San Diego Is Tom J. Pousti

Talk to former patients. A lot of women will be only to happy to not just talk about their procedure but show you the results. This is the best way to ask all the questions you want answered and get a clear picture of what’s in store for you.

Ask about the risks, but do so after you’ve made some research yourself. This is a test for the doctor to see whether he or she are truthful about potential risks. If your doctor mentions only some of the risks, they may be desperate for you to be their client. You want someone reliable and responsible.

What About Money?

You may have noticed that I didn’t mention money in the list of things you need to discover about your doctor. That’s because I think that money is a secondary issue. Of course, some doctors will charge more than others and you need to be sure that you can afford the operation.

However, a good doctor costs money but it’s worth it. You will need to live with the results of this surgery for the rest of your life. It’s important to choose the best doctor.
