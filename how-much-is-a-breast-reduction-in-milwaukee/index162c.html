How Much Is A Breast Reduction In Milwaukee?

Breast reduction in Milwaukee is an operation to eliminate the excess volume of the breast. It is referred to as mammoplasty. This operation makes it possible to make the body harmonious and remove the problems that arise in women with large breasts.

General information

The operation involves a preliminary choice of the method of reducing the breast and marking. The duration of the operation depends on the method and takes an average of an hour to three hours.

In general, surgery is performed using general anesthesia. Local anesthesia can be used only in exceptional cases. The choice of anesthesia or anesthesia depends on the chosen method of operation.

There are such types of operations for breast reduction:

Chest Reduction May Be The Solution To The Discomfort Caused By Excessively Large Breasts

  • Standard breast reduction;
  • Vertical reduction mammoplasty;
  • Breast reduction with nipple transplantation;
  • Breast liposuction.

The process of the procedure

Preparation

Before the procedure, the patient must:

  • Get medical advice;
  • Undergo a medical examination;
  • have the necessary tests (blood tests, ECG).

In general, the patient is consulted by an anesthesiologist and breast physician. Immediately before the operation you need to stop smoking, use alcohol-containing drinks and medications.

The procedure of breast reduction

Standard breast reduction is performed using three incisions (anchoring form). The doctor removes excess tissue and raises the nipple area. Vertical mammoplasty involves 2 incisions (the shape of a candy), through which the excess is extracted.

After such an operation, the patient can accumulate glandular tissue under the breast. In this case, one more corrective operation is needed. Breast reduction with nipple movement requires transfer of nipples to a new glandular tissue.

Dr. Larry Sterkin Breast Reduction Operation

This method is unsafe for the patient’s health. Most often after surgery, there are significant scars, the nipple loses its color and shape. Partial or total loss of its sensitivity is also possible.

Liposuction of the breast is not suitable for all patients. If the breast is very sagging and has the wrong shape, the procedure will not help. During the operation, fat cells are extracted by any method: ultrasound, vacuum or laser method.

The most safe and suitable is considered vacuum (classical) liposuction. After the operation, small scars remain, which disappear after a few months. The choice of liposuction as a means of breast reduction should be approved by a doctor.

Recovery course

Reducing the size of the breast requires the mandatory wearing of compression underwear. It should be worn for a minimum of 3 weeks. The termination of use of such underwear should be carried out with the permission of the attending physician.

Dr. Thomas G. Korkos Mastopexy

Compression underwear perfectly helps to fix the breast and helps to form the right shape. In the first days after surgery, the patient experiences minor pain, which is removed with anesthetic medications prescribed by the doctor. It is forbidden to take warm bath, as well as visit saunas, gyms. Within a year after the operation, you can sunbathe only if the seams are covered.

Indications

Breast reduction surgery can be done by patients who have:

  • Problems with the elasticity and sagging of the breast;
  • Too large breasts;
  • Ugly form of the breast.

Also, such an operation is resorted to by those who:

  • Feels uncomfortable sensations;
  • Suffers from severe intertrigos and irritation under the breast;
  • Wants to get rid of stretch marks;
  • Is limited in movement due to the weight and size of the breast.

Post Breast Reduction Surgery Scar With Dr Kenneth Dembny

Contraindications

Do not reduce the breast to patients who have:

  • Cardiovascular, immune and chronic diseases;
  • diabetes;
  • Diseases of the endocrine system and blood;
  • Problems and weight disorders.
  • Also, the operation is contraindicated in pregnant women and those who have not yet given birth, nursing mothers.

Complications

Breast reduction surgery can lead to such complications:

  1. The appearance of bleeding or inflammation in the seams area;
  2. Development of skin necrosis;
  3. Loss of nipple sensitivity or lactation ability;
  4. Asymmetry of the breast.
  5. Severe complications can occur in patients who smoke and women aged.
