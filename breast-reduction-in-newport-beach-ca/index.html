Breast Reduction In Newport Beach CA

Large breasts can interfere with the quality of life, causing physical pain, emotional upset and can interfere with the quality of life.

Breast reduction surgery in Newport Beach reduces the size and weight of the breasts and improves the shape and positioning of the breast.

Women often choose surgery due to pain in the back, shoulders and neck, excessive sagging and because they are unable to participate in certain physical activities as a result of the size of their breasts. Our board certified plastic surgeons perform a variety of breast reduction procedures.

You can learn more about breast reduction procedures, including reduction mammaplasty and male breast reduction surgery on our website.

In addition to reduction mammaplasty and male breast reduction surgery, our surgeons perform other breast surgeries. We specialize in surgeries to correct complications of breast augmentation surgery, including hard breast implants, capsular contracture and breast capsulectomy procedures. A breast capsulectomy is performed to remove the capsules that can form around breast implants.
