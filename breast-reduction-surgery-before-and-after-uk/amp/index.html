Breast reduction or reduction mammoplasty is a surgical procedure aimed at reducing breast size by removing fat, breast tissue and skin.

Too large breasts can cause disorders of posture, restrict mobility, cause back pain, stress and local inflammation.

Breast reduction surgery removes the problems of physical and psychological plan.

Excessively developed mammary gland is a problem that worries many women. This is not only an aesthetic problem, but also a medical one.

Too large size and weight of the breast can be the cause of a large number of abnormalities in the state of health.

This operation helps to eliminate discomfort due to excessive volume of mammary glands, as well as some problems associated with it â€“ back pain and neck pain, irritation of the skin under the breast and respiratory diseases.

The goal of reduction mammoplasty is to achieve a more aesthetically proportional breast shape and subsequent functional benefit.

KEY MOMENTS OF SURGERY

  • Reduction mammoplasty is performed under general anesthesia.
  • Duration of the operation: 2,5-4 hours.
  • Stay in the hospital: 1 day.

REHABILITATION PERIOD

  • During the first month it is recommended to abandon from physical exertion, visiting the solarium and bath.
  • It is also necessary to wear special compression underwear. The result can be assessed 2-3 months after the operation.
