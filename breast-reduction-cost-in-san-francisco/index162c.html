Breast Reduction Cost In San Francisco

Breast reduction in San Francisco is an equally practical and aesthetic operation for women with large and very large breasts.

Excessive weight of the breast is not only an aesthetic problem, but also a risk factor for health. Over time, the skin hangs, and the degree of ptosis of the breast worsens.

Often there are pains in the spine, neck and shoulders, and in some cases irreversible deformities of the spine (kyphosis or scoliosis). Often, cases of skin infections under the breast (dermatitis, mycosis, eczema, etc.).

Women often complain about the restriction of physical exercises, caused by the large weight of the breast. Reduction mammoplasty is the best option for solving this problem.

After surgery, the shape and size of the breasts are in harmony with the rest of the body, and all of the above negative symptoms disappear.

Breast Pain

Breast reduction involves the removal of a large part of the breast tissue, depending on the individual characteristics.

This operation is allowed after the age of majority and the development of the mammary gland, or after breastfeeding, if you intend to breastfeed in the future. In extreme cases of a huge breast size (gigantomastia), which requires immediate operation, there is a risk that a woman will not be able to breast-feed. If you recently gave birth, this type of surgery should be postponed for at least 6 months after the end of breastfeeding. Thus, possible complications associated with breast milk secretions are excluded.

Breast reduction is recommended in the following cases:

Bust Reduction Is A Common Component Of Mommy Makeover Surgery

  • Too large breasts compared to the body;
  • Large breasts with a low-lying nipple and areola;
  • One breast is bigger than the other;
  • Pain in the back, neck and shoulders, caused by the weight of too large breasts;
  • Skin irritation under the breast;
  • Deep marks on the shoulders of bra straps;
  • Limited physical activity caused by excessive volume or weight of mammary glands;
  • Lack of confidence and dissatisfaction with one’s own breast.

Breast reduction is important to plan ahead, because there are a number of drugs that should be stopped before surgery (for example, contraceptive, anti-inflammatory, antidepressant, etc.).

The operation is contraindicated during menstruation or immediately after childbirth. Before the operation, you need to perform a series of necessary tests (indicated by your doctor). Be sure to conduct a mammogram to identify possible disorders before surgery. Therefore, any previously identified or new health problems should be reported to your physician before surgery.

The surgeon will make standard photos before the operation and measure the chest to establish individual characteristics.

The doctor examines the shape and size of the breast, the quality of the skin, the location of the areola and nipple. The doctor will examine your med-card to identify any health problems, allergies, medications taken. It is necessary that you provide full information.

The surgeon recommends normalizing the weight before the operation, especially if you intend to lose weight. After the reduction mammoplasty, you will resume normal activity in a few weeks, depending on your work.

In most cases, it is recommended to refrain from any physical activity. It is necessary to abstain from sexual relations for a week and to be cautious for at least 6 weeks.

After the operation, your breasts will decrease and improve the shape. You will be able to engage in any kind of sport and exercise without problems because of the weight of your chest.

With time, the post-operative scars will heal and turn pale. It is important to know that will remain permanent scarring, which in some women are more visible. Fortunately, the incisions are made in hard-to-reach areas, which are invisible even in the deep decollete.

Mammoplasty will change your appearance dramatically. Therefore, it takes some time to get used to the new image. The shape and size of the breast do not change, except for very strong weight loss and lactation.

Good Candidates For A Breast Reduction

Breast reduction surgery inevitably contributes to the appearance of new scars, more or less noticeable, the amount of which is proportional to the amount of excess mammary tissue. The new breast size will persist for a long period, especially if the weight is stable.
