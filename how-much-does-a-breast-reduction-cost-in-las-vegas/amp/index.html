How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Las Vegas?

Plastic surgery makes it possible to reduce a woman’s breast. Women with very large breasts are experiencing such inconveniences as oppressive heaviness, sweating, pain in the cervical and thoracic parts of the spine, violation of breathing during sleep, the need for careful care.

This creates discomfort in a woman’s life. The operation of breast reduction is carried out not only for cosmetic, but also for medical indications.

This is operation is called reduction mammoplasty.

Indications for breast reduction surgery in Las Vegas:

– Very large breasts,
– Asymmetrical mammary glands,
– Sagging breasts (ptosis), which has lost its elasticity,
– Improvement of the shape of the breast,
– Restoration of the correct position of the nipple.

Breast reduction surgery

During the breast reduction surgery in Las Vegas removes excess fat and the gland tissue, nipple and areola are moved to a new position.

Then, moving the skin flaps around the areola, the surgeon forms a new contour of the breast.

Breasts Reduction Surgery With Doctor Bryson G. Richards

The operation is performed under general anesthesia. Preoperative examination, which is carried out necessarily, are aimed at eliminating pathology from the mammary glands. The duration of the operation, as a rule, does not exceed 2 hours. Sutures are removed on the 7-10th day after the operation.

In the postoperative period, it is mandatory to wear special underwear, and also to exclude physical activity for 4 weeks. Stay in the hospital after the operation is 1 day.

A month after the procedure and then six months later, the patients come for a follow-up examination. The breast becomes beautiful, of the desired size, the posture is corrected, the pain in the back and neck disappears, the breathing is restored.

This operation reduces the size of the breast and removes patients from physical and mental discomfort, coupled with the very large size of the mammary glands.

The essence of the operation

Reduction mammoplasty can reduce the mammary glands and improve their shape. Usually women seeking this surgery suffer from a number of physical symptoms associated with the weight of the mammary glands.

Recovery After Plastic Surgery Breast By Dr J. Timothy Katzen

Such symptoms include pain in the back, neck, and prints of bra straps on the shoulders. The shape and extent of the incisions depend on the size of the breast before surgery and on the desired size of the breast after.

Usually, an incision is made around the areola, the vertical component is then added below the areola and in some cases, a horizontal component in the groove under the breast.

Methods of breast reduction with a short scar are very common and can significantly reduce the size of the mammary glands, improve their shape, while not leaving too long scars on the skin.

If the breast is very large before the operation, in some cases a full separation of the nipple with the areola is required, which are then placed back on the mammary gland and acclimatized as a free graft. This method leads to a loss of sensitivity to the nipple, as well as the inability to breastfeed in the future.

Breast Reduction With Lift With Doctor W. Tracy Hankins

Duration

The surgery usually takes 2-4 hours. Duration depends on the initial size of the mammary glands and on the chosen technique.

Anesthesia

Usually it is anesthesia in combination with infiltration of the area of operation with a solution of local anesthetic. Small reductions can be performed under an intravenous sedation.

Hospitalization

Depending on the initial size of the breast, the type of anesthesia and the duration of the operation, the patient can go home on the day of the operation, or stay overnight in the post-operative department of the clinic.

Possible side effects is the prolonged swelling and prolonged healing of wounds.

Possible risks

Formation of gross scars, loss of nipple sensitivity, loss of ability to breastfeed, loss of nipple and / or breast tissue, unaesthetic breast shape.

Recovery period

The recovery period usually takes 1-2 weeks. Most patients return to normal life at these times. Force loads should be limited in the first 3-6 months after surgery.

Results

Most patients, who underwent reduction mammoplasty are satisfied with the size and shape of their breasts. Symptoms associated with a large weight of the chest, such as back pain and discomfort when wearing underwear, disappear after the operation.

Women have more opportunities for sports, they like their new shape, and often they update the wardrobe, buying clothers that could not afford to wear before surgery.
