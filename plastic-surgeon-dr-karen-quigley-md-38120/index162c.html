Is my nipple dying? And is my breast shape normal?
Is my nipple dying?
Feb 4, 2019
Your right nipple appears to have what we call "epidermolysis". This occurs when there has been some ischemia but it is only on the skin level. This should get better with some TLC and the shap of your breasts will round out. You also have some glue that is still there from surgery. I would start putting some vaseline or antibiotic ointment over the area at least twice a day. Hopefully you are seeing uour plastic surgeon in follow up soon.

1 year post op breast reduction and lift. Still too big and nipples are too high, what would you do?
Nipples too high after breast reduction
Sep 20, 2018
It is very hard to move the actual nipple location down and this is why it is best to err on making them too low rather than too high. When I have this situation and the patient wants to also be smaller I excise a strip of skin and breast tissue from the bottom of the breast in the scar that is at the fold. This makes the nipples look lower and stay in the bra better.

Jun 7, 2018
There is no scientific evidence that liposuction in one area stimulates weight loss in other areas. Also, with all your weight loss you really need an abdominoplasty. A large part of what ytou have is skin excess which liposuction isn't going to correct.
