Virginia Cosmetic Breast Reduction Surgery

A large, heavy, hanging breast, which often appears at a young age, can eventually lead to deformation of the spine and be a source of chronic pain, discomfort in the neck, shoulder, in the thoracic and lumbar spine.

In the folds under the breast and on the abdominal wall, maceration and other skin problems may appear, so operations aimed at reducing the mammary glands can be attributed not only to aesthetic, but also to the rejuvenating.

The main indication for reduction mammoplasty is an increase in the size and ptosis of both (more often) or one mammary gland.

Contraindications for reduction mammoplasty

– Oncological diseases
– Infectious diseases
– Disrupted blood clotting
– Severe diseases of internal organs
– Unfinished lactation (the average child is on natural feeding for 1 year)
– Age to 18 years

Breast Reduction Without Surgery By Dr. Michael Zwicklbauer

14 days before the date of the operation (provided that the patient is healthy), it is impossible to take medicines containing salicylates and to use hormonal contraceptives.

7 days before the operation, you must stop using cigarettes (nicotine entering the body, seriously worsens the flow of blood), because otherwise the seams will be very long to heal.

The operation is performed under general anesthesia and, depending on the type of surgical intervention, lasts about 2.5-4 hours

Reduction mammaplasty in Virginia, as a rule, is carried out with simultaneous breast lift, as its main goal is to form the optimal form of the mammary glands, with the minimum number of stitches and incisions.

During the operation, excess tissues of the mammary gland and subcutaneous fat are removed, then a new shape and size is formed with the movement of the nipple-areolar complex.

When applying seams using non-absorbable materials, they are removed on the 12-14 day after the operation. In the postoperative period, it is mandatory to wear special compression underwear.
