Recommendations for the Best Reconstructive Surgeons Who Do DIEP
DIEP Reconstruction Preop
Mar 25, 2020
All of our patients get a CT scan prior to surgery to identify the vessels to see if they are a candidate. If very few cases complications can arise in which the connections of the blood vessels do not work and other options for reconstruction are used.
Seek a board certified plastic surgeon to see if you are a candidate!
DIEP Flap Surgery Best for Natural Breast Reconstruction Results?
DIEP Breast Reconstruction
Mar 25, 2020
There are pros and cons for everything we do. Using your own tissue(DIEP breast reconstruction) has benefits such as:
No implant complications/revisions, Tummy tuck, Replaces breasts with similar tissue of fat, Softer and more natural result if you have to have radiation
The cons:
Longer surgery, Longer recovery(due to multiple incisions)
The caveat to this is that you have to be a good candidate for DIEP reconstruction. See a board certified plastic surgeon to help evaluate this!
Is it possible to get a double mastectomy diep reconstruction and lipo at the same time?
DIEP and Liposuction
Mar 25, 2020
With DIEP breast reconstruction there are stages. Doing liposuction at a later stage (ie nipple reconstruction, fat grafting) may be a better option because it could add increase risk of longer anesthesia time, decreased blood flow to the tummy tissue or wound healing issue. With doing the procedure at a later stage it may be part of the fat grafting.

Shower after Bodytite

Usually 24-48 hours. Each plastic surgeon may have a different time period. This usually depends on whether they closed the liposuction sites or not. If they did not, then they may wait until they are closed until you can shower.

How long is face fat transfer recovery?
Time off after fat grafting to the face
Aug 13, 2018
Usually a weekend. The swelling typically decreases after 24-48 hours. The area around the eyes may stay puffy for up to a week. Keeping your head elevated can help keep the swelling down.
I Have 2 Open Wounds and Im Putting Everyday Vaseline or Triple Ointment...what else I Can Do?
T point healing
Aug 11, 2018
This area has the least amount of blood supply and can have some healing issues. Using Vaseline or bacitracin ointment will help keep it moist and allow it to heal. Please avoid any bras or clothing that would cause rubbing in this area.
How Do You Massage Breasts After Breast Augmentation?
Massage after implants
Jul 29, 2018
This is a highly debated topic by plastic surgeons. The studies done show no difference between either option. Our practice believes in sports bras after surgery with no underwire. Sometimes topofoam compression may be used to set the bottom of the implant. The biggest thing we try to prevent is the implants moving out towards the arms while in the healing stages.
