The shape and size of the female breast are genetically laid, therefore it is impossible to influence in any way the development of the breast. Naturally beautiful shape of the breast and its equally attractive size are the pride of any woman.

However, there are situations when the breast with time turns from a symbol of female pride and beauty into a huge burden.

Genetic factors, hormonal disorders, and a sharp set of body weight lead to a significant increase in breast size, resulting in the breasts becoming a significant disadvantage of women.

So, because of hormonal failures in the body in adolescence, the breast can increase to a non-teen size. And during the menopause, a sagging increase is added to the breast enlargement.

A Reliable Method To Reduce The Size And Weight Of Your Breasts

Too much breast is not only an aesthetic problem, it can cause serious violations in the body of a woman, including problems with the spine, constant pain in the back and neck, rashes under the breast, etc.

In addition, large breasts give the woman a lot of inconvenience when walking, playing sports, which add to the inconvenience of a psychological nature (appearance on the beach in a swimsuit).

For women who have such shortcoming, breast reduction surgery in Pennsylvania is the only solution, since there are no other options to get rid of too large a breast size.

In addition, breast reduction surgery for such women is simply necessary, as it helps to prevent posture disorders, the occurrence of pain in the spine, and also to gain ease of gait and breathing.

In addition, this surgical intervention is quite simple, gives excellent results and does not require a long stay in the hospital.

Breast reduction surgery consists in removing superfluous adipose tissue, part of the mammary gland, excision of excess skin, nipple displacement, reduction of the nipple-areola complex and formation of the most optimal breast size.

Methods of breast reduction

In practice, there are several options for breast reduction, the type and technique of surgery is chosen by the physician on the basis of the patient’s desire and careful examination.

The least traumatic method of breast reduction is a method with the use of liposuction, when excess fat from the breast is pumped out with the use of special cannulas.

This method practically does not leave any scars. As a rule, this method of breast reduction is recommended for patients who need only to slightly reduce their breasts. But, if at the same time a woman has signs of ptosis, one should not expect an ideal result from this method.

The method of vertical (short) seam is the most popular way to reduce breasts among women. This seam passes down from the nipple to the lower fold of the breast. This method of breast reduction is used in cases where one breast is supposed to remove no more than 800 grams of tissue.

This operation gives a good aesthetic result, which creates a natural form of the breast, and the sensitivity of the nipple is preserved.

This method also applies to low-traumatic methods without complications.

The technique of breast reduction with the help of a T-shaped seam (anchor incision) is a classical operation. The seam passes down from the nipple and joins there with a seam under the pectoral fold.

This technique is well developed by plastic surgeons and is mainly used in patients who need to remove a sufficiently large volume of tissue.

The diagnosis of gigantomastia is rarely enough, to show excision (amputation) of the breast with the movement of nipples to the place of newly formed breasts. When performing an operation using this technique, it is likely that the nipple tissues will not survive.

The use of any technique of breast reduction leaves small scars around the areola of the nipple. The seam, which runs along the crease under the breast, is generally invisible.

The vertical seam also heals well enough, the horizontal scars are almost invisible. Axillary sutures can cause some inconvenience, and then only within the first two weeks, but for others they are invisible.

The final form of the suture acquires after six months or a year after breast reduction surgery.

Breast reduction surgery can lead to the inability to breast-feed, because during its carrying out some milk ducts can be stopped. Therefore, this kind of operation is better planned for the period after the birth of children. Often after this operation, women lose the sensitivity of the nipples.

It is important to note one more point. The operation is performed using general anesthesia, which can cause a thrombus in a woman’s veins. The likelihood of this occurring in women who smoke, women with excess weight, as well as those who take birth control pills. In this situation, such women are recommended for six months or a year before the proposed operation to quit smoking, lose weight, and refuse to take tablets. It should be noted that it is in smoking patients after breast reduction surgery, that there are unpleasant sensations near the nipple-areola complex and on the border of the suture and the lower part of the chest.

Contraindications

Breast reduction surgery also has its contraindications, which include age over 14 years, the presence of diabetes, poor cardiac activity, oncological diseases and other serious diseases, a high degree of obesity, and pregnancy planning in the near future. In any case, the possibility of carrying out the operation is discussed with the doctor, who only after careful examination of the particular patient speaks about its possibility or impossibility.

Before the operation

Immediately before the operation to reduce the breast, you need to go to an appointment with breast physician, who during the conversation will find out the reason why you decided to take such a step.

As a rule, during the examination, the doctor offers the most optimal solutions to the problem, and also assesses, whether it is possible to reduce the breast to the desired size.

The surgeon will show the approximate lines of the incisions and approximately estimate the shape and volume of the breast after the operation. To the patient was satisfied with the result, it is necessary to discuss all the issues of interest with the doctor in advance and agree on all the details of the forthcoming operation.

If Your Breasts Are Causing You Physical Pain

The patient is assigned a series of tests, after reviewing the results, which the doctor makes a decision about the possibility or impossibility of the operation. In case of a positive response, a specific date for the breast reduction surgery is appointed.

It is important to note that the date of surgery should not coincide with menstruation. Otherwise, the operation date is transferred either four days before or after the menstruation.

Ten days before surgery, it is forbidden to take medications that affect the composition and coagulability of the blood (analgin, lecithin, vitamin E). In addition, it is important to monitor your health before the operation, because colds can cause a delay in the operation.

On the eve of the forthcoming operation, rest, bathing is necessary. You should also remove the nail polish. The last meal, including drinking, should be at the latest eight hours before the operation.

For those who decided to lose weight on a diet, it is necessary before the operation, but not after, as the diet can change the shape of the breast not for the better.

After operation

Also, for several months, you may be troubled by some chest pain, which you should not worry about, it will eventually disappear. Return to normal life can be after the healing of the sutures.
