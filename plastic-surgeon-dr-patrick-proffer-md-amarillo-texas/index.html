Is 4 weeks enough time for nicotine to get out of my system before breast augmentation?
Nicotine and Breast Augmentation
Sep 19, 2015
Nicotine plays a significant role in wound healing and in many cosmetic surgeries. It can effect the small blood vessels' ability to function properly but comes into play in some surgeries more than others (facelift, breast lift, tummy tuck, etc.). Four weeks of absolute smoking cessation should be adequate for straightforward breast augmentation without lift. It sounds like you're a great candidate for quitting altogether.
