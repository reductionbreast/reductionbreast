How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Philadelphia?

Breast reduction surgery is health and beauty for life. Too large breasts can cause some women health problems, as well as emotional problems. You can also experience physical pain and discomfort, and the excess weight of the breast tissue can affect your ability to engage in active physical activity, sports.

Breast reduction surgery (also known as reduction mammoplasty) in Philadelphia removes excess breast fat, mammary glands and skin, so that you can achieve a breast size that matches the proportions of your body and reduce the discomfort caused by too much breast.

Breast reduction is suitable for you if:

– You are tired too much breast;
– The chest restricts your physical activity, the opportunity to exercise;
– You have a pain in your back, neck and shoulders due to the weight of your breast;
– Constantly formed dents from the bra straps, which support a heavy saggy breast;
– You have skin irritation under the bones of a bra;
– Your chest is hanging and stretching the skin;
– Your nipples are below the breasts, when nothing supports them;
– Areoles of your breast enlarged.

The operation is performed under anesthesia, after which it is recommended to spend 1-2 days in the hospital.

The results of breast reduction surgery

The results of breast reduction are long-term. You will lose back pain, you will be able to play sports freely, the figure will become more proportional.
