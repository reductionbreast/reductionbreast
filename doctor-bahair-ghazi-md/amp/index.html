What does the term "bottoming out" refer to in terms of a Breast Augmentation?
Breast Augmentation, Bottoming out
Dec 26, 2016
When a breast implant  "bottoms out", the position of the nipple moves compared to the center point of the implant. This commonly occurs when an implant is heavier than the inferior pole support of the soft tissues. These tissue expand...and the distance from the nipple to edge of inferior implant increases. To treat this the soft tissue needs to be excised and the implant supported or downsized.
