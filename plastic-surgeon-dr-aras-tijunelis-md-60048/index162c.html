Are my nipples dying?
Free nipple graft mastectomies
Jan 8, 2018
The free nipple graft technique is essentially a skin graft technique. The grafted nipple/areola relies on the blood supply from the skin on which it is laying. Therefore, it is not uncommon to have partial or incomplete “take” or healing of the grafted tissue. The scabbed area will fall off, but the skin behind will heal in leaving a scar that sometimes resembles an areola. There are options. Be patient. Nothing to do about it at this point.
