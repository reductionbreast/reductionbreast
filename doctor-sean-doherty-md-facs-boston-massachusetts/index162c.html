Should I have liposuction while breast feeding?
Apr 8, 2020
Thanks for your question! There are 2 reasons to delay your procedure. Firstly, you want to avoid anesthesia and pain medicine and antibiotics from an elective procedure in your breast milk. Secondly, your body is still likely going through changes after your pregnancy and you want to be at a stable and sustainable weight before having any body contouring procedure. I highly recommend you speak with a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon for you consultation.
