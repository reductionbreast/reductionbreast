It is not uncommon to have some breakdowns to the incisions of the breast after large surgeries, especially where the incisions all meet. Most of the time it is just a problem with an internal suture. Patience, and keeping the wounds clean will allow these superficial openings to heal.Stay in touch with your surgeon, and let him check the site every so often, especially if there was an implant placed. These should heal well with time.Best of Luck
