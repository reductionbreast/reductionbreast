Breast Reduction Surgery In Ottawa Canada

In the first hours after breast reduction surgery, you will feel pain in the chest area. The doctor will prescribe you an effective anesthetic that will relieve severe pain.

During the recovery period, you must comply with all the recommendations and requirements of the doctor: this will help the tissues heal faster and better. Wear only comfortable clothes and shoes, without fasteners, since your movements will be limited.

Be sure to wear a bandage, this can be a gauze dressing, elastic bandage or compression underwear. This bandage should be worn 24 hours a day for 4 weeks.

The day after the operation, you can take a shower, but do not rub your breast with washcloths, do not press.

A Bust Reduction Procedure May Help To Reduce Neck And Back Pain

The first week is recommended to sleep on the back, as the chest can ache, be swollen and sensitive to any touches. Put a pillow under your back, so it will be more convenient for you and besides it will help prevent swelling of the chest.

The first few weeks should limit the movement of the hands, physical activity, especially on the thoracic area. Sports activities can be resumed no earlier than 2-3 months after the operation. Sunbathing is possible only in a swimsuit, additionally use high-quality sunscreen.

In a week or two after breast reduction in Ottawa, when the pain disappears, you will feel that the burden on the spine has decreased, that everything that caused you all the time before the operation, now for you does not exist.

Achieve A More Proportionate Bust With Breast Reduction Surgery

In 2-3 months after the operation, the breast will take the final shape, it will look natural and beautiful.

If you are confused by the presence of scars, and they seem noticeable to you, one year after the operation you can attend a course of laser therapy and eliminate scars.

The risk of complications after breast reduction is not large. Before performing breast reduction every woman should be aware that nipple sensitivity may decrease or disappear altogether.

Breastfeeding can be difficult or impossible (depending on the technique of surgery chosen), so if you plan to give birth and the process of breastfeeding is extremely important for you, it is better to perform the operation after childbirth.

Among the complications that can occur immediately after surgery are called inflammation, bleeding, bruising. Bleedings are small, but sometimes blood transfusion may be required, hematomas are treated without surgery. Very rare are infections that are treated with antibiotics. The healing of different parts of the skin can be uneven.

Breast reduction in Ottawa does not create conditions that provoke the development of breast cancer. Sometimes in the chest there are seals, this partial necrosis of fat cells. This complication can be eliminated through surgical intervention.

After surgery, the mammary glands may be asymmetric, but after 3-6 months the shape of the breast will become beautiful and symmetrical.

In rare cases, necrosis of the nipple or skin flaps may occur. This complication occurs if the operation was performed unprofessionally.

The effect of breast reduction surgery is quite long, but you need to monitor the weight. If you gain excess weight, your breast automatically becomes fuller. Over time and in the course of the natural aging process, the previously operated breast will sag and lose shape.

Go in for sports, try to maintain your weight (not to lose weight and not to get fat) and you will save the result for a long time. Breast reduction is an operation that will not only help you eliminate physical discomfort, but also moral.
