Whats the best treatment for my sagging breasts and excess skin?
Sagging breast help
Jan 8, 2019
A breast lift is a great option to give you younger (more age appropriate) looking breasts. Sometimes this is combined with an implant but given the laxity of your skin I’d say better to do just the lift alone and not put any excessive weight on your skin. I would keep in mind that this will almost surely have to be tightened in the future; how far in the future is hard to say (maybe 5 years maybe 25 years) depending on how well your skin holds up. Good luck.

Exercise
May 7, 2018
Generally I tell my patients 4-6 weeks (all incisions are fully healed) before they can begin exercising.
