Does my upper lip look disproportiante to my bottom lip? 1 day post lip fillers.
Swelling after Lip Injection
Apr 12, 2018
The first few days is too early to tell if the lips are asymmetric after injection with filler. Your lip swelling will resolve in a few days to one week. If the asymmetry is still present, this can easily be corrected by your injector. Repeated application of ice over the next 24 hours will help reduced the swelling as will sleeping on a few pillows to help keep your head more upright. Good Luck!

Is there a Risk of Implant Explosion of Breasts During Commercial Flying?
Safe to fly with breast implant
Jan 27, 2012
It is quite safe to fly with saline or silicone implants. There is no risk of rupture with either flying or even deep sea diving. I have had one patient whose implants ruptured with a high velocity direct impact with automobile airbags, but the ususal cause of rupture of breast implants is usual wear and tear of the implant. Enjoy your travels.
