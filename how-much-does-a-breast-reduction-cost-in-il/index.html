How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Illinois?

Breast reduction surgery in Illinois is recommended for women, who have a heavy and large breasts. In this regard, many of them experience physical pain and embarrassment.

The operation can be carried out by individuals, beginning with the senior adolescence and up to 70 years old.

Typically, plastic surgeons recommend breast reduction in case, if the client does not plan pregnancy in the future.

Breast reduction in Chicago is performed under general anesthesia, and the operation takes 2-3 hours.

A plastic surgeon removes excess fat, glandular tissue and skin, which make the breast large and sagging.

To achieve the desired shape, the surgeon makes incisions in the form of an anchor, creating vertical lines down from the areola (the colored area around the nipple) and horizontal incisions under the breast.

Dr Niki Christopoulos Boob Reduction And Lift

Areola, its blood vessels and nerve endings in most cases undergo reposition. The edges of the vertical incisions are sewn together to form a smaller breast.

Sometimes, with a significant reduction in breast size, it may be necessary to completely separate the areola and move it to another location. If necessary, the size of the areola can be reduced.

A plastic surgeon decides on the use of this or that technique. Scars will not be visible in clothes and most swimwear.

Immediately after the operation, the scars will have a red tint, and they will also be solid and convex.

Scars will heal, take a normal color and become softer for 3-12 months. Sometimes the scars fade and become almost invisible, but sometimes they become broad and convex. The final appearance of the scars can not be predicted before surgery.

After the operation, you will be invited every 2-3 days to the clinic for examination. The minimum period of stay in the country is 9-10 days after the operation (2-3 at the clinic + 7 in the spa center or hotel), the recommended period is 10 days from the date of the operation.

If necessary, the seams are removed by your plastic surgeon 10 days after the surgery – on the day of your departure.
