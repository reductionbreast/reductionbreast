In wich position can you lay down after BBL and TT?
BBL and TT
Jan 8, 2018
After BBL and TT, you can expect a challenging week or two after surgery involving sitting, moving and lying down. I advise my patient to lay on their sides until their abdomen loosens enough for them to lie face down. A recliner with well placed cushions can also be used to maintain a flexed position without putting pressure on your precious fat grafts.
