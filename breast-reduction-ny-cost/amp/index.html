Breast Reduction New York Cost

Breast reduction (reduction mammoplasty) in New York can be performed both for aesthetic and for medical reasons.

Too large breasts, in addition to physical discomfort, can cause such unpleasant phenomena as:

– Pain in the thoracic and cervical spine,
– Deformation of the spine,
– Chronic mastopathy.

Discomfort due to excessive volume of mammary glands is the main indication to the operation. Often the main reason that prompts the patient for such an operation is the problem of clothing selection. Other indications for this intervention may be significant differences in the volume and shape of the mammary glands, as well as their pronounced ptosis.

19 Year Old Woman Treated With Breast Reduction Before By Doctor Anthony Deboni, MD, Syracuse Plastic Surgeon

Breast reduction surgery is one of the most complicated in plastic surgery. The purpose of this procedure is not only to reduce the size of the breast, but also to give it a new, more aesthetic form.

Due to the fact, that the excess volume of the mammary glands is almost always combined with their ptosis, the technique of breast lift and breast reduction are very similar, with the difference that druring breast reduction: The volume of the mammary gland is not only redistributed but also decreases. Part of the breast tissue is removed, mainly from its lower sector. Many patients do this more on the one hand, which is necessary with significant differences in volume and shape between the right and left breasts.

Reduction of one breast

There are cases when a reduction and breast lift only needs on one side.

The postoperative period is not characterized by severe pain, on the contrary, the patients quickly return to their habitual lifestyle, if all the recommendations are followed and there are no signs of inflammation from the wound. The patient is in the hospital for 1-2 days, and return to work after 1-2 weeks.

Complications of surgery

The complications associated with reducing the mammary glands are the same as after the breast lift surgery. However, due to removing a portion of breast tissue probability of excess blood in the wound clusters, as well as increasing the frequency sensitivity disorders nipples and areolas.

In the most difficult cases (gigantomastia), a blood flow disorder may occur, leading to the death of part or even all of the transferred tissues due to clamping of the vessels during the return of the areola with the nipple to the correct position and requires additional treatment.

Breaking breastfeeding can be the result of breast reduction due to the movement of the nipple-areola complex to a considerable distance. In this case, the connection of the teat with the milk ducts is broken and breastfeeding becomes impossible.

The results of breast reduction in New York can reduce the severity of the complaints of patients or completely eliminate its. And it is worth remembering, that the more the breast and the need to reduce it, the more difficult the operation and the higher the risk of complications.

Reduction mammaplasty is carried out under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation is on average 3 to 5 hours. During the operation, the plastic surgeon dissects excess superficial tissues and reduces the volume of breast tissue.

Also, the nipple-areolar complex is moved with the preservation of blood vessels. This stage of the operation requires the surgeon’s high professionalism and excellent knowledge of anatomy, therefore one should trust the performance of reduction mammoplasty exclusively to a qualified surgeon.
