Reduction Mammoplasty In Manhattan

Reduction and lift of the breasts (reduction mammoplasty and mastopexy)

Undoubtedly, physical exercises contribute to the development of muscles located under the mammary gland. However, there is no muscle in the mammary gland itself. Therefore we can not significantly improve the shape of a large and sagging breasts only through exercise.

The mammary gland is located under the skin, permeated with a multitude of ducts that connect it to the nipple. If the breast is not hypertrophied, but its shape is broken due to loss of skin elasticity, a special operation is performed without excision of the gland and fat tissue. Only excess skin is removed. Such an operation is called mastopexy.

Breast lift or mastopexy

28 Year Old Woman Who Underwent Breast Reduction With Doctor Elan B. Singer, MD, Manhattan Plastic Surgeon

Breastfeeding, concomitant hormonal changes in the breast, loss of skin elasticity and elasticity, as well as the impact of the breast’s own weight lead to its sagging. Mastopexy or breast lift is reduced to moving the breast to the top, as well as removing excess skin of the breast.

Mastopexy is complemented by the implantation of the mammary glands in cases where the sagging of the skin is accompanied by an involuntary change in the glandular tissue. There are several ways of mastopexy.

Which way to choose the surgeon will decide based on the initial condition of the mammary glands, as well as on the condition of the skin of the breast and the aesthetic requirements of the patient.

Often in women with very large breasts, there are various problems caused by excessive weight. This is skin irritation, neck and back pain, skeletal deformation, respiratory failure. In such cases, use reduction mammoplasty in Manhattan.

In carrying out this operation, it is possible to remove not only the skin, excision of the gland tissue, the removal of excess fat. The breast becomes lightweight, smaller and denser. Depending on the type of defect to be removed, sometimes combined methods are used.

The degree of need for an operation should be carefully discussed with the surgeon. Initially planning the course of the operation, the doctor tries to place the proposed incisions in the lower part of the breast.

But it is necessary to understand that both operations, probably, will leave scars. Ugly breasts or surgical scars should be taken on their own. However, it is not necessary to choose when it comes to gigantomastia – cases when the mammary glands are large enough that they can lead to a whole bouquet of serious chronic diseases. In this case, the operation is done not for aesthetic, but for medical reasons.

It is necessary to clarify all the stages of the operation and the course of the postoperative period. It is necessary to understand the professional recommendations that correspond to individual characteristics.

Operation

Mammoplasty is performed under general anesthesia and lasts for more than two hours. The doctor removes excess fat, then conducts excision of the gland tissue, smoothes the skin and puts it back into place, thus forming a new contour of the breast.

The question of reducing and correcting the shape of the nipple-areola complex requires additional time to resolve. Sometimes liposuction is used to remove excess fat from the armpit.

In most cases, the nipples remain connected with nerves and vessels, so their vitality and sensitivity are not violated. For unobtrusive scarring, a special suture material is used.

Postoperative period

In the first days after the operation, moderate pain is possible. The pain finally disappears within two weeks. Medical bandages are allowed to be removed only after two or three days.

For a few weeks, you should wear a compression bra. In the next six weeks, the body comes back to normal. Finally, the sutures are removed after one or two weeks.

Efficiency

The more breasts, the longer the process of resorption of scars and the period of recovery. Most often this does not depend on the skill of the surgeon, but on the individual characteristics of the organism. The surgeon tries to hide most of the scar in the crease under the breast or in the areola area.

Within a year, the scars turn into strips of flesh color. Aesthetic scars are more common in non-smoking women who underwent surgery after 30 years of age.

Reduction of mammary glands in men

Most often, the increase in breast glands in men, namely gynecomastia, is formed against the background of taking hormonal drugs, such as, antiandrogens, anabolic steroids or female sex hormones. With obesity, pseudo-gynecomastia develops.

This is due to the fact that men have a tendency to accumulation of large amounts of subcutaneous fat in the area of mammary glands. With the breast glands with normal anatomical dimensions, a similar female bust is formed. The solution to this problem is mastectomy.
