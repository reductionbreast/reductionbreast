Who is the best candidate for reduction mammoplasty?

Reduction mamoplasty in Seattle is necessary for girls and women who are troubled by severe back pain and whose scoliosis has long hinted at the appearance of osteochondrosis.

Breast Implant Reduction By Dr Mary Lee Peters

In addition, women who are unhappy with the shape of their breasts or who have ptosis usually turn to us for reduction mammoplasty. For many women, the big breasts interfere with sports, and when choosing between keeping the size of this part of the body and an active lifestyle, they choose the latter.

How is reduction mammoplasty performed?

The operation is quite simple, only in some cases the surgeon will have to “sweat”. After consultation and tests, the date of the operation will be assigned. Reduction mammoplasty requires anesthesia.

The surgeon will make 2 small vertical incisions under the nipples and cut out some of the breast tissue. The nipples after such an operation will significantly shift to make the breast shape harmonious.

The techniques of performing a breast reduction surgery are actually different, so you can discuss with the surgeon the course of the operation on an initial free consultation.

Postoperative period

The time of the patient’s stay in the clinic is no more than a day. In order that the traces of the operation heal faster and the “new breast” acquired a beautiful form, the patient is recommended a special supporting underwear.

A dressing is necessary 2-3 times a week for 2 weeks, and then the surgeon removes the stitches. The remaining scars will finally heal and cease to be visible after 6-8 months.
