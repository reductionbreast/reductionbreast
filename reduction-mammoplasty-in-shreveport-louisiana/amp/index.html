What are the risks of reduction mammoplasty?

Like all surgical interventions, reduction mammaplasty is not without risk and some complications. Each case is individual. Usually it is infectious complications in the form of infection (the problem is solved by the course of antibiotics), hematomas as a result of bleeding, as well as an allergic reaction to anesthesia and the formation of keloid scars.

Reduction mammoplasty in Shreveport is accompanied by incisions, in which inevitably the intersection of small nerve branches running to the skin of the breast, as well as to the nipple and areola. As a result, the sensitivity of the nipples decreases or disappears completely. This phenomenon can be temporary or in some cases permanent.
