How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In Colorado?

It is worth noting that while most female representatives dream of a large, attractive breast, there are a sufficient number of women for whom a large breasts are a big problem.

And this is due not only to aesthetic and psychological discomfort. The enlarged mammary glands are often very painful. In addition, premenstrual conditions significantly increase symptoms.

This is the pain in the shoulder girdle, back and neck, deep furrows from the strapless bras. The skin under the glands is in extremely unfavorable conditions. From friction, the dermal surfaces begin to become wet and inflammatory reactions appear.

Too large breast size is associated with the so-called hypertrophy of the mammary glands.

Glandular hypertrophy is the result of endocrine disorders.

Before And After With Dr. Paul M. Steinwald, MD, Denver Plastic Surgeon

It can develop both in adolescence and during the postpartum period. There is a certain difficulty in objective confirmation of breast hypertrophy due to a different understanding of its “normality.”

Therefore, for practical purposes, classification is quite suitable, where the “chest” of 275 cubic centimeters is “desirable”. Consequently, an increase in this volume by 50% is already considered a degree of hypertrophy.

For a general understanding of the presence or absence of hypertrophy, you can focus on the following indicators:

  • Normal breast – 250-300 cm3
  • A small hypertrophy – 400-600 cm3
  • Relatively pronounced – 600-800 cm3
  • Expressed or significant hypertrophy – 800-1000 cm3
  • Gigantomastia – 1500 cm3 and more

Thus, when a critical degree of hypertrophy is detected, doctors recommend a breast reduction surgery, or reduction mammoplasty in Colorado, for patients. The purpose of the operation is to form a new contour of the breast of a smaller volume.

The result of a plastic surgery to reduce the breast will not take long. You will get rid of many problems, since such an operation helps not only to make your breasts sexy, but also relieves the painful sensations associated with large breasts.

You no longer have to struggle with diaper rash under the breast, and you can also afford a more sexual wardrobe. In some countries, particularly in the US, such transactions are even included in the insurance policy.

To start breast reduction surgery should only after a course of treatment aimed at stopping the growth of the gland.

With fatty hypertrophy, which most often develops after childbirth, before the operation it is enough to undergo a general examination and get a consultation of the breast physician.

Doctor Winfield Hartley Breast Reduction Recovery Images

During the operation, excess fat is removed, the gland tissue, nipple and areola are moved to a new location corresponding to the new volume of the breast. The sutures are usually located around the areola, vertically down to the crease under the breast and in the crease itself.

Postoperative scar at the beginning is dense, red and protrudes above the surface of the skin. However, it remains invisible, as it is in the zone, usually covered with underwear and is visible only during the first 6 months after the operation.

Over time, it turns pale and leveled. For the best healing, doctors are strongly recommended to wear special compression underwear for a month, until bruises and swelling disappear. Sutures are removed for 12-16 days. Stay in the hospital takes 1-3 days.
