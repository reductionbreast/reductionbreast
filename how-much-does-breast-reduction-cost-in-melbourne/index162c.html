How Much Does Breast Reduction Cost In Melbourne?

Breast reduction in Melbourne is a surgical operation, indicated for women with an excessively large mammary gland. Macromastia is a pathological condition caused by a malfunction in the hormonal system. There is a primary pathology (disproportionate increase of the mammary glands during puberty), and postpartum syndrome, the essence of which is that the glandular tissue is replaced by an extensive fat layer after the abolition of breastfeeding.

Breast reduction in Melbourne is often required for women with obesity of the second and third degree, when the breast increases in volume due to excess weight. The increased weight of the breast, which does not correspond to body proportions, adversely affects the condition of the supporting-motor system, since the load on the spine exceeds physiologically acceptable standards.

Breast Reduction For Aesthetic Reasons

The consequence of macromastia are such unpleasant symptoms as increased fatigue, pain in the cervical spine area, insomnia.

In old age, to the listed symptoms joins shortness of breath, pain in the chest area, impaired posture.

The essence of reduction mammoplasty

Reduction mammoplasty is an actual direction in plastic surgery.

The goal of the operation is not only to reduce the size of the mammary glands, but also to create their beautiful form. During the plastic surgery, the doctor has to simulate a new nipple-areola complex, correct asymmetry, and perform a breast lift.

Reduction operations are performed only by experienced surgeons of the highest qualification. During excision of glandular (or adipose tissue), it is necessary to preserve the normal blood supply of the gland and the nipple-areolar complex. Before the doctor there are several priority tasks:

Breast Removal Operation By Dr Carmen Munteanu

  • Choose the option of forming a blood supply channel in the area of the nipple-areolar complex;
  • Choose a technique for modeling parenchyma of the mammary glands after excision of tissues;
  • Determine the scheme for removing excess skin and creating the shape of the seam.

The basic method of breast reduction involves changing the shape of the chest hill with the use of glandular and dermal tissue, modeling the feeding leg (upper, lower or central).

Instead of a circular seam over the hemisphere of the breast, a small periareolar suture around the nipple-areolar complex is applied on the border between the pigmented and unpainted skin.

Choice of operation technique

Before choosing a surgical technique for breast reduction surgery, the doctor assesses the condition of the chest muscles, ligamentous apparatus, fatty and glandular tissue, as well as the dermal membrane (epidermis, dermis, hypodermis).

Doctor Chris Moss Breast Lift Reduction Scar

The surgical procedure contains two main technical aspects:

  • Method of incision of skin defects;
  • Technique of excision of tissues.

During the operation, the mammary gland is lifted with a change in the position of the nipple-areola complex. Most cases of macromastia are combined with ptosis (hanging of the breast), because the muscles can not hold excessive weight of glandular fatty tissue. The final result of the performed surgery is fixed 6-8 months after the operation.

Contraindications

Contraindications to breast reduction surgery are:

  • Acute and chronic infections;
  • Systemic diseases;
  • Lactation period;
  • Renal and heart failure.
  • The doctor accepts the final conclusion about the possibility of performing breast reduction surgery after a comprehensive diagnosis.

Recovery period

The patient is in the clinic for 2-3 days, if there are no complications after the operation. The doctor develops individual recovery programs, taking into account the age and health condition.
