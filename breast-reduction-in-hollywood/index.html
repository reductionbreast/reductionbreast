The course of breast reduction surgery in Hollywood

Prior to anesthesia, the surgeon makes a marking on the chest with a special marker. Are planned those sites that need to be removed. Then a general anesthesia is given: first an anesthetic of short action is injected into the vein, and after the person falls asleep, an intubation tube is inserted into the trachea. The operation is performed with controlled breathing.

During the dive into anesthesia, the surgeon checks the markings and charts the lines of the future incision with iodine. Then, along all lines of marking, the skin is cut to the dermis, the lower layer of skin on the border with muscles and subcutaneous fat. First of all, the areola is cut out – a circle around the nipple. The size of the areola is chosen according to the size of the breast that will be after the operation.

Then begins a very painstaking work – need to distinguish a vascular-neural bundle that supplies the areola nipple with food. Each vessel that is suitable for the areola must be preserved and separated from the muscles and fat, so that in the future the areola retains sensitivity. On average, breast reduction surgery takes about 3 hours. It takes at least an hour to separate the vascular pedicle.

The next step is that large flaps of skin, subcutaneous fat and glandular tissue are removed. The flaps are removed in such a way as to preserve the full blood supply of the mammary gland.

Removable flaps have a triangular shape, with the wide part located at the base of the gland.

One flap is removed on one breast. As the flap is separated, the vessels are pinched and cauterized. The big difficulty lies in the fact that the vessels of each person have an individual arrangement. Therefore, it is necessary to cut out the flap as dictated by the structure of the vascular network.

At the end of the operation, the remaining tissues are sewn together, the areola is sewn onto a new place. Used absorbable thread, they performed an intradermal suture, which goes continuously. After complete healing of such a seam, the joints of the tissues are visible only after careful examination. If necessary, drainage for drainage is installed. The pressing bandage is applied, which has high elasticity.

On the eve of discharge, compression underwear is selected for permanent wearing. After the end of the postoperative period, no special care is required for the new breast.
