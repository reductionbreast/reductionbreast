Is Cryosurgery a Better Option Than Dermabrasion to Treat Hypertropic Scars in Darker Skins?
Cryoshape is a new "effective " treatment for Keloids
Oct 11, 2012
Dermabrasion in dark skin would have a higher incidence of pigment changes and possible worsening of scars. Internal cryotherapy (Cryoshape) has a better result than external cryotherapy( liquid nitrogen ) with much less risk of pigment changes
Keloid on the Chest
Cryoshape is a new "effective " treatment for Keloids
Oct 11, 2012
Cryoshape is a new method for Keloid treatment where a probe is inserted and liquid nitrogen is used to freeze the keloid from the inside out. It is done with local anesthesia and has a very high resolution rate with much lower rate of pigment changes.
Will There Be Bruising or Other Visible Side Effects from a Cellulaze Treatment?
The Potential Risks of Cellulaze are minimal.
Sep 14, 2012
The side and forward firing Cellulaze fiber is inserted just below the skin. It is done under local anesthesia with oral sedation. Because of the location of very small vessels in this area, some bruising and swelling can occur. Most people having Cellulaze are back to normal activity in 24 hours. The results continue to improve over 6 months to 1 year.
Is Endermologie Helpful After Cellulaze to Speed Process?
Post-Op SmoothShapes Or Exilis Speeds effects of Cellulaze
Sep 14, 2012
The Cellulaze Laser fiber is inserted just below the skin so bruising and swelling can occur. The swelling and bruising are lessened AND the results from Cellulaze are enhanced by post-op SmoothShapes or Exilis Radiofrequency treatments.
