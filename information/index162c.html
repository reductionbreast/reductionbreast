In plastic surgery, there are situations when the size of the breast needs to be reduced.

This plastic surgery is called reduction mammoplasty.

A Cosmetic Surgeon Can Make The Breasts Smaller, Lighter, And Firmer

Excessively large breasts can cause both psychological discomfort and the occurrence of a variety of health problems, among which is the overload of the spine in the cervicothoracic area, which is manifested by prolonged pain syndrome, impaired posture.

Modern plastic surgery solves not only aesthetic problems, but also functional ones. Plastic surgery offers a safe breast reduction while maintaining the size and shape that a woman desires after discussing all the nuances of plastic correction with an experienced surgeon specializing exclusively in breast plastic surgery.

Medical indications for surgery

Pre Op And Post Op

  • Mastoptosis (sagging) of the mammary gland of various levels of severity.
  • Asymmetrical shape of the breast.
  • Ptosis (omission of the breast, while preserving its size).
  • Post-lactation involution of mammary glands (correction of breast size after completion of baby feeding).
  • Congenital malformations of the breast.

Techniques of reduction mammoplasty

“Periareolar” mammoplasty. Applicable in the case of a small breast ptosis (I-II The degree), the breast size can not be transformed. In this case, the scar has a ring shape and is located exclusively around the areola.

Getting A Breast Reduction

This technique is characterized by minimal traumatism, short operation time and rapid healing of postoperative sutures. This kind of mammoplasty is aimed exclusively at improving the existing form of the breast, without striving for an ideal. This operation is combined with one-stage implantation to create the ideal shape of the breast.

Breast Reduction Anchor method. It is the most commonly used technique in plastic surgery. It is used in the case of a pronounced chest ptosis (III-IV degree), as well as to reduce its size. To support a new form in the correct position, the doctors applied internal (disappear in the next 3 months) and external (intradermal) sutures, using high quality suture material.

Possible complications

Most Women Who Get Breast Reduction Are Very Satisfied With The Results

Potential risks of breast reduction surgery include scarring, an increase or decrease in the sensitivity of nipples and breasts, the development of swelling and bruising, inability to lactation, allergy to suture material, and also on the drugs used. However, the ten-year experience of doctors, the use of high-quality technologies and preparations, a serious approach to safety, minimize the aforementioned risks.

Self-preparation for surgery

  • 14 days before surgery, the patient should stop taking hormone and aspirin-containing medications. If this is not possible, you should always consult with the surgeon for further action. Also during this period, the patient should stop smoking or reduce the number of cigarettes to 2 per day.
  • 7 days before surgery, you should stop using spicy and fatty foods, as well as taking alcohol.
  • 8 hours before surgery, you must stop eating food to ensure safe anesthesia.
  • In the morning, on the day of breast reduction surgery, collect things: comfortable clothes that do not squeeze the chest and which need not be removed through the head, personal hygiene products, replaceable underwear, all necessary documents and test results.
