Can Breast Reduction Also Involve Liposuction of the Chest Area or is It Requested Separately?

Jan 10, 2012
Breast reduction can involve liposuction of the chest area. Specifically, it can involve the lateral chest area. However, not every patient is a candidate. If excess fat tissue of the lateral chest or axilla is a concern this should be brought to the attention of your surgeon. Breast reduction can be achieved through excision of excess skin and fat tissue, with liposuction or a combination of the two.

If I Do a Crash Diet Before Lipo, Will Dr Be Able to Remove More Fat Cells?
Liposuction and Crash Diets
Oct 19, 2011
After your teenage years, you have a set # of fat cells in your body, but they are not evenly distributed. This gives your body its shape.When you gain weight, each fat cell gets bigger. When you lose weight, they get smaller.

Dieting will not affect the number of cells removed but being in a stable weight that does not fluctuate more than 10 lbs up or down helps to make your results more long lasting.
