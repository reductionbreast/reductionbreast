Reduction mammoplasty (i.e. breast reduction surgery) in Grand Rapids is not only an aesthetic procedure.

The desire to reduce the bust arises primarily because of the inconvenience that brings to the life too large breasts.

This is a constant weight, pain in the back and shoulders (try to wear down every day such a weight!), sweating and a lot of other uncomfortable moments, greatly complicating life and worsening well-being.

Bust Reduction Is One Of The Most Common Procedures Performed On The Breast

What is the cause of hypertrophy, that is, the excessive development of the mammary glands? There are two main types: fatty and endocrine hypertrophy.

Fatty hypertrophy of the breast develops usually after childbirth and feeding, or even simply with age. The breast increases and loses its shape due to accumulation of fat in the breast. This is the case when breast reduction is possible by “folk” methods – breast reduction without surgical intervention.

In the fight against excess fat tissue can trigger such funds for breast reduction as a diet to reduce the size of the breast or reducing body fat in the breast area with the help of exercises. If the case is not neglected, then these measures of non-surgical breast reduction are enough, but in most cases the problem is not in the size of the breast, but in the loss of its tone and shape. Breasts can be beautiful only with the help of a plastic surgery to reduce the breast and by lifting it.

Doctor David R. Alfonso Bilateral Reduction Mammoplasty Procedure

Endocrine hypertrophy develops in youth and has a genetic origin. In this case, breast reduction without surgery is impossible – because excess volume is created by not fatty, and glandular tissue. In this case, exercise will not help to reduce breast and reduce breast by diet. And before performing a breast reduction operation, it is necessary to conduct a course of treatment in an endocrinologist, aimed at stopping the growth of the breast.

How to reduce breast size without surgery?

How to reduce breast size without surgery and is it even possible? Let’s repeat: in case “excess” were formed due to adipose tissue – it is quite possible to reduce the breast size without surgery.

In many women, breast size is highly dependent on the diet. And we are not talking about a special diet to reduce the volume of the breast, but about a diet for losing weight in general.

Breast is just the area in which excess fat goes away quickly enough – much easier than from the hips or waist. But it is important to remember that any diet for breast reduction will lead to its reduction, but not to give it the same beautiful form. So often without breast lift can not do.

The traditional way to deal with excess fat is sports. How can reduce breast with sports exercises?

Most instructors considered aerobics appropriate sports exercises for breast reduction, push-ups and exercises with dumbbells, which strengthen the pectoral muscles and the muscles of the shoulder girdle.

And, of course, no exercises for breast reduction will not be effective without a beautiful posture – after all, it is often the stoop creates a feeling of sagging, disproportionate large breasts.

Well, is it possible to reduce the breast, without putting any effort at all? It is possible – with the help of clothes. First, some companies produce special clothes, which creates the effect of the reduction and breast lift. However, oncologists are alarmed unduly constricted breasts is dangerous. Therefore, we proceed even simpler.

In any manual on modeling of clothes it is in detail written, how to reduce the breast size visually, by means of tailor’s tricks. Tops with closed shoulders, dark tones of blouses and a thin vertical pattern on jackets, a scarf wrapped around the shoulders, a skirt flared upwards, or pants are all elements that visually reduce the breast and allow the body to be brought to harmony and balance.

Of course, all the above tips on how to reduce the breasts without the intervention of surgeons can be useful only for those, who have not hypertrophied breast, but just a little more than we would like. If it is a question of excessively large mammary glands, attempts to reduce breast size without surgical intervention will not succeed.

And the answer to the question of how to reduce the breast, there is only one – surgically.

Insurance Typically Covers This Procedure

Fortunately, modern reduction mamoplasty allows to create the chest of your dreams in one operation, lasting 2-3 hours. During surgery, excess fat, glandular tissue and skin are removed. A very frequent addition to breast reduction surgery is the reduction of nipples and a decrease in the size of the breast areola.

This is especially important, if the cause of breast hypertrophy is breastfeeding, after which sometimes the breast not only sags, but is also “decorated” with ugly-huge nipples. In this case, to make the breast look natural and beautiful after a reduction and breast lift, a reduction in the areola of the breast is mandatory.

During breast reduction surgery, you can also perform breast correction – for example, to remove asymmetry of the mammary glands, or to install implants to return the seductive forms of the torn breasts.

Before performing breast plastic surgery and reducing nipples, the patient needs to have a test including ultrasound of the breast, a consultation with a breast physician and oncologist, the necessary tests and ECG. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, and the doctor tells about the peculiarities of the procedure in each case. As a rule, the recovery period after breast reduction surgery lasts about a month or two. During this time will be removed stitches, swelling will go and you will see results.
