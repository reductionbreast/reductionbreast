Why do most plastic surgeons want to use anchor pattern scar as opposed to vertical scar for breast red
Effective
Feb 6, 2018
Hi. Your question is good. The anchor type incision pattern allows the most versatility for a large group of body types. Yes, the scar is longer, but a large variety of breast types can achieve more satisfying results more often. Think of it as the power of the longer scars. The additional transverse scar allows for control of the vertical length of the breast. In practice the longer scar is seldom an issue after the surgery.
