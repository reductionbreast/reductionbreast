Breast Reduction Miami Prices

If the breast lift and breast enlargement should be performed only to improve the aesthetics of the mammary glands, then breast reduction is already a health-improving operation in Miami.

The fact is that the hypertrophy (increase) of the mammary glands, which appears often at a young age, eventually leads to deformation of the spine, which, as is known, can cause not only pain, but also various diseases.

Indications for surgery:

– Complaints of pain in the thoracic and cervical spine
– Complaints about intertrigos in the area of the pectoral fold that are difficult to treat
– Discomfort due to excessive volume of mammary glands

Often the main reason for encouraging the patient to reduce the volume of the breast is the problem of clothing selection.

Big Breast Reduction With Dr Tal T. Roudner

Contraindications:

  • Less than 1 year after lactation
  • Tumor diseases of mammary glands
  • Any somatic diseases, diseases of internal organs in severe form
  • Violation of blood clotting
  • Diabetes
  • Venereal diseases

Postoperative period

Stay in the hospital is usually 1-2 days. After that, in order to reduce post-operative swelling of tissue in the first week you must sleep on your back, slightly in the raised position. Sutures are removed on day 7.

Approximately one and a half month, intradermal sutures dissolve themselves. About a month after breast reduction in Miami is necessary to refrain from any physical activities because they can cause swelling and bleeding in the chest area.
