Planning for breast reduction surgery

The preliminary step before breast reduction surgery is diagnosis. The cost and plan of the examinations include blood and urine tests, coagulogram, ECG, chest x-ray, ultrasound of the breast, receiving an opinion from the therapist and anesthesiologist. Other types of tests may be required.

Course of the operation

After diagnosis, the breast is photographed, measured with a centimeter ruler and the lines of incisions are outlined. Special treatment of the epithelium is then performed.

The surgical procedures for breast reduction are performed under general anesthesia and last about 3 hours. The specialist removes excess tissue, fatty layer, skin, moving the nipple to a different position.
