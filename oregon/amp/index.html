How Much Is A Breast Reduction In Oregon?

All women are in search of the ideal size and shape of the breast. Contrary to most expectations, sometimes the breasts not only have to be increased, but also reduced.

Do not think that this is a joke, because for this there are not only aesthetic recommendations, but also medical ones. Believe, the result will please not only the owner of the breast, but her environment.

What are the reasons for breast reduction in Oregon?

Medical reasons

Doctors advise to reduce excessively large breasts to avoid the appearance of a cancerous tumor. Also, women with large size are hard to breast-feed after giving birth.

Well, of course, this is a constant discomfort and inconvenience during exercise. If someone in your family suffers from such problems, you can give her a certificate for a breast reduction surgery.

Aesthetic reasons

In terms of aesthetics, a large breast is a dubious advantage. Over time, breast saggers and becomes an excuse for ridicule.

Yes, and initially it can upset its owner disproportionate to the complexion of the body. Do not put up with these problems, when you can just reduce the breast.

Consultation

On the consultation you will be asked about the psychological problems associated with large breasts.

They should be answered honestly, as well as questions about health. Only in this way the doctor will make a complete picture of the patient’s state of health in order to tell her later about the operation.

Preparation for surgery

Before the operation, it is necessary to have a stable weight of at least 3 months. Still need to give up smoking, because it affects the healing of wounds. The day before you can not take medications that affect the coagulability of the blood, and still need to buy a supporting bra, which must be worn after the operation.

Operation

Before and after surgery, the doctor will take a photo to compare the result. Then anesthesia will be injected and incisions will be made to remove excess fat. After this, the breast and the nipple-areola complex will be restored. Then, the surgeon will apply cosmetic sutures, leave small drains and make aseptic bandages to put on a fixing bra. From 1 to 3 days, you have to stay in the hospital.

Long-awaited beautiful breasts

After 5-6 months after the operation, you can admire the beautiful breasts. In the first month, you must refuse to visit the bath, gym and marital duty. Water procedures can begin within a week after breast reduction. And the main thing is not to forget to change the bandages every day.
