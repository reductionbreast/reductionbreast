Breast Reduction Surgery Cost In Pennsylvania

Very large and / or saggy breasts often negatively affect a woman’s self-esteem. Breast size does not correspond to body proportions and limits the woman in many situations.

Excessively large breasts can even cause disruption of posture or pain in the spine.

The purpose of the operation is to give the breast a shape and dimensions corresponding to the proportions of the body.

Thanks to a special technique, post-operative sutures are of minimal size and are almost invisible.

In addition, the methods used today provide long-term preservation of the new breast shape.

Timely treatment can prevent both psychological disorders (insecurity, fear of contacts and feelings of inferiority), and physical changes, in particular, incorrect posture, tightening of the muscles in the back and neck, the appearance of cord grooves and painful.

Breast Reduction Before After With Dr. Michael Stefan, MD, Philadelphia Plastic Surgeon

Breast reduction or breast lift surgery require especially careful consultation and planning, as applied here any rules or can not be templates. It is much more important here to analyze each specific case and discuss with the patient the required size and shape. Breast tissue requires regular examination in accordance with preventive recommendations.

Reduction or breast lift is carried out in our clinic under general anesthesia. Before the operation, the location of the incisions, the nipple and the new profile is indicated in the standing position. In the course of the operation, the nipple is moved primarily, with particular attention being paid to maintaining a sufficient number of blood vessels and nerves to provide sensitivity.

After that, the tissue of the breast and fat tissue move, so that the breast as a whole takes a new position. With a reduction in the breast at this stage, tissue is removed. After the new form is given by means of the self-resorbing sutures of the breast, removal of excess skin is performed.

Breast Reduction With Dr Michael Stefan, MD, Philadelphia Plastic Surgeon

The skin is sewn with threads that will need to be removed after 1 to 2 weeks. At the edges of the wound, drainage catheters are installed. Catheters are removed the next day or on the second day after surgery. After the operation, a wide elastic bandage is applied on the wound supporting the breast.

After surgery (breast reduction)

After surgery, breast cooling is applied. The patient regularly receives medications that prevent swelling and pain. Usually patients suffer little from pain after surgery.

A few hours after anesthesia, the patient can get up with help. Draining catheters are usually removed the next day after surgery.

Doctor Benjamin Lam, DO, FACOS, FACS, Philadelphia Plastic Surgeon – 21 Y.o. Male With Excessive Male Breast Tissue

After the operation, about 3 days of hospital stay are needed. Within the next few weeks, the breast should occupy a new position, it will drop a few degrees and become softer. After 7 days, the first seams are removed. The patch remains on the wound for another 2 weeks.
