Prices Of Breast Reduction In Brisbane

Breast lift and reduction (mastopexy and reduction mammoplasty) in Brisbane is an operation aimed at eliminating ptosis and reducing the size of the mammary glands.

How is the operation performed?

Before surgery

The patient comes to the clinic at 8:30 am. Breast lift and breast reduction, performed under anesthesia, so the last meal should be the previous evening at 18:00, since the morning nothing to eat and drink can not.

This is important, since it affects the safety of anesthesia. As soon as the patient comes to the clinic in Brisbane, nurses accompany her to the ward.

A therapist comes to the ward, who on the basis of the preoperative examination of the patient makes a conclusion about the safety of the operation under anesthesia.

Achieve A More Proportional Figure With Breast Reduction

Then an anesthesiologist comes in the ward, who evaluates the results of the tests, the conclusion of the therapist, examines the patient and makes an anesthesia plan. After then, a plastic surgeon comes to the ward.

The doctor talks with the patient, answers all the questions concerning the operation, the postoperative period, the result of the operation.

Then the plastic surgeon takes pictures of the patient. For breast lift and / or the breast reduction, 5 standard projections are used (facet, profile on both sides and half-turned on both sides), except for these projections sometimes use a photo with an inclination forward, because it is important to assess the sagging of the mammary glands.

The markup for the breast lift and breast reduction is performed standing upright and lying. After marking, nurses perform premedication, which includes sedative, antibacterial drugs and, in some cases, preparations for the prevention of thrombosis.

Breast Reduction And Lift In Brisbane

Then the patient is dressed in surgical underwear, compression stockings (for prophylaxis of thromboses) and escorted to the operating room.

During the operation

Breast reduction and breast lift are performed in the supine position on the back. Since these operations are performed under anesthesia, at the first stage an anesthesiologist works, which introduces anesthesia and then all the time stay in the operating room, controlling the adequacy and safety of anesthesia.

Then the surgeon, assistant and scrub nurse treated hands, change into sterile surgical linen and put on sterile surgical gloves.

The area of the operation is treated several times with an antiseptic solution and the patient is covered with sterile surgical sheets. It is difficult to overestimate the importance of aseptic and antiseptic for performing surgical interventions.

Breast Reduction Therapy Before And After

In our work, we adhere to the strictest protocol for ensuring sterility in the operating room, which negates the risk of infectious complications.

Then the surgeon, strictly following the marking lines, performs all the stages of the operation, the assistant supervises the hemostasis and helps the surgeon, and the operating sister watches the instruments and delivers them to the surgeon on time.

During operation, the nipple-areola complex returns to the correct position, and the right cone is formed from the glandular tissue.

This is important for the further attractive look of the breasts. Modern bioresorbable threads are used to close surgical access.

The operation ends with the installation of drains, the imposition of dressings and the dressing of compression underwear. For some time the patient is under surveillance in the intensive care unit, and then transferred to the ward.

After operation

Most of our patients remain in the clinic under supervision for a day. On the area of the operation several times at a certain interval, an cold compress is applied.

Pain after breast lift are mild. During the first night after surgery, it is preferable to sleep on the back.

The question of the possibility of discharge is decided on the first dressing. At the first dressing, the general condition of the patient, the condition of the area of operation, the amount of drainage are estimated.

During the first dressing, drains are removed, sutures are treated and bandages are changed. After breast reduction, it is recommended to wear compression underwear for a month. It is also important to understand that the final result of the operation can be evaluated after 6 months.

Indications and contraindications

Indications for breast lift and / or breast reduction: ptosis of the mammary glands and unsatisfactory breast size. Contraindications for breast lift and / or breast reduction: severe diseases of the cardiovascular system, diseases that affect blood coagulability, cancer, acute inflammatory diseases, breast diseases accompanied by the formation of cysts, inflammatory skin diseases over the mammary glands, unrealistic patient expectations. Breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia.

Types

There are three types of breast lifts:

1. CIRCUMAREOLAR BREAST LIFT TECHNIQUE

The final technique, circumareolar breast lift (sometimes called a periareolar or donut lift), uses a single incision around the nipple border. The scar is only around the areola. This type of breast lift has very narrow indications.

2. VERTICAL BREAST LIFT TECHNIQUE. The scar goes around the areola and down to the crease under the gland. This is a fairly often performed modification.

3. TRADITIONAL BREAST LIFT TECHNIQUE (sometimes called a Wise pattern or anchor lift). The scar has the shape of an anchor and goes around the areola, down to the crease under the gland and in both directions along the fold.

Recovery period:

It must be remembered that the result of the operation can be fully assessed only after the final resolution of the swelling. On average, the swelling finally disappears within 3-4 months after the operation. In some cases, the swelling may disappear within 6-12 months after the operation.

Maturation of scar tissue proceeds according to completely defined stages. In the beginning, the scar has a bright pink color and a dense consistency, then the rumen matures gradually, at the end of which the scar becomes thin, soft and white. Maturation of the scar is a long process and can take up to 12 months. It is important to understand this and be ready for it.

With pain, take pain medications according to the prescribing physician’s prescriptions. Sexual rest lasts 10 days after the operation. You need to wear compression underwear for 30 days. With ANY arising questions and doubts, it is necessary to contact the attending physician.
