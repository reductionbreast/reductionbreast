Bust Reduction In Kansas City Missouri

The shape and size of the female breast is laid genetically, which means that no woman can influence the development of the breast in any way.

Of course, the breast of a good size and beautiful shape pleases any representative of the fair sex, but it happens that over time the breast becomes too full and becomes a big burden. Heredity, hormonal failure or a sharp increase in body weight contribute to a significant increase in the breast.

As a consequence, the breasts can turn into a huge flaw. For example, with some hormonal disorders already in adolescence can form a breast is not a teenage size.

And with the onset of menopause, the breast can not only increase significantly, but also greatly hanging.

Believe me, this is not only an aesthetic problem. Too large breasts can cause serious disorders in the whole body, such as problems with the spine, pain in the back and neck, a rash under the breast, besides, the woman will experience psychological inconvenience when walking, playing sports, not speaking already about to appear in a swimsuit on the beach.

Such women often turn to a plastic surgeon with a request to surgically reduce the breast, because there are no other ways to get rid of too large a breast. In addition, breast reduction surgery in Kansas City is fairly simple, does not require a long stay in the hospital and gives excellent results.

The essence of the operation is as follows: depending on the technique of carrying out from the breast, excess fatty tissue, part of the mammary gland is removed, an excessive flap of skin is also excised, nipples are displaced, the areola of the nipple is reduced and a breast of normal size is formed.

Pre-operative instructions

After consultation with a plastic surgeon, a preoperative therapeutic examination follows, after which you can agree on the timing of the operation.

After payment of the advance payment, the term of the operation becomes mandatory for both parties, the remaining amount for the operation is paid on the day of the surgery.

On an operation it is necessary to come on an empty stomach (do not drink, do not eat, do not smoke), with a clean head, without make-up!
All ornaments, including earrings, leave at home!

After the operation, it is necessary to stay in the ward until the general health is stabilized. Home care is allowed not earlier than the next day after surgery after the dressing.

We recommend that someone accompany you. Non-resident patients are advised to ensure the possibility of staying in the city until the bandage is removed (4 -5 days after the operation).

Breast reduction methods:

Plastic surgeons practice several ways to reduce breast, the form and technique of surgery is chosen by the doctor after a thorough examination and in accordance with the patient’s desire.

So, the least traumatic method is breast reduction with the help of liposuction, or pumping out subcutaneous fat from the breast with the help of special cannulas. Liposuction does not leave visible scars, and is recommended for patients who do not need to greatly reduce the size.

However, if the woman’s chest is somewhat lowered (there is a pronounced degree of ptosis), then this method will not give an ideal result.

The method of breast reduction with the help of a vertical (short) suture, which goes from the nipple down to the lower fold of the breast, is rather popular in patients. This method is recommended in case of removal of not more than 28,2 oz of tissue from one gland. With this operation, a good aesthetic result is achieved, a natural shape of the breast is formed and the sensitivity of the nipple is maintained. This technique is also considered to be low-traumatic and practically does not give complications.

Doctor J. Douglas Cusick Post Breast Reduction

Classics of mammoplasty is the technique of breast reduction with the help of a T-shaped seam, sometimes this seam is called an anchor incision and goes from the nipple downwards and at the bottom is connected with a seam, that passes under the pectoral fold. The technique is well developed by plastic surgeons and is recommended for excision of a sufficiently large volume of tissue.

If the woman’s breast has increased very much, and the doctor diagnoses gigantomastia, in this case, the excision (amputation) of the breast is performed with the nipples moved to the place of the newly formed breasts. There is a risk of non-survival of the nipple tissues, and if they take root, they will remain insensitive.

After such an operation, the possibility of breastfeeding is excluded. This method is quite dangerous and is used only in exceptional cases.

Quite often there is a natural asymmetry of the breast. In some, it is almost invisible, in others it is quite pronounced.

Here we can recommend either a breast lift, or a reduction in the larger side, or an increase in a smaller breast, if the big breast is of good shape and normal size. The technique of the operation in this case is chosen by the doctor together with the patient’s wish.

After operation:

The hospital stay after the operation is from one to three days, depending on the patient’s well-being. Unpleasant sensations in the chest and moderate pain are removed with the help of pain medications.

Two weeks you need to wear special compression underwear, during the month after breast reduction in Kansas City is best to refrain from physical activity, swimming and sunburn.
