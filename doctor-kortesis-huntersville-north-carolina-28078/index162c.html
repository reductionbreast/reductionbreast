I had to travel after Brazilian Butt Lift surgery and had to sit for 6 hours. Is this okay?
It is not ideal to sit for long periods of time
Jul 16, 2014
It is not ideal to sit for long
periods of time after having fat grafting to the buttocks area. This can
diminish blood supply and diminish viability and fat grafting take. It is important
to try and stay off the buttocks area for an extended period of time to enhance
the viability of the fat grafting. I know there are some times where you cannot
do so, but ideally in a perfect world situation you would stay off of it as
much as possible.
