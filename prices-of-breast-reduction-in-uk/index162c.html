Prices Of Breast Reduction In United Kingdom, GB

Breast reduction in UK is a surgical operation, the purpose of which is to reduce the volume of the breast and correct its shape.

A disproportionately large breast can cause pain in the back and neck, be associated with shoulder strain and chronic headaches, and in the folds of the skin may develop intertrigos and inflammation.

In the case of too large breasts is difficult to properly engage in sports, there may be difficulties in finding suitable clothes.

The method is suitable for women who want to improve their quality of life by reducing the volume and improving the shape / appearance of the breast. Achieving ideal symmetry of the mammary glands is unlikely.

Preoperative consultation

Breast Reduction In Portsmouth

The patient’s expectations and ability to fulfill these wishes are elucidated.

The patient’s health status is revealed, first of all it is important to know everything about breast diseases (breast cancer in personal and family history), it is also important to get information about breast changes that occurred during pregnancy / breastfeeding and due to weight changes; receive information about the medicines used.

Before the operation, it is necessary to perform breast examinations (mammography). Before the operation, it is desirable to stabilize the body weight.

When examining the patient, the chest and thoracic glands are examined (asymmetry, ptosis of the breast, its size, size and location of the areolar-nipple complex), the quality of the skin and glandular tissue is assessed.

The patient’s breast photos are taken in order to compare the results. Breast measurement is performed, a procedure of operation and the location of future scars are planned.

It is important to discuss with your surgeon your possible desire to continue becoming pregnant and breastfeeding (it is possible to choose the method of surgery that maximizes the retention of the milk ducts, while the further ability to breastfeed may not persist).

Operation

The operation is performed under general anesthesia and usually lasts 2-3 hours. The operation is a combination of removing excess tissue volume and moving the tissue together with the removal of excess skin. In most cases, drains are left in the mammary glands.

Recovery

Drainage is removed and the patient is allowed to go home usually in the morning following the operation of the day. Recovery for each patient individually. In the first days after the operation, it is necessary to use painkillers, because swelling is caused by tissue damage.

Breast Reduction Surgery In Manchester UK

Wounds are covered with wound dressings and plasters for up to 2 weeks, after which the scar treatment begins. Constant wearing of the supporting bra is necessary for 6 weeks, and then at an additional load of 4-6 weeks, of course, after surgery it is not recommended to use a push-up bra. The first few weeks after surgery, it is advisable to avoid heavy physical exertion.

Possible risks

Immediately after surgery, swelling is possible, which can last for a long time, discomfort, changes in nipple sensitivity or insensitivity, and subcutaneous hemorrhages. Because of internal bleeding in the breast, there may be a need for a second operation. Inflammation occurs rarely.

More often there are problems with wound healing, which can lead to an increase in the scar. Often there are asymmetries. Possible necrosis of tissue, in particular, areola necrosis, dissatisfaction of the patient with the shape and size of the breast after the operation.
