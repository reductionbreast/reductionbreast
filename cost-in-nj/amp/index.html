How Much Does A Breast Reduction Cost In New Jersey?

Reduction mammoplasty is one of the types of plastic surgery on the chest.

With its help, the size and shape of the bust are corrected, the feeling of discomfort due to the excessive volume of mammary glands is eliminated.

Reduction mammoplasty is a plastic surgery, which is aimed at reducing the breast.

Mostly, it has medical, not aesthetic, indications, as often patients complain of pain in the neck, back, breathing problems and mobility restriction.

Almost always, breast reduction is combined with a mammary gland lift, because their severity leads to ptosis.

Indications for breast reduction

Hypertrophy is an excessively large volume of mammary glands, which brings discomfort;
the desire and pursuit of the perfect appearance.

Contraindications to the reduction of mammary glands

  • oncological diseases;
  • Bleeding disorder;
  • diabetes;
  • Somatic and chronic diseases of internal organs in the stage of decompensation;
  • Lactation and pregnancy.

Features of the breast reduction surgery

Breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia. During surgery, excess skin and part of the tissue are removed, a “new” mammary gland is formed.

Surgical access to the tissue is carried out through vertical or anchor incisions. Duration is 2-4 hours. Vertical access with a reduction in the breast with an additional incision around the areola is less traumatic.

It allows you to perform breast reduction and breast lift of almost any size. Anchor incision is indicated for women with a very large bust and severe forms of ptosis of tissues.

Recovery after breast reduction

After breast reduction surgery, the patients experience pain and sometimes nausea from anesthesia.

There are swelling in the area of the operation, and bruising is less common. At first, the sensitivity of the breast and nipples will be reduced. Sutures from the chest are removed after 1-2 weeks, depending on the characteristics of the surgical procedure.

Within a month after the operation, it is recommended to protect yourself from physical exertion, visiting the sauna, solarium and bath. Wearing compression linen and examinations by the doctor is indicated.
