Features of the breast reduction surgery

Operative interventions on the mammary gland account for the largest percentage of all plastic surgeries.

The desire to increase and / or tighten the breasts appears in almost every woman, especially after the birth of a child and lactation, and plastic surgery provides such an opportunity.

But for some women, large breasts is not a reason for pride, but, on the contrary, a disadvantage. Women, who by nature are owners of an excessively large bust, turn to the specialists of the clinic to perform a breast reduction surgery.

Breast reduction is an aesthetic and wellness operation.

Breast reduction in Sacramento is considered one of the most complex surgical interventions in plastic surgery, which is not only aesthetic, but also health-improving. Excessive breast enlargement during puberty or during pregnancy occurs as a response of breast tissue to changes in the hormonal background of a woman.

Indications for breast reduction surgery

The main indication for breast reduction surgery is a big, heavy, and sagging breasts. Women with a breast is a significant load on the spine, persistent pain in the back, neck, shoulders and forearms.

In the fold under the breast, maceration can form, creating ideal conditions for the development of the infectious process. In addition, with large breasts is difficult to engage in sports, and even simple physical exercises.

Women often hesitate to their large breasts and feel a strong psychological discomfort in communicating with people. Another indication for reduction mammoplasty is the hypertrophy of one of the mammary glands with their asymmetry. In the presence of indications for surgery in the clinic, breast reduction is carried out, in order to make it beautiful and attractive.

Variants of skin flaps for breast reduction

Breast reduction surgery consists in excision of excess mammary gland tissues with the subsequent scraping and moving of the skin-fat flap with the nipple-areolar complex to a new position.

Such variants of skin flaps are used:

Upper-lower skin flaps: formed after excision of the skin and tissue of the glands from the middle part of the outer and inner quadrants of the chest, their blood supply is provided by both upper and lower feeding vessels;
Upper and lateral flaps: formed after removal of the skin and tissues of the gland in the region of the lower quadrants, blood supply to the lateral and upper feeding vessels;
Lower flaps: the skin and glandular tissue of the outer and inner upper quadrants are removed, the blood supply is due to the lower feeding vessels;

Side flaps: the result of removing a large volume of skin and glandular tissue in the upper, lower and lateral quadrants. The flap is blooded in this case due to internal or external feeding blood vessels.

Important conditions for performing breast reduction surgery.

Breast reduction in Sacramento is a complex and rather specific operation, the technique of implementation of which has some characteristic features:

  • From a large mammary gland it is possible to form as small a mammary gland as you like;
  • Breast reduction surgery should be performed only in one stage, without the possibility of conducting corrective operations;
  • The natural appearance of the nipples and areolas should be preserved, if necessary, the diameter of the areola can be reduced.
  • Another important point for performing breast reduction surgery is associated with breastfeeding.

    • Providing Balance In Size And Position Between Your Breasts

  • After breast reduction, lactation is strictly prohibited, it is important to inform the patient in a timely manner.

The clinic experts recommend performing breast reduction in the event that the pregnancy is no longer planned. If a woman wants to reduce her breasts now and have children in the future – she will need to take drugs that suppress lactation.
