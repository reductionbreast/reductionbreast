Will I get good results from a thigh lift with only a bit of loose skin?
You Must Weigh Your Options
Jul 9, 2014
If it is minimal sagging of the skin, then this may be difficult to justify. You must decide if it is worth having scars in the groin area. If there were significant laxity, I would say this procedure would be worth it.
I had a Mini Tummy Tuck 12 days ago, is it normal for my stomach to be numb?
Numbness For Several Months After Surgery is Normal
Jul 9, 2014
This is normal and it may take several months for numbness to go away as the nerves regrow. The pinkness is normal and indicative of good blood flow and means that circulation is improving. If you have redness, swelling or heat radiating from the area, contact your surgeon as soon as possible as that could mean infection.

Pregnant Right After Mommy Makeover, Are There Additional Risks Now?
No risks if you become pregnant again!
Nov 3, 2011
Congratulations! Your cosmetic surgery will not affect your pregnancy. During this next pregnancy your skin will stretch and after the birth of your child, you may opt for further skin removal to retighten the abdominal contours. In other words, a redo or modification of your tummy tuck can be done to restore your results.
skin removal during mini-tummy tuck vs. standard tummy tuck?
Full vs. Mini Tummy Tuck
Nov 3, 2011
Mini tummy tucks are performed on individuals who have fairly tight muscle with smooth upper abdominal skin but have loose skin below the naval. A shorter incision is made just above the pubis and a horizontal ellipse of skin is removed below the naval. All tissues above and including the naval are left in place. Patients with stretched muscle and loose skin above the naval will require a full tummy tuck.
