Is it possible for perform a breast reduction on small asymmetrical breasts?
Breast Reduction
Oct 24, 2019
Yes, it is possible to perform a breast reduction on small asymmetrical breasts. The procedure and amount of tissue removed can be catered to the needs of each patient, as well as adapted to achieve symmetry. I encourage you to make an appointment with a board certified plastic surgeon to determine if you’re a good candidate!

Breast Reduction
Oct 24, 2019
There are multiple techniques available for breast reduction, some of which provide a longer lasting lift. There are multiple factors affecting how weight loss will impact the appearance of your breasts, including composition of breast tissue, skin integrity, amount of weight lost, etc. If you are considering major weight loss, we do recommend you wait to get surgery until after your weight loss. However, with minimal weight loss we typically do not see a drastic impact on results.
