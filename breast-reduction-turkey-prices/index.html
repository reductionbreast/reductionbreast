Modern society cultivates the image of a large breast. But, perhaps, you will be surprised by how many women come to me for a consultation with a single desire – to make the breasts smaller.

Gigantomastia, macromastia is a pathologically large breast.

In fact, the big breast gives its mistress a huge inconvenience. It breaks the proportions of the body and does not look aesthetically pleasing. Women have to avoid tight clothing, they are forced to abandon many active sports and recreation.

And in general, they feel uneasy. Not to mention the fact that large breasts due to the severity and purely physical inconvenience – pain in the back, shoulders; Constant wearing of a bra, brassiere straps rubbing shoulders, rashes and intertrigos under the breast are the problems that the patients share with me.

Hypertrophy of the breast can be due to heredity or caused by hormonal disorders during adolescence, during pregnancy, breast-feeding, menopause. Hypertrophy can be either bilateral or unilateral.

Breast reduction surgery

Surgical breast reduction is an operation during which excessive volume of breast tissue is removed. Breast reduction can be combined with other plastic breast operations, such as:

  • Breast lift.
  • Correction of the areola.

Traditionally, two methods are used for breast reduction in Turkey:

  • Anchor breast lift
  • Vertical.

Where will the scars be located?

I prefer the method of vertical breast lift, when one of the incisions passes around the areola of the nipple, and the second goes vertically down from the areola to the crease under the breast.

But, if the volume of the mammary glands is too large, a classic “anchor” lifting is needed – the incision is around the areola, vertically downwards and along the breast fold.

Surgery and postoperative period

The operation is performed under general anesthesia and lasts 2-3 hours. Hospitalization takes 1 day. After surgery, you must wear special surgical underwear for a minimum of 2 weeks.

Return to work, if it is not related to physical activities, it is possible in a week, and all sports events should be postponed for at least a month, according to the state of health.
