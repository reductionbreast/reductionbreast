Breast Reduction Prices In Florida

Breast reduction (reduction mammoplasty) in Florida is a kind of plastic breast surgery aimed at reducing the size and shape of both breasts by removing excess fat and glandular tissues, as well as excess skin, in order to achieve the size of the breast that will be proportional to your body.

Indications for surgery

There are several Indications for the procedure. The weight of the excess tissue of the mammary glands can lead to a deterioration in a person’s ability to lead an active lifestyle. Excessively large breasts can also cause health problems. Breast reduction surgery in Florida helps relieve the emotional discomfort associated with excessively large breasts, and also remove the physical pain from the back caused by the severity of the breast.

Breast Lift And Reduction With Doctor Russell Sassani

First of all, you must be physically healthy, have realistic expectations, that is, clearly see what should be your breasts. If you are sick of the feeling that your breasts are too large and restrict your physical activity, provoke pain in the back, neck, shoulders, there is irritation of the skin under the breast folds, then breast reduction surgery is indicated. Also, if you have a congenital or acquired asymmetry after childbirth, breastfeeding, or suffered injuries.

Principles of the breast reduction surgery

The operation lasts for 1.5-2 hours under general anesthesia. Breast reduction is usually performed with the help of incisions on the chest, followed by removal of excess fat and glandular tissues, as well as removing excess skin.

Bust Reduction Has An Extremely High Satisfaction Rating Among Our Patients

In certain cases, when the size of the breast is largely determined by the presence of adipose tissue, then excess fat can be removed through liposuction in combination with the method of excision of excess skin. There are several variants of the incisions: a circular incision around the areola, either in the form of an inverted T or an anchor-shaped incision.

Breast Reduction Cosmetic Surgery With Doctor Patrick L. Basile

After the incision, excess fat and glandular tissue is removed, the breast rises. The nipple, which all this time remains connected to the original blood supply and nerves, is then applied back.

The size of the areola is reduced by excision of the skin along the perimeter, if necessary.

Then the incisions are closed. The cosmetic seam is made, not on the skin, but in the tissues of the breast to tightly support the newly formed breasts. Incisions line in the first time after the operation will be visible, but soon they will disappear.

Recovery after surgery

Reduction mammoplasty is uncomplicated, so the recovery period takes place in the interval of 5 to 7 days. At this time, the seams are removed.

Depending on the volume of the operation, you will be advised to stay for a few days in the clinic for observation by a surgeon.

For those, who are experiencing that there is a terrible seam on the chest, it all depends on the skill of the surgeon, and on his experience. Also it is worth remembering that within 1-2 months after the operation it is necessary to exclude heavy physical exertion.
