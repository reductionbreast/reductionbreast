Is Bellafill a good filler to augment the chin without an implant? Will it last longer than Juv
Nonsurgical chin augmentation
Apr 19, 2019
Bellafill is one of my favorite fillers for nonsurgical augmentation of the midface, nose and chin. I absolutely do recommend trying a hyaluronic acid based filler first (ie Juvederm) like you did, as it’s completely dissolvable, while Bellafill is not. I recommend a conservative approach—1-2 syringes Max per session to avoid overcorrection.
How to treat this wound on incision after tummy tuck?
Postoperative Wound
Jan 15, 2019
Partial wound dehiscence is not that uncommon after body contouring surgery. Wound care is based on amount of drainage and wound cultures. Culture-specific antibiotics and protecting the surrounding skinflint moisture are key. Aside from packing, I find negative pressure wound therapy (KCI-wound VAC) may expedite healing. The good news is that with time, this will heal, so hang in there.

Will laser improve the look of scarring after a bra line lift?
Scars
Oct 5, 2018
Scars are mature 12-18 months after they’re made. Until then (or in the immediate postop period), keeping them out of the sun, avoiding frictional forces, and barring any wound healing complications, will ensure an optimal appearance. After that, scars with a pink hue respond well to a broadband light laser (bbl). Thick or raised scars respond to a few sessions of injection with 5-fluorouracil +/- Kenalog (if your skin color permits).

Final cup size
Oct 3, 2018
Your ultimate cup size will depend on the additive effects of implant + your breast tissue + where you get fitted for a bra. Cup size is very subjective. We aim for a final appearance rather than a cup size to ensure your aesthetic goals are met. How can I have a more 'V-shaped' face?
Facial contouring
Sep 15, 2018
Facial contouring and final appearance is a matter of adding and subtracting volume in the right places and in the right proportions. I agree with the other surgeons in that an exam is needed to tailor the right approach to your desired goals. I combine buccal fat reduction with masseteric Botox to decrease lower facial volume with fillers/grafted fat to augment other areas. The result is a youthful facial shape (a triangle with the point facing down) in the correct proportions.
Should I be worried about the gaunt look that can happen after buccal fat removal?
Buccal fat
Sep 15, 2018
Overresection, paired with fat loss that occurs with normal facial aging, can lead to a gaunt/too thin appearance. I perform buccal fat reduction (not complete excision) to lessen the likelihood of this. A conservative approach is best (much like treatment for lower eyelid fat), as this procedure yields permanent results.
