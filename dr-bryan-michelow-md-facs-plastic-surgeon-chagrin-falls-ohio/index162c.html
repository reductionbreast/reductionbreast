Breast lift vs Breast reduction?
Reduction vs Lift
Feb 14, 2016
Breast lifts improve  the contour of droopy breasts where the nipple and areola are below the inframammary line. The size of the breast is usually maintained.  A breast reduction reduces the size of the breast as well as lifting the nipple and areola complex into a more youthful position.  This is a good question and speak to your plastic surgeon about your options. Good luck with your decision.Bryan Michelow MD 
Skiing after Breast Augmentation?
Skiing Post Breast Augmentation Surgery
Jan 31, 2016
Thank you for the question. Each surgeon has their own protocol regarding post operative activity following breast augmentation. I recommend that my patients do no heavy lifting during first week and then increase their activities over the next two weeks. Skiing presents a risk of falling and this could result in healing problems within the early stages of recovery. My recommendation is to speak to your board certified plastic surgeon.
