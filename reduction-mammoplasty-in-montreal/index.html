What can be changed during breast reduction surgery?

Does breast reduction is possible in reality, it turns out to consultation with the surgeon. During the procedure of breast reduction, it is often necessary to lift the breasts and move the nipples higher.

Breast reduction also makes it possible to correct the difference in the size of the breasts and / or their asymmetry. The ability to breastfeed may be temporarily restricted after surgery. If you are planning to become pregnant, we recommend that you postpone the surgery for the future.

Preparation for surgery

Before the operation, it is necessary to perform a mammographic or ultrasound examination of the breast in order to exclude the possibility of having nodules in the mammary glands (at the age of 30 years – ultrasound, from 30 years old – mammography), and a blood test should also be performed.

Before the planned operation, you should be in the best possible state of health.

Women With Large, Heavy Pendulous Breasts Suffer From Numerous Health Issues

You need to limit the use of nicotine and alcohol to a minimum. In addition to general circulation, smoking also slows the microscopic circulation in the operated area. You should not take aspirin or preparations containing acetylsalicylic acid, at least 14 days before the operation. At least a week after the operation, you will have to give up active movement, including at work and in your personal life. If you have the opportunity to agree that someone cares for you in the postoperative period, talk with this person, first of all, about recommendations for post-operative care.

Hospital stay

We recommend a short-term, 1-2-day stay in the hospital. A specific period of stay in the hospital will assign and agree with you doctor.

Anesthesia

The breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia, so that you will sleep throughout the operation. Before the operation, the anesthetist conducts the necessary studies to provide you with the appropriate anesthesia.

Breast reduction and course of operation

Usually the operation lasts 2-3.5 hours. Usually during the operation, the excess glandular and fatty tissue is removed from the lower part of the breast, and also rises and, if necessary, the nipple decreases. The surgical method is chosen by the surgeon.

Sometimes breast reduction surgery is carried out together with liposuction. If the breasts are too wide, this can give good results. Depending on the size of the breast, various techniques are used, which causes the arrangement of scars.

They may be:

  • Around the areola of the nipple and vertically down
  • Around the areola of the nipple and vertically down and in the side, or in case of the largest ptosis of the breast
  • Anchor scars (T-shaped)

The course of the operation is generally the following:

Breast Reduction Scar With Doctor Karl Schwarz

First, the surgeon on the skin draws the estimated sites of the incisions and the new location of the nipples. After disinfection of the surgical site and the application of a sterile surgical coating, the surgeon perform the necessary incisions. Then remove the planned amount of skin and tissue, move to a new location and fix the nipples. After that, the skin under the nipple is juxtaposed together and a new breast is formed.

Sutures are usually located around the nipple and vertically down to the fold of the breast. If the breast is large or very saggy, it is necessary to perform another horizontal incision at the fold of the breast, in which case an L- or T-shaped scar is obtained.

After surgery, the bandage is superimposed or a special bra is put on, which helps to reduce postoperative swelling. Small drainage tubes in the postoperative period allow the wound fluid to flow out.
