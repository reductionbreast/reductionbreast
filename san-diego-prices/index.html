San Diego Prices

Breast reduction surgery in San Diego is suitable for women whose health condition is compromised due to the large weight of the breast or incorrect posture.

After the operation, the pain in the back, neck, shoulders decreases in the women, physical activity increases.

In the course of breast reduction surgery, removal of excess skin and lifting of the remaining skin are performed.

As a result, the breast decreases and acquires a more beautiful shape.

Different techniques are used for breast lift, depending on the desired degree of reduction.

1) Vertical technique of breast reduction or “lollipop” skin incision is made around the areola and down vertically in the direction of the folds under the breasts. This forms the so-called form of candy on a stick, or “lollipop.” This technique is recommended for women who want to slightly reduce their breasts.

Before And After Breast Reduction Pic

2) Horizontal method of breast reduction

The horizontal technique leaves a circular scar around the areola and a horizontal scar in the pectoral fold. This technique is suitable for women with a narrow chest, when the vertical technique can not give the desired result.

3) Anchor technique of the breast reduction (T-shaped)

The technique got its name thanks to the scar in the form of an anchor, which remains on the chest after the operation.

This is the most invasive operation, and is suitable for those who wish to significantly reduce breasts.

During the consultation, we will be able to decide what method is best suited to your breast.
