Preoperative preparation for breast reduction

First of all, you need to make sure that there are no inflammatory and oncologic diseases of the mammary glands, and also undergo a comprehensive medical examination to exclude contraindications.

In the list of major contraindications there are uncontrolled diabetes mellitus and other diseases considered to be severe.

On pre-surgical consultation, you must tell the doctor about all your illnesses. This is very important for the operation to be absolutely safe. After the doctor was convinced that there are no contraindications to the operation, talk with him about the size of the breast that suits you.

When making a decision, listen to the advice of a doctor, because it comes from many important factors, for example, your body proportions, height, weight.

Techniques of breast reduction surgery in Beverly Hills

Such a correction is called reduction mammoplasty. It is performed under general anesthesia and lasts about 2.5 hours.

The surgeon performs the following manipulations:

  • Removes excess parts of the tissue of the mammary glands;
  • Eliminates ptosis of the breast (this removes excess skin areas);
  • The doctor places the nipples in an aesthetically correct position.
  • Similar methods used for a breast lift.

Vertical method. The tissues of the mammary glands are removed in the central part (fat and glandular tissues). The nipple is placed in a new place.

The seams are on the border of the areola. Vertical suture runs from the areola to the pectoral fold. This method is effective with moderate hypertrophy of the female mammary glands.

If hypertrophy is at the extreme stage, it is better to use the T-shaped or anchor technique of reduction mammoplasty.

The T-shaped method involves removing the bulk of the mammary glands and subcutaneous fat, mostly in the side sections.

Recovery period

Immediately after the operation, at the very first time, a feeling of bursting in the chest is allowed. That’s OK, because in this period the swelling is still quite strong. Soon it will decline, and the feeling of bursting will disappear.

On the next day after the operation, the patient is bandaged, drains that were installed during the operation are removed.

The doctor prescribes drugs that improve blood flow and wound healing ointments. Cosmetic sutures are removed after 2 weeks.

For 5-8 weeks, it is necessary to wear supporting underwear. For the recovery period, heavy physical exertion should be avoided, as well as not visiting the sauna and sauna.

After 2-3 months, the breast will improve significantly. In the lower pole it will slightly round out and take its final form. Scars after the sutures will not be pink, but bodily. Over time, they will be almost invisible.
