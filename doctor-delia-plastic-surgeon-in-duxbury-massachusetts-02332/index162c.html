My Breast Size is a 42 D. If I Have 628 Grams Removed from Each Breast What Cup Size Will This Make
Bra cup size after Breast Reduction
Jul 24, 2012
It is difficult to predict bra cup size after a breast reduction. The procedure is designed to remove an adequate amount of tissue to help relieve symptoms of neck, back pain, shoulder grooving, a rash under the breasts, etc. The amount of tissue removed will result in a different cup size in different patients. You should carefully discuss your goals with your surgeon prior to surgery to ensure that your expectations are realistic.

Does Smoking Slow Down the Healing Process in Breast Reduction Surgery?
Does smoking slow down the healing process in Breast Reduction Surgery?
Jun 8, 2012
Smoking, in general, affects wound healing in a negative way. Nicotene may affect the microcirculation to soft tissues and can even lead to tissue loss in some cases. It is usually preferable to try to quit smoking to reduce risk factors prior to proceeding with plastic surgery procedures. After all, many plastic surgery procedures are elective and quiting smoking at least 6 weeks before the surgery may have some benefit on wound healing.
