Reduction mammoplasty: a technique

The operation consists in removing excess parts of the mammary glands and skin with further suturing of the incision. The technique of breast reduction is far from simple and requires great attention and skill, because:

  1. removes a significant volume of tissues, among which are the ducts of the mammary glands;
  2. during the operation, it is required to maintain the skin sensitivity to the maximum.

Professionally performed breast reduction surgery is almost imperceptible scars and preservation of the result for at least a year.

General anesthesia is used for breast reduction surgery.

Access. The most common is the incision around the nipple-areola complex and vertically down from the areola to the skin fold under the breast.

Features of the operation

Before the operation, the surgeon applies marking. Lines of prospective incisions are applied in a standing position, depending on the patient’s particular structure, as well as on her wishes regarding the desired aesthetic result after breast reduction. After the formation of access, the most gentle removal of excess fatty, glandular tissues and overstretched skin are carried out.

Breast reduction in Houston can be performed by moving the nipple to a new position, and without (rarely). The remaining tissues form new mammary glands. The operation for breast reduction takes on average 2 to 3 hours. It should be noted that the duration of the operation depends on the amount of intervention, individual characteristics of the organism and, of course, on the experience of the surgeon.

After the operation is completed, a sterile elastic bandage is applied to the patient. 1-2 days after breast reduction, the bandage is removed and replaced with compression underwear.

Recovery period

The first day after the operation the patient is observed in the hospital, after further examination by a surgeon, you can be discharged home. Within a month after breast reduction, minimal physical activity is recommended.

During this period, the patient also needs to wear compression underwear. The first time may experience swelling of the breast tissue and bruising, pain in the breast area. As a rule, these symptoms are short. Postoperative scars gradually become less noticeable. The final aesthetic result after breast reduction can be estimated after about six months.
