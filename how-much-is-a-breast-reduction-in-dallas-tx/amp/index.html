How Much Is A Breast Reduction In Dallas TX?

Big breasts are often a matter of envy for others, but for the owner of this “wealth” it is often a health problem, discomfort, problems with the selection of clothing. In addition, the shape of a large breast is not always ideal. Reduction mammoplasty in Dallas is more a health improvement operation, and, of course, an aesthetic one.

There are several methods of reduction mammoplasty. The choice in favor of the use of this or that method of breast reduction is carried out depending on the individual characteristics of the patient and on the experience of the plastic surgeon. Breast reduction surgery in Dallas consists of removing excess mammary gland tissue, excising excess skin, forming a new breast shape, and moving the areola and nipple to a new position.

Breast Plastic Surgery With Dr. Laurence Weider

On average, the volume of tissue removed is 400-600 g (from each breast). The final volume of breast tissue that is to be removed can be determined only during the examination in a face-to-face consultation, taking into account the wishes of the patient about the desired volume of the breast.

Dallas breast reduction is recommended for women who do not plan to give birth in the future. This is due to the fact that after the operation breastfeeding is impossible.

Breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia (intravenous or endotracheal). Depending on the complexity of the operation lasts 2 – 3 hours. After surgery for 1-3 days the patient is in a hospital, under the supervision of a doctor.

The wearing of compression underwear is indicated the first month after breast reduction surgery. During the recovery period, you should follow all the prescriptions of your plastic surgeon, including taking medications.

Breast Reduction Methods With Doctor Charles T. Slack

Most often, the appearance of the breast in the early postoperative period is not aesthetically attractive, but this is not a cause for grief. There must be time for the breasts to return to normal. Over time, the scars are smoothed and become less noticeable, the breasts become more beautiful.

The final result is estimated 6-8 months after the operation.

RESULT

The postoperative result of breast reduction is usually better in young women, because they have more elastic and denser mammary glands, the resection volume is significantly smaller than in older women, whose fat tissue is much larger than glandular.

The result of the operation is stable. In a remote postoperative period, most patients note that the result is excellent, or satisfactory.

It should be borne in mind that all people are subject to natural aging, regardless of the fact of performing any corrective operation. Other things being equal, the breast after surgery will look more aesthetically attractive than the same breast, but without surgical correction.
