Breast reduction surgery or reduction mammoplasty is an operation aimed at reducing breast size. Many women suffer from physical or psychological symptoms and uncertainty as a result of having a very large breasts.

Heavy breasts can cause back pain, poor posture and related medical problems. Breast reduction surgery in Tucson can be performed to remove excess breast tissue and change the position of the nipple (if necessary). Thanks to this procedure, the breasts become smaller and get better shape.

Breast reduction surgery

Slightly similar to the operation for a breast lift, breast reduction surgery, because they assume the creation of anchor incisions on the chest.

A circular incision is applied around each nipple, after which, large areoles can be reduced (if necessary).

Doctor Jeffrey Nelson Mastopexy Results

The incision runs vertically, from the lower part of the areola to the crease under the breast. And at the end, the incision is made in the crease around the lower part of the chest.

The surgeon removes a certain amount of fat and glandular tissue. Excess skin is separated and the nipple is repositioned.

Seams and gauze bandages are applied. The surgical bra should be worn for several weeks to support the breast and allow for proper recovery.

Complications following breast reduction surgery

Like all surgical procedures, this operation is associated with a risk of complications.

There is the possibility of loss of sensation of the nipples and areolas, especially cases associated with significant reduction and movement.

There is also the risk of surgical complications such as bleeding, infection, or excessive scarring. The results of breast reduction surgery may not always meet expectations, so it is better to make realistic representations before such an operation.
